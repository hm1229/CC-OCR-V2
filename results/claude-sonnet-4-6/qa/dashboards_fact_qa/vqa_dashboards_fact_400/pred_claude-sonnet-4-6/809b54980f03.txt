## Shelf Life Comparison

**Dry dog food** has a longer shelf life compared to canned dog food:

| | **Dry Dog Food** | **Canned Dog Food** |
|---|---|---|
| **Shelf Life (Unopened)** | **18-24 months** | 12-15 months |
| **Once Opened, Store At** | <80°F | <40°F |
| **Once Opened, Keeps For** | **90 days** (in a cool, dry place) | 3 days (refrigerated) |

Dry dog food wins on both counts — it lasts longer **unopened** (up to 24 months vs. 15 months) and significantly longer **once opened** (90 days vs. just 3 days).
