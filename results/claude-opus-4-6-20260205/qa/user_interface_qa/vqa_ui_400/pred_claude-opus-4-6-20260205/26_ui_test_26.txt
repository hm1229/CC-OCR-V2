## Best Job Search Method

According to the infographic on "Best Job Search Techniques of 2013," **Online recruitment platforms** is the most popular/best method for job searching, used by **26%** of job seekers.

The ranking of job search methods is:

1. **Online recruitment platforms** - 26%
2. **Personal research & contacts** - 11%
3. **Recruitment agency** - 9%
4. **Traditional media** - 7%
5. **Social media** - 6%

Additionally, **41%** of respondents said they use **ALL** of these methods combined in their job search.
