The two main categories of COVID-19 test, per this guidance, are:
1.  **Swab test**: Checks if you *currently have* COVID-19 (answers the question *"Do I have it?"*)
2.  **Antibody test**: Checks if you *have previously had* COVID-19 (answers the question *"Have I had it?"*), and it has two further sub-types: laboratory antibody test, and home finger-prick antibody test.
