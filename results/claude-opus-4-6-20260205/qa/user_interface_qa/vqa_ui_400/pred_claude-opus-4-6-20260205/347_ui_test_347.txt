# Operation Phantom Fury (2004) - Force Strength Comparison

The **Coalition** had significantly more strength with approximately **13,350 total troops** broken down as:
- 10,500 U.S.
- 2,000 Iraqi
- 850 U.K.

Compared to the **Insurgents** who had approximately **4,000** men.

So the Coalition forces outnumbered the Insurgents by more than 3 to 1.
