## Calculating Household Expenditure

Based on the **Average Annual Energy Expenditures per Household in the U.S.** from the infographic:

| Category | Cost |
|----------|------|
| **Other (Lighting and Electricity)** | $827 |
| **Refrigeration** | $153 |
| **Water Heating** | $280 |
| **Total** | **$1,260** |

The household would incur an expenditure of **$1,260** per year for a well-lit home with a refrigerator and water heater.
