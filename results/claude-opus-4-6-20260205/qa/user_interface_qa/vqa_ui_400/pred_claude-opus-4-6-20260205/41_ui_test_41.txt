## Key Findings from the Infographic

Looking at the **"States with Highest Employment"** column for each journalism career:

**Editors** - The states listed are: **NY, CA, TX, MR** (with NY appearing first)

**Reporters and Correspondents** - The states listed are: **NY, CA, FL, OH, TX**
