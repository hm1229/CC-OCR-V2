She turned back to the desk, picking up a small notebook. It was filled with notes and sketches. She flipped through it, reading each page

She looked around again, but there was still no one. She closed the notebook and put it back on the desk. Then she took the key and  
walked towards the door.

She walked down the hall, passing by several rooms. Each one was empty. She finally reached the end of the hall and turned left.
