To find the percentage of Malaysian online shoppers who **do not** shop online a few times per year, we subtract the percentage of those who do from 100%.

From the chart:
- **56.7%** of online shoppers purchase over the internet **a few times per year**.

So, the percentage who **do not** shop online a few times per year is:

$$
100\% - 56.7\% = 43.3\%
$$

### ✅ Answer: **43.3%** of Malaysian online shoppers do not shop online a few times per year.
