The English Football Association (The FA) was founded in **1863**.

This is clearly indicated in the infographic at the bottom left, which states:

> **THE FA**  
> THE ENGLISH FOOTBALL ASSOCIATION CELEBRATES ITS 150TH ANNIVERSARY THIS YEAR  
> **1863 — 150 YEARS — 2013**

Since the infographic was created in 2013 (marking the 150th anniversary), subtracting 150 years from 2013 gives us **1863** as the founding year.

✅ So, the answer is: **1863**

*(Note: The FA is the oldest football association in the world and played a key role in codifying the rules of modern football.)*
