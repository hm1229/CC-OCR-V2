## Federal Government Expenses That Could Be Saved If Drugs Were Decriminalized

According to the infographic, if drugs were decriminalized, the **Federal Government would save $15.6 Billion** annually.

This breaks down by drug type as follows:

- **Marijuana**: $8.7 Billion
- **Cocaine & Heroin**: $20 Billion (this appears to be a combined state and federal figure)
- **Other Drugs**: $12.6 Billion

The total annual government savings (combining both state/local and federal) would be:
- **$25.7 Billion** in State & Local Government expenses
- **$15.6 Billion** in Federal Government expenses

So the direct answer to your question is **$15.6 Billion** in federal government expenses could be saved annually if drugs were decriminalized.
