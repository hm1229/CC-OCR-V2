## Support for Children with Additional Learning Needs

According to the infographic, Torfaen County Borough Council provided the following support for children with additional learning needs:

- **1,000 days of support** specifically for children with additional learning needs
- **1,200 laptops** made available for digitally excluded pupils at home
- **550 MiFi devices** made available for digitally excluded pupils at home
- **4,600 days of support** provided at **childcare hubs**

These measures were implemented during the period of **March – 12 June 2020** as part of the Council's response to the Covid-19 crisis.
