The subject of the memorandum is:

**Change of Due Dates for Monthly Payroll Recommendations for December – Academic and Nonacademic**

This memo informs Department Heads that, due to a reduced number of working days in December, the deadline for submitting payroll recommendations has been changed. Recommendations must now be submitted no later than **December 14, 1967**, to accommodate the Payroll Office’s revised schedule. Paychecks will still be issued as usual on **December 29, 1967, at 2:00 p.m.**
