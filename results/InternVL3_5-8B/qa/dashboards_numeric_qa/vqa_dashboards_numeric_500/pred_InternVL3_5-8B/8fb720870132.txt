During the pre-match phase, the two aspects of training and fatigue management that should be at their highest levels are **Preparedness** and **Fitness**. 

1. **Preparedness**: This refers to the mental and physical readiness of the players. It encompasses their strategic understanding of the game, their confidence, and their ability to execute the game plan effectively. High preparedness ensures that players are mentally sharp and ready to perform at their best during the match.

2. **Fitness**: This involves the physical condition and endurance of the players. High fitness levels ensure that players have the stamina, strength, and agility required to perform throughout the match. It includes cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, flexibility, and recovery capacity.

Both preparedness and fitness are critical to ensure optimal performance and minimize the risk of injuries during the match.
