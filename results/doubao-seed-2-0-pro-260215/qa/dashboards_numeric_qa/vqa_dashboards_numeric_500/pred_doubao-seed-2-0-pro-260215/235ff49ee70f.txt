To get the total, add the two support amounts:
1.  Direct support for Canadian workers and businesses: **$27 billion**
2.  Business liquidity support via tax deferrals: **$55 billion**

Total = $27B + $55B = **$82 billion** (CAD, Canadian dollars)
*The $5B Emergency Support Benefit is part of the $27B direct support pool, so it is not added separately.*
