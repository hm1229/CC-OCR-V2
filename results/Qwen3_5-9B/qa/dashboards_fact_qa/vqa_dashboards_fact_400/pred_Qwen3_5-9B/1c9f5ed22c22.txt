As of March 2014, Yahoo had a **10.1%** market share in the U.S. search engine market.

This is shown in the “Where are people searching?” section of the infographic, which breaks down search engine market shares as of March 2014:

- Google: 67.5%
- Bing: 18.6%
- **Yahoo: 10.1%**
- Ask: 2.5%
- AOL: 1.3%

So, the answer is **10.1%**.
