Factor the difference of two squares.
\[
(16x^4 - 81)
\]

\begin{itemize}
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(2x+3)^2(2x-3)^2$
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(4x^2 + 9)(4x^2 + 9)$
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(4x^2 + 9)(2x+3)(2x-3)$
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(4x^2 + 9)(4x^2 - 9)$
\end{itemize}

\noindent\textbf{Question 8}

Factor using the formula for the sum or difference of two cubes.
\[
27x^3 + 64
\]

\begin{itemize}
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(3x+4)(9x^2 + 12x + 16)$
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(3x+4)(9x^2 + 16)$
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(3x+4)(9x^2 - 12x + 16)$
    \item $\bigcirc$ $(3x-4)(9x^2 + 12x + 16)$
\end{itemize}
