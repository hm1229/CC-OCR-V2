Based on the infographic provided, there are two emergency benefits listed for working Canadians:

*   **Emergency Care Benefit:** This is for those who must stay home due to COVID-19 and do not have paid sick leave. It includes individuals who cannot access EI and are sick, or are taking care of a child or someone who is sick. It provides up to $900 bi-weekly for up to 15 weeks.
*   **Emergency Support Benefit:** This provides up to $5 billion in support to workers who are not eligible for EI and are facing unemployment and reduced hours.
