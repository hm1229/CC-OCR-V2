The Dubai Sevens tournament was founded in **1970**.

This is clearly stated in the infographic:  
> **“1970 — THE YEAR THE TOURNAMENT STARTED IN DUBAI”**

Additionally, it notes that since 1970, there have been **11 different international team winners** of the tournament.

So, while the modern “Dubai Rugby 7s” as part of the HSBC World Rugby Sevens Series began later (and Emirates became sponsor in 1987), the original tournament itself dates back to **1970**.

✅ **Answer: 1970**
