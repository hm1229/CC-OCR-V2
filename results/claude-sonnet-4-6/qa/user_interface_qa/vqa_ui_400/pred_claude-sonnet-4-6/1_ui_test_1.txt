## Largest Indicator of the Impact of Happiness

According to the infographic, **125% less burnout than their peers** is the largest indicator shown in the "Impact of Happiness" section for thriving (happy) employees.

This significantly outpaces the other metrics:
- 16% better overall job performance
- **46% more satisfied with their jobs**
- 32% more committed to the organization
- **125% less burnout than their peers** ← **Largest**

So the answer is **low exhaustion (less burnout)** at **125%**, which is by far the most dramatic statistic among the four indicators listed.
