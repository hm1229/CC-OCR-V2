## Disability Chance for Senior Citizens (65+)

According to the infographic, senior citizens have a significantly higher chance of disability:

- **50.7%** of people aged **65 and older** have a disability (1.8 million people)
- **2 in 5** (40%) of all people with disability are **65 years or older**
- The infographic explicitly states that **"the likelihood of living with disability increases with age"**

This is in stark contrast to those **under 65**, where only **12.5%** (1 in 8) have a disability — meaning seniors are roughly **4 times more likely** to have a disability compared to younger Australians.
