Based on the infographic “Romance Survey United States 2012” by hotels.com, under the section **“Where’s the most romantic place for hanky panky in a hotel?”**, the top 3 voted romantic places are:

1. **The Bed** – 32%  
2. **The Pool** – 28%  
3. **The Balcony** – 15%

*(Note: “The Tub” is at 12%, “The Couch” at 5%, and “Other” at 8% — so they fall outside the top 3.)*

✅ So, the top 3 are:  
**1. The Bed (32%)**  
**2. The Pool (28%)**  
**3. The Balcony (15%)**

This data reflects what respondents *most liked* to find in their hotel room on Valentine’s Day — specifically, where they’d prefer to be intimate.
