To determine the life expectancy of males in Australia, refer to the "LIFE EXPECTANCY COMPARED TO AUSTRALIA" section.  

- The icons: A blue cross represents **males**, and a pink cross represents **females** (consistent with typical gender color coding, where blue = male, pink = female, and female life expectancy is higher than male, which matches the numbers: 79.4 for blue/male and 84.4 for pink/female in Australia).
