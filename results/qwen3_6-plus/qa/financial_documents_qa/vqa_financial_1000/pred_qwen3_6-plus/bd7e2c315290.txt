The **“From” period** on this NYC-1 (PNS) form is:

> **2/1/74**

This indicates the start date of the reporting period for which the employer is filing the return — in this case, **February 1, 1974**.

The corresponding **“To” period** is **2/28/74**, meaning this return covers wages and tax withheld during the entire month of February 1974.

---

✅ So, to directly answer your question:  
**“From” Period = February 1, 1974 (2/1/74)**

This is clearly printed in the box labeled “FROM:” on the left side of the form.
