To find which day December 25, 2006 falls on, I need to look at the calendar.

Looking at the calendar for December 2006:
- December 1 is on Friday
- December 25 is in the row that shows 24, 25, 26, 27, 28, 29, 30

Following the column headers:
- Sun: 24, 31
- Mon: 25 (with "Holiday" noted below it)
- Tue: 26
- Wed: 27
- Thu: 28
- Fri: 29
- Sat: 30

December 25, 2006 falls on **Monday** (and it's noted as a Holiday, which is Christmas Day).
