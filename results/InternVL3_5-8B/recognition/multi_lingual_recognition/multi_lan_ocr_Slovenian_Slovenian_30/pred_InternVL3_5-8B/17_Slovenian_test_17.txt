19Mme a mo robatsa fa mangoleneng a gagwe; a bitsa monna mongwe, a beola makaoi a supa a thogo ya gagwe; a simolola go mo koafatsa, le thata ya gagwe ya mo togelera.

20Mosadi a re: "Sameone, Bafilisitia ba a go wela." Mme a kubuga mo borokong a ithaya a re: "Ke tlaa tswele kwa ntle jaaka gale ke ikudubatsa." A ba a sa isite fa Jehovah a mo tlogetshe.

21Bafilisitia ba mo tshwara, ba mo gonya matho; ba mo isa kwa Gasa, ba mo golega ka mefiri ya kgotho; a sela mo ntlong ya kgolegele.

22Le fa go nts'e jalole moriri wa thogo ya gagwe wa simolola go thoga a sena go beolwa.

Losi Iwag a Sameone

23Barena ba Bafilisitia ba phuthega ba tla go isetsa Dagone modimo wa bone thabelo e kgolo le go itumela; gone ba re: "Modimo wa rona o tsentsse Sameone, mmaba wa rona mo seatleng sa rona."

24E rile batho ba mmona, ba boka modimo wa bone, gone ba re: "Modimo wa rona o re tsentsse mmaba wa rona seatleng, le mosenyi wa lefatshela rona, yo bolaieng ba rona ba le bantsi."

25Ga dirala ya re dipelo ts'a bone di ira mokete, ba re: "Bitshang Sameone gore a tle go re dirala ditshameko." Ba bitsa Sameone kwa ntlong ya kgolegele; a dira ditshameko fa pele ga bone; ba mmaya fa gare ga maotswana ntlo.

26Sameone a raya mosimane yo o mo tshwereng ka letsogo a re: "Ntse ka apaape maotswana a ntlo e emeng mo go one, gore ke ikaege mo go one."

27Jaana ntlo ya bo e tlets'e banna le basadi; le barena bothle ba Bafilisitia ba le gone; le mo godimo ga ntlo go ne go le banna le basadi, e ka ne e le 3 000, ba ba lebileleng Sameone a dira ditshameko.

28Sameone a biletsa kwa go Jehovah a re: "Ao, Jehovah Modimo, ke a go rapela, a ko o nkgo pole, o nthatafatshe, ka lobaka lono fela Modimo, gore ke ipusolosetse Bafilisitia ka bonako ka ntla ya matho a me a mabedi."

29Sameone a tshwara maotswana a mabedi a gare, a ntlo e emeng mo go one, a ikaega mo go one, mo go lengwe ka letsogo le legolo, mo go le lengwe ka la molema.

30Sameone a re: "A ke swe le Bafilisitia." A inama ka thata yothle ya gagwe; mme ntlo ya wela mo bareng, le mo bathong bothle ba ba mo go yone. Jalo baswi ba bolaieng ka lobaka lwa losi iwag a gagwe ba ne ba le bantsi go feta ba o ba bolaieng a sa tsedile.
