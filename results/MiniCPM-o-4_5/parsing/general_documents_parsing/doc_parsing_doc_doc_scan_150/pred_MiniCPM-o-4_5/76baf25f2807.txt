\section*{Exercise}

Karen is writing an article about Vivienne Tam for the school newspaper. Help her to complete the article using the correct passive form of the verbs in brackets. One of the verbs is negative.

Fashion designer Vivienne Tam \textbf{(1)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (name) 'Yin Yok' when she was born. She \textbf{(2)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (give) her English name when she started studying in Hong Kong. She \textbf{(3)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (teach) English at school but spoke Cantonese at home. She \textbf{(4)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (tell) to always remember her Chinese culture. At the same time, western ideas \textbf{(5)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (introduce) to her since people from many cultures were living in Hong Kong. This mix of East and West in her life \textbf{(6)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (see) in her designs even now.

Vivienne moved to New York when she was twenty-five. Her new and interesting designs \textbf{(7)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (like) by important shops there, so a lot of orders for her clothing \textbf{(8)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (place). Today her East-meets-West clothes \textbf{(9)} \underline{\hspace{2cm}} (sell) only in New York, but also in cities around the world.
