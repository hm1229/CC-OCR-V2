## Developmental Benefits of Early Childhood Education

According to the infographic, there are **three key developmental benefits**:

---

### 🤝 Social and Emotional
- Students develop **relationships** with adults and peers
- Gain a **sense of security**
- Experience **greater participation and cooperation**

---

### 🧠 Cognitive
- Access to **age-appropriate learning** through developmental curriculum
- Students **build on existing skills**, increasing knowledge
- Teachers help reach **important developmental milestones**

---

### 🗣️ Language
- Language **develops quickly** between ages 1-5
- Routine interactions help **develop language skills**
- **More advanced skills emerge** as conversations and vocabulary progress

---

These developmental benefits lay the foundation for the **long-term benefits** highlighted in the infographic, including greater student success and adult responsibility.
