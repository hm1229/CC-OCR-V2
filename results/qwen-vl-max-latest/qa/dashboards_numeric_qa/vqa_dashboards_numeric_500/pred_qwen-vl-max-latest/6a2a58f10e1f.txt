The pie chart shows the share of the world's population by country, based on UN data from 2019.

- **India** accounts for **18%** of the world's population.
- **United States** accounts for **4%** of the world's population.

To find the gap between India and the US:

$$
18\% - 4\% = 14\%
$$

### ✅ The gap in the population percentage between India and the United States is **14 percentage points**.
