To find how much more the total civilian casualties per month were in 2012 compared to 2011, we subtract the 2011 monthly total from the 2012 monthly total.

From the infographic:

- 2011: 2,510 total civilian casualties per month
- 2012: 2,882 total civilian casualties per month

Now calculate the difference:

2,882 - 2,510 = 372
