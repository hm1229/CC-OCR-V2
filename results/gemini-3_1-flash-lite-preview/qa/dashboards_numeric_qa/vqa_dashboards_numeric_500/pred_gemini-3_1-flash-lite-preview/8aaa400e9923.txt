Based on the infographic, the developmental benefits of early childhood education are categorized into three main areas:

*   **Social and Emotional:**
    *   Students develop relationships by being surrounded by adults and peers.
    *   The constant presence of these people provides a sense of security.
    *   This feeling of security leads to greater participation and cooperation.

*   **Cognitive:**
    *   Students receive age-appropriate learning through a developmental curriculum.
    *   They build on existing skills, which increases their knowledge.
    *   Teachers help ensure students reach important developmental milestones.

*   **Language:**
    *   Language develops quickly between the ages of 1 and 5.
    *   Routine interactions with teachers and peers help students develop language skills.
    *   As conversations and vocabulary progress, more advanced skills emerge.
