Based on the infographic, there are actually **zero** appliances that suck up electricity when *not* plugged in (as that would be physically impossible).

However, the section on **"Vampire Appliances"** lists **10** types of appliances and gadgets that suck up electricity while **plugged in** even though they are switched off. These are:

1.  Flat Screen TVs
2.  Gaming Console
3.  Computers
4.  DVRs / Cable Boxes
5.  DVD / VCR
6.  Space Heater
7.  Phones
8.  Printer
9.  Router / Modem
10. Kitchen Appliances
