## Two Types of Coronavirus (COVID-19) Tests

According to the infographic, the two main types of coronavirus tests are:

1. **Swab Test** ("Do I have it?")
   - Tests for a **current infection**
   - Uses a swab sample from the throat and nose

2. **Antibody Test** ("Have I had it?")
   - Tests for a **past infection**
   - Available in two forms:
     - **Laboratory test** – blood test taken by a medical professional
     - **Home test** – 'finger-prick' test taken at home
