To determine which lasts longer once opened, we analyze the “BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD” section:  

- **Canned dog food**: Once opened, store at <40°F and keeps for 3 days (refrigerated).  
- **Dry dog food**: Once opened, keep for 90 days (store in a cool, dry place).  

Thus, **dry dog food** can be stored for a longer time once opened.
