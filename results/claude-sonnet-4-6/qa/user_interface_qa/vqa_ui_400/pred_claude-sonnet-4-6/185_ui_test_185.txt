## Difference in After-Tax Earnings

According to the infographic:

- **Washington D.C. resident** needs **$53,000** in annual after-tax earnings
- **Kentucky resident** earns **$40,000** in after-tax earnings

### $53,000 - $40,000 = **$13,000 more**

A Washington D.C. resident needs to earn **$13,000 more** than a Kentucky resident to achieve an equal standard of living, due to tax and cost of goods variations.
