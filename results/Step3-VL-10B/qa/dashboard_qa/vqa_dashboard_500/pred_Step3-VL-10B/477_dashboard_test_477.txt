To determine the emergency benefits for working Canadians, we analyze the image:  

### 1. Emergency Care Benefit  
- **Eligibility**: For individuals who must stay home due to COVID - 19 and do not have paid sick leave. This includes people who can’t access Employment Insurance (EI) while sick, or those caring for a sick child.  
- **Amount**: Up to $900 bi - weekly, for a maximum of 15 weeks.    

### 2. Emergency Support Benefit  
- **Eligibility**: For workers who are not eligible for EI and are facing unemployment or reduced working hours.  
- **Total Support**: Up to $5 billion in support for this group.    

Additionally, the image shows broader financial support for working Canadians:  
- $27 billion for *direct support to Canadian workers and businesses*.  
- $55 billion for *business liquidity support via tax deferrals*.  

However, the **specific “emergency benefits”** (clearly labeled with “Emergency” in the image) are the *Emergency Care Benefit* and *Emergency Support Benefit* outlined above.
