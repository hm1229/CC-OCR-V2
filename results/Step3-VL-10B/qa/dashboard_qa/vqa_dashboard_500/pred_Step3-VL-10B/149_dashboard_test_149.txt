To find the difference in percentage of men and women who work out several times a week, we first locate the "Several times a week" category in the "WORKOUT HABITS" section.  

- For women, the percentage is 32%.  
- For men, the percentage is 40%.  

Then, calculate the difference: \( 40\% - 32\% = 8\% \).  


The difference in percentage is 8 percentage points.
