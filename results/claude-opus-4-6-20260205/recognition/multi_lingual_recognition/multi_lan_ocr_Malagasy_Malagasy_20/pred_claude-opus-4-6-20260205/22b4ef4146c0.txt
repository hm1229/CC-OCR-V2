2 Misy fahavalo araiky maniañà atsika jiaby. Tsy ino
izio fa fahafatesaña. Atsika tsisy mahavita miskana. Fa izio
tsy ho avy. Efa maro koa olo haintsika nazony. Fa Je-
hovah avy. mahery maro olo haintsika nazony. Fa Je-
fanahy, fotony izy efa maniañò raha hahavotaña atsika,
baka atsika tsy hofoaty. Amy 1 Korintianina 15:26 mi-
volana fa 'fahafatesaña io fahavalo farany hitongany
Zanahary.' Tena mampararavo vaovao zeñy!
