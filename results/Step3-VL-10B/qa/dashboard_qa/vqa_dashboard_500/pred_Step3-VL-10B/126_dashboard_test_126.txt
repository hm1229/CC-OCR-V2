To determine how much more total civilian casualties per month there were in 2012 compared to 2011, we subtract the 2011 monthly casualty figure from the 2012 figure:  

- 2011 total civilian casualties per month: 2,510  
- 2012 total civilian casualties per month: 2,882  

Calculate the difference: \( 2,882 - 2,510 = 372 \)  


So, there were 372 more total civilian casualties per month in 2012 than in 2011.
