The infographic shows that the percentage of Scots pine sites within 1 km of a Dothistroma Needle Blight (DNB) outbreak increased from **9% in 2007** to **61% in 2013**.

To calculate the increase:

61% - 9% = **52%**

So, the increase in percentage of DNB outbreak from 2007 to 2013 is **52 percentage points**. 

(Note: The infographic does not provide data for 2016 specifically for DNB outbreaks, only for 2007 and 2013.)
