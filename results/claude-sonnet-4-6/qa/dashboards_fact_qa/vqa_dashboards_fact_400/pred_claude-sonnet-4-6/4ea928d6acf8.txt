## Ingredients for Akavit

Based on the infographic, **Akavit** requires the following ingredients:

1. **Grain**
2. **Water**
3. **Yeast (Starter)**
4. **Caraway**

So, **4 ingredients** are required for the preparation of Akavit.

*(The remaining steps — Boiled, Fermented in Container, and Distilled — are **processes**, not ingredients.)*
