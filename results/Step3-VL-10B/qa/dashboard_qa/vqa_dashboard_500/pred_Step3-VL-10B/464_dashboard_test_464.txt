To determine the difference in the percentage of reduced spending on luxury items between Generation Z and Millennials, we analyze the section “Consumers are more concerned with essential everyday items”:  

- For **Generation Z**: 27% stated their spend on luxury products had reduced.  
- For **Millennials**: 23% stated their spend on luxury products had decreased.  

Calculate the difference: \( 27\% - 23\% = 4\% \).  


The difference in the percentage of reduced spending on luxury items by Generation Z and Millennials is 4 percentage points.
