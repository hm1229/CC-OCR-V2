## Three Basic Approaches for Organizing Websites

According to the infographic "The Science Behind Google," the three basic approaches that **all modern search engines** use for organizing websites are:

1. 🕷️ **Crawling**
   - Search engine bots follow links to browse the billions of pages that make up the web

2. 📊 **Sorting**
   - Results get sorted by a variety of factors (rankings, relevance, etc.)

3. 📁 **Indexing**
   - Sorted pages get stored in an index so search engines can match queries with results faster
