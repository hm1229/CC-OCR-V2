## Groups Most Likely to Have Purchased:

### Solar Panels / Photovoltaic (PV):
Based on the infographic, **Democrats 🔵 and Republicans 🟠** are the groups most likely to have purchased solar panels (both are shown as top purchasers, with the note "We're all on the same page!").

### All-Electric Vehicles:
**Democrats 🔵 and Republicans 🟠** are also the groups most likely to have purchased all-electric vehicles.

In both cases, **Independents** appear less likely to have purchased these technologies compared to Democrats and Republicans.
