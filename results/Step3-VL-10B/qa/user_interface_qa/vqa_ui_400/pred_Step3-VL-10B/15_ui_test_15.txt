To determine the percentage of U.S. students with access to all kinds of digital technologies at home, we refer to the **"ACCESS TO INTERNET & DIGITAL TECHNOLOGIES"** section of the infographic.  

- Look at the "At Home" column (left - hand side of this section).  
- Identify the "All or almost all" category (marked by the dark green color in the legend).  
- The value associated with this category for "At Home" is **54%**.  


Thus, 54% of U.S. students have access to all kinds of digital technologies at their home, as reported by teachers.
