## Calculation

In **2003**, **57.2%** of academic leaders believed online education was the same or even superior to face-to-face learning.

**Two years later (2005)**, that number increased to **77%**.

**Difference: 77% - 57.2% = 19.8%**

So, **19.8% more** academic leaders in 2005 found online education the same or even superior to face-to-face learning compared to 2003.
