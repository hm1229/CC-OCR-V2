## Ethiopia Contributes the Most Women as UN Military Peacekeeping Forces

According to the infographic, **Ethiopia** contributes the most women in the **military** category with **394** personnel, making it the top contributor among the "Top Five Female Uniformed Personnel Contributing Countries."

For the **police** category, **Rwanda** leads with **183** female police personnel.

Overall, Ethiopia ranks **#1** in total female uniformed military contributions to UN peacekeeping missions.
