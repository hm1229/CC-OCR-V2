Determining the "best" method for job search can vary depending on individual circumstances, industry, and personal preferences. However, based on the infographic, here are some insights into the most effective methods:

1. **Online Recruitment Platforms (26%)**: These platforms aggregate job listings from various sources, making it easier to find opportunities in one place. They often have advanced search features and can save time.

2. **Personal Research & Contacts (11%)**: Networking and leveraging personal connections can be highly effective. Many jobs are filled through referrals, and personal research can provide valuable insights into companies and roles.

3. **Recruitment Agencies (9%)**: These agencies specialize in matching job seekers with suitable positions. They can offer personalized services and access to exclusive job listings.

4. **Traditional Media (7%)**: While less common today, traditional media such as newspapers and magazines can still be useful, especially in certain industries or regions.

5. **Social Media (6%)**: Platforms like LinkedIn are powerful tools for job searching. They allow you to connect with professionals, join industry groups, and apply for jobs directly.

6. **Researching the Company (52.4%)**: This is crucial for interview preparation and showing genuine interest in the role. Understanding the company's mission, values, and culture can help tailor your application and interview responses.

7. **Relaxing to Ease Nerves (33.9%)**: Mental preparation is important for performing well in interviews. Techniques such as meditation or exercise can help manage stress.

8. **Practicing Interview Questions (32.9%)**: Preparation is key to success in interviews. Practicing common interview questions can boost confidence and improve performance.

9. **Videotaping Themselves (3.2%)**: While not a primary method, recording oneself can be a useful tool for practicing presentations and interviews.

### Conclusion
While all methods have their merits, **online recruitment platforms** and **personal research & contacts** are highlighted as the most popular and potentially effective methods. Combining these with thorough company research and interview preparation can significantly enhance your job search efforts.
