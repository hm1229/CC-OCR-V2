Based on the infographic, **1/3** (or approximately 33.3%) of all mobile phone users will access Instagram at least once a month in 2016.

To find the percentage of users who are **not** using it at least once a month, you subtract that portion from the whole:

1 - 1/3 = **2/3**

Therefore, approximately **66.7%** of all mobile phone users are not using Instagram at least once a month in 2016.
