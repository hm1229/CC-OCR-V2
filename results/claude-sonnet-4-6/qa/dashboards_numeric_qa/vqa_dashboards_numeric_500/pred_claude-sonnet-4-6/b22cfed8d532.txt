## Protein Group - What to Eliminate

According to the infographic, in the **Protein Group**, you should:

**Eliminate processed meats that contain chemicals or additives** — this is noted as a distinction **not made by MyPlate**.

The infographic recommends eating protein from a variety of sources including:
- 🫘 Beans
- 🥜 Legumes & nuts
- 🥚 Eggs
- 🐟 Wild-caught fish
- 🥛 Pasture-based dairy and meats
