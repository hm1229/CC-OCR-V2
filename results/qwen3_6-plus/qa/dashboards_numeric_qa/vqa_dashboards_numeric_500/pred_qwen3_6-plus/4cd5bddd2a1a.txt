According to the infographic, **60% of drivers did not consider colour as an important factor**.

That means the remaining **40% *did* consider colour as an important factor**.

This is derived from:

> “60% of drivers did not consider colour as an important factor...”

So, if 60% did *not* consider it important, then:

**100% - 60% = 40%** considered it important.
