To determine the growth in the percentage of Australians online from 2003 to 2013, we locate the relevant data points:  

- In 2003, the percentage of Australians 16+ online was 73%.  
- In 2013, the percentage of Australians 16+ online was 82%.  

To find the growth, subtract the 2003 value from the 2013 value:  
\( 82\% - 73\% = 9\% \)  


The percentage of Australians online grew by 9 percentage points from 2003 to 2013.
