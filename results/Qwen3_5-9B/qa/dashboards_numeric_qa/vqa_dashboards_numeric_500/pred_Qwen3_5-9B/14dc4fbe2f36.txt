Based on the infographic, under the section **“Consumers are experimenting, purchasing items they would not usually purchase online”**, we see:

- **Overall**: 39% of respondents said the lockdown encouraged them to try and buy goods online they had not considered before.
- **Millennials**: 52% said the same.

To calculate the **increase in percentage from overall consumers to millennials**:

> 52% (Millennials) – 39% (Overall) = **13 percentage points**

---

✅ **Answer: 13%**

*(Note: This is a 13 percentage point increase, not a 13% relative increase. The question asks for “increase in percentage,” which typically refers to absolute difference in this context.)*
