- **Audit Objective**: Evaluate whether the quality management subsystem for Production and Process Controls has been effectively implemented.
- **Scope**:
  - Review effectiveness of corrective actions previously identified during internal audits.
  - Focus only on specific quality subsystems related to production and process controls; it is not a full assessment of the entire Quality System.
