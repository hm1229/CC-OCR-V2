The candidate for president is officially chosen at the **National Party Conventions**.

As shown in the infographic under "How Do the Candidates Win?", the process involves:

1. **Caucuses and State Primaries**: Voters select delegates for their preferred presidential nominee.
2. **Delegates from each state** go to the **National Party Conventions**.
3. At these conventions, the party officially chooses its candidate for president.

So, the final selection of the presidential candidate happens at the **National Party Conventions**.
