Based on the map provided, the total number of CAR refugees since January 2013 is the sum of the figures in the blue boxes pointing to neighboring countries:

*   **Chad:** 14,477
*   **Cameroon:** 4,859
*   **Congo:** 10,748
*   **Democratic Republic of the Congo:** 56,309

Total = 14,477 + 4,859 + 10,748 + 56,309 = **86,393**

Note: While the question asks for refugees "in" the Central African Republic, refugees are by definition people who have fled their country. People displaced within the country are called **IDPs** (Internally Displaced Persons), and that number is **935,000** (indicated by the red box).
