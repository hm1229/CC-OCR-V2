According to the infographic, the **DEMI** cup size is:

> **3 oz (89 ml)**

It’s described as a **“secret size”** that is **off the menu**, and it’s basically used for **Espresso shots**.

---

✅ So, the size of DEMI = **3 fluid ounces (89 milliliters)**

This is smaller than the “Short” (8 oz), which is the smallest standard size listed on the menu. The Demi is typically used internally or for espresso-based drinks where only a shot or two are needed — not usually offered as a standalone beverage size to customers.

---

*Note: While not officially marketed, baristas may use this size for precision in crafting espresso drinks.*
