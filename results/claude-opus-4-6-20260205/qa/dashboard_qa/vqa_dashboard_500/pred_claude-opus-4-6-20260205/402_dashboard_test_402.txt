## Why the Majority of Millennials Choose Online Viewing

According to the infographic, the **top reason** millennials choose online viewing is **Immediate Access**, cited by **56%** of respondents.

The three reasons listed are:

1. **Immediate Access** – **56%**
2. **Multitasking** – **49%**
3. **Convenience of viewing location** – **44%**

So the majority of millennials choose online viewing primarily because of the **immediate access** it provides to content, allowing them to watch what they want, when they want, without waiting.
