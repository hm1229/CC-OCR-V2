The stage of the process where the company finally decides to make a product and produce enough quantity to satisfy the market is called the production stage. In this stage, the company has to decide how much to produce and what resources are needed. The company also has to decide how to distribute the product to the market. The production stage is very important because it determines the success of the product in the market. If the company produces too much, it may not be able to sell all the products. If the company produces too little, it may not be able to satisfy the demand of the market. Therefore, the company has to carefully plan the production stage to ensure the success of the product.

The production stage is also important because it affects the cost of the product. The company has to decide how much to spend on production and how to minimize the cost. The company also has to decide how to maintain the quality of the product while minimizing the cost. The production stage is a critical part of the product development process and requires careful planning and execution.
