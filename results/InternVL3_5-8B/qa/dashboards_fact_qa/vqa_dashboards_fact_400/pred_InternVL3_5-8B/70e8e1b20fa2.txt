To determine the percentage of free apps in the iPhone and Android stores taken together, we need to analyze the provided infographic data on the distribution of paid versus free apps in these stores.

1. **iPhone App Store:**
   - Paid apps: 29%
   - Free apps: 71%

2. **Android Market:**
   - Paid apps: 41%
   - Free apps: 60%

To find the combined percentage of free apps, we first calculate the individual contributions of free apps from each store and then combine them.

### Step-by-Step Calculation:

1. **Calculate the percentage of free apps in each store:**
   - iPhone: 71% of apps are free.
   - Android: 60% of apps are free.

2. **Assume equal number of apps in both stores for simplicity:**
   - Let's assume there are 100 apps in each store for simplicity.

3. **Calculate the number of free apps in each store:**
   - iPhone: 71 free apps out of 100.
   - Android: 60 free apps out of 100.

4. **Total number of apps and total number of free apps:**
   - Total apps: 100 (iPhone) + 100 (Android) = 200 apps.
   - Total free apps: 71 (iPhone) + 60 (Android) = 131 free apps.

5. **Calculate the combined percentage of free apps:**
   \[
   \text{Percentage of free apps} = \left( \frac{\text{Total free apps}}{\text{Total apps}} \right) \times 100 = \left( \frac{131}{200} \right) \times 100 = 65.5\%
   \]

### Conclusion:
The percentage of free apps in the iPhone and Android stores taken together is **65.5%**.
