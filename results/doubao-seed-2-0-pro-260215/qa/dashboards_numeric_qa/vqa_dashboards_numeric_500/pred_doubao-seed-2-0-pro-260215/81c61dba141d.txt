Australia has **6 states** in total, plus two major self-governing internal territories (the Northern Territory/NT and Australian Capital Territory/ACT, as seen at the bottom of this image, are the territories, not counted as states):
The 6 states are:
1.  New South Wales (NSW)
2.  Victoria (VIC)
3.  Queensland (QLD)
4.  Western Australia (WA)
5.  South Australia (SA)
6.  Tasmania (TAS)
