## The Gift Gender Gap Analysis

The two aspects considered in the **Gift Gender Gap** analysis are:

1. **"Women Do All the Shopping"** — examining the percentage of men and women who agree that women handle all the gift-buying responsibility
   - 49% of **men** agree
   - 75% of **women** agree

2. **"Couples Share the Responsibility"** — examining the percentage of men and women who agree that gift-buying is a shared responsibility
   - 33% of **men** agree
   - 20% of **women** agree
