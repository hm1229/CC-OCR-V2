The document is an **Audit Agenda** for a **Production & Process Controls Audit** at **DePuy Orthopaedics, Inc., Warsaw, IN**, dated **13–17 July 2009**.

It outlines:

- **Audit objective:** assess compliance with **21 CFR 820** and **ISO 13485:2003** for selected parts of the Quality Management System.
- **Audit scope:** evaluate whether the **Production and Process Controls** quality management subsystem has been effectively implemented, review prior corrective actions, and clarify that this is **not a full quality system assessment**.

So, in short, it is about a **quality management audit plan focused on production and process controls**.
