## Durability Comparison: Resistive vs. Infrared

**Infrared** touchscreens are **more durable** than Resistive touchscreens.

- **Resistive**: Only **Fair Durability** — screens are vulnerable to scratches and other minor damage, and the screen will wear over time and need replacing.
- **Infrared**: **Good Durability** — glass shatters upon major impact only.

So Infrared clearly offers better durability compared to Resistive technology.
