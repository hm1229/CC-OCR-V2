To find this, look at the **INIHAW (Barbecued)** section, count items made from chicken parts:
1.  *Isaw (chicken intestines)*
2.  *Adidas (chicken feet)*
3.  *Helmet (chicken heads)*

Betamax (blood can be pork *or* chicken, not a specific chicken part) and Pork BBQ/Pig skin are pork/non-chicken, so they are not counted.

Answer: **3**
