According to the 2013 Survey of Digital Technology and Internet Use by Statistics Canada, the value of gross sales over the internet to the United States was 15%. This indicates that a relatively small portion of Canadian businesses' online sales were directed towards the U.S. market compared to other destinations. The majority of the gross sales over the internet went to Canada itself, accounting for 80%, while only 6% went to other countries. This distribution highlights the significant domestic focus of Canadian businesses in terms of their online sales during that period.
