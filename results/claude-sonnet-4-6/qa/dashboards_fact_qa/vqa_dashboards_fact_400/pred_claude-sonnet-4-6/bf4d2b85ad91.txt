## AOL, Inc.

According to the **Search Engine Intelligence** section of the infographic, **AOL, Inc.** has the highest Q&A intelligence index at **77%**, followed by:

- Ask Network: 64%
- Yahoo: 55%
- Bing: 50%
- Google: 29%

Interestingly, despite Google dominating **search engine popularity** (67% of searches), it scores the **lowest** on the Q&A Intelligence Index.
