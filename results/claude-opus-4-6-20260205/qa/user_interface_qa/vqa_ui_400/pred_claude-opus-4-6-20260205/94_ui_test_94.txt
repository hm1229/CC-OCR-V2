## Difference in Percentage Approval at Inauguration between Truman and Clinton

According to the infographic:

- **Truman's** % Public Approval at Inauguration: **88**
- **Clinton's** % Public Approval at Inauguration: **55**

**Difference: 88 - 55 = 33 percentage points**

Truman had a 33 percentage point higher approval rating at inauguration compared to Clinton.
