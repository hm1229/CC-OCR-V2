{\fontsize{20}{24}\selectfont \textbf{\textcolor{sjsublue}{INTERNATIONAL APPLICANT FINANCIAL FORM}}}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
International applicants must affirm that they are responsible for paying all tuition, fees and living expenses for the entire period of intended study by completing the Declaration of Finance form. In order to issue your I-20, we are required to collect documentation that shows you have funds available to pay for the total estimated expense for the first year of your academic program. Estimated expenses include tuition, fees, living expenses and health insurance. Actual expenses may vary.

\noindent
Financial documents that demonstrate proof of funding must be in English and must:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Be on official bank letterhead with a stamp/seal and/or bank officer's signature.
    \item Demonstrate at least the minimum total estimated expenses for one academic year in your academic program.
    \item Include an issue date that is within the recent 6 months of when you submit your online application.
    \item Include the account holder's name.
    \item Include a specific amount in dollars (USD).
\end{itemize}

% --- Dependent Support ---
\noindent
{\large \textbf{\textcolor{sjsublue}{DEPENDENT SUPPORT}}}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
Students wishing to have a spouse or children accompany them to San José State University (SJSU) must be able to provide proof of funding for an additional \$5,000 for each family member. A copy of marriage certificate and passport are required for adding a spouse. A copy of birth certificate(s) and passport(s) are required for each child. Additional proof of funding will also be required. *Please note a dependent cannot also be a sponsor.
