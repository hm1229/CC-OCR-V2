To determine the percentage of respondents in the UAE not using credit cards, we first identify the percentage of those **using** credit cards. The infographic states that 72% are using credit cards. Since the total percentage of all respondents is 100%, we subtract the percentage of users from 100%:  

\( 100\% - 72\% = 28\% \)  


Thus, 28% of respondents living in the UAE are not using credit cards according to the survey.
