### Standard Scientific Answer (for completeness):
In general chemistry, the **four fundamental states (phases) of matter** are:

- **Solid** – fixed shape and volume
- **Liquid** – fixed volume, takes shape of container
- **Gas** – no fixed shape or volume; expands to fill container
- **Plasma** – ionized gas, conducts electricity, found in stars and lightning

*(Some advanced contexts include Bose-Einstein condensates or fermionic condensates, but those are not shown on this map.)*
