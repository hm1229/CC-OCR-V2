14Ke ntse ke itumetse mo tsotlhe tsa ditshupo tsa gago mo go kanang ka fa ke
itumelela dikhumo tsotlhe.
15Ke tlaa tlhathlanya dikaelo tsa gago, ke tlaa tsaya tsotlhe tsa gago tsia.
16Ke tlaa ijesa monate mo ditaong tsa gago; ga nketla ke lebala lefoko la gago.

Boitumelo mo molaong wa Modimo
Gimel

17Direla motlhanka wa gago sentle ka pelo e e feteleletseng, gore ke tle ke tshele;
jalo ke tlaa tsaya lefoko la gago tsia.
18Budulola matlho a me, gore ke bone dilo tse di gakgamatsang tsa molao wa
gago.
19Ke mojaki fela mo lefatsheng, se ntshubele ditaolo tsa gago.
20Mowa wa me o phatlolwa ke go tlhologelelwa dikatlhololo tsa gago ka metlha
yotlhe.
21O kgalemetse bapelompe, ba ba hutsegileng, ba ba timelwang ke ditaolo tsa
gago.
22Tlosa kgobo le lonyatso mo go nna; gonne ke bolokile ditshupo tsa gago.
23Dikgosana le tsone tsa dula, tsa nna tsa ntsheba; mme motlhanka wa gago a
nna a tlhathlanya ditao tsa gago fela.
24Ditshupo tsa gago le tsone di njesa monate; ke digakolodi tsa me.

Maitlamo a go obamela molao wa Modimo
Daleth

25Mowa wa me o ngaparetse lorole; ntsitsibosa ka fa lefokong la gago.
26Ke ne ka kakanya ditsela tsa me, mme wa nkaraba; nthuta ditao tsa gago.
27Ntlhaloganyetsa tsela ya dikaelo tsa gago; jalo ke tlaa tlhathlanya ditiro tsa
gago tse di gakgamatsang.
28Ke nyera mowa ka go imelesega; nthatafatse ka fa lefokong la gago.
29Tlosa tsela ya maaka mo go nna; o nneye molao wa gago ka pelotshweu.
30Ke itshenketse tsela ya boikanyo; dikatlhololo tsa gago ke di tlhomile fa pele ga
me.
31Ke ngaparetse ditshupo tsa gago; Jehofa, o se ka wa ntlhabisa ditlhong.
32Ke tlaa taboga mo tseleng ya ditaolo tsa gago, mogang o katolosang pelo ya me.
