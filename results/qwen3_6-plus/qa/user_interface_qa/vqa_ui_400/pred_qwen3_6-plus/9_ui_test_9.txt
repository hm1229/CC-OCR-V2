According to the infographic, under **“The Gift Valuation Gap”**:

- **Gift Price (actual value)** of the Diamond Ring: **$1,000**
- **Value to Recipient**: **$820**

To find how much less the recipient perceives its value:

> $1,000 - $820 = **$180**

---

✅ **Answer: $180**

This represents an 18% devaluation by the recipient, which aligns with the introductory statement that behavioral studies show individuals place up to 18% lower value on gifts than their purchase price.
