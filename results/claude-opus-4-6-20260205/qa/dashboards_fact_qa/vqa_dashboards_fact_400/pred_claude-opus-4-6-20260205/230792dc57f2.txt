## Dog-Friendly Short Cycling Routes in Ireland

Based on the infographic, the following short cycling routes are **dog friendly** (marked "YES" for Dog Friendly):

1. **Great Western Greenway Cycling Route, Wild Atlantic Way, Co. Mayo**
2. **Inishmore Entire Island Cycle, Aran Islands, Co. Galway**
3. **Loop Head Heritage Trail, Kilkee, Co. Clare**

The **Gap of Dunloe & Black Valley Cycle from Killarney, Co. Kerry** is the short route that is **NOT** dog friendly (marked "NO").
