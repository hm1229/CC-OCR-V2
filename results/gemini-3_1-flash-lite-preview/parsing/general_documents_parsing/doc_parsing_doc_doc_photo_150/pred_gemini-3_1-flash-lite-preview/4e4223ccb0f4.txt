\section*{NCERT Solutions for class 10 Maths}
\section*{Chapter 1 - Real Number Ex-1.1}

\noindent 1. Express each number as a product of its prime factors:

\noindent (i) 140 \\
Prime factors of 140 = $2, 2, 5, 7$ \\
\hspace*{2.5cm} = $2^2 \times 5 \times 7$

\noindent (ii) 156 \\
Prime factors of 156 = $2 \times 2 \times 3 \times 13$ \\
\hspace*{2.5cm} = $2^2 \times 3 \times 13$

\noindent (iii) 3825 \\
Prime factors of 3825 = $3 \times 3 \times 5 \times 5 \times 17$ \\
\hspace*{2.5cm} = $3^2 \times 5^2 \times 17$

\noindent (iv) 5005 \\
Prime factors of 5005 = $5 \times 7 \times 11 \times 13$

\noindent (v) 7429 \\
Prime factors of 7429 = $17 \times 19 \times 23$

\hrulefill

\noindent 2. Find the LCM and HCF of the following pairs of integers and verify that LCM $\times$ HCF = product of the two numbers

\noindent (i) 26 and 91 \\
Prime factors of 26 = $2 \times 13$ \\
Prime factors of 91 = $7 \times 13$ \\
HCF of 26 and 91 = $13$ \\
LCM of 26 and 91 = $2 \times 7 \times 13$ \\
\hspace*{2.5cm} = $182$

\noindent Product of two numbers = $26 \times 91 = 2366$ \\
LCM $\times$ HCF = $182 \times 13 = 2366$ \\
So, Product of two numbers = LCM $\times$ HCF
