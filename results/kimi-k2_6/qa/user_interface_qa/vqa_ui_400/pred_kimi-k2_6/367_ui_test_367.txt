I need to find information about smartphone users and Instagram usage in 2016.

Looking at the infographic, in the "USER BASE" section, there's a statistic that says:
"1/3 OF ALL MOBILE PHONE USERS will access Instagram AT LEAST ONCE A MONTH IN 2016"

So 1/3 of all mobile phone users will access Instagram at least once a month in 2016.

To find the percentage that are NOT using Instagram about once a month:
- 1/3 are using it at least once a month
- 1 - 1/3 = 2/3 are not using it at least once a month

Converting to percentage: 2/3 = 66.67%

Or more precisely: 100% - 33.33% = **66.67%** or approximately **66.7%**

If we want a cleaner number, we can say **66.66%** or simply **66⅔%**, or rounded to **67%**.

However, if we're keeping it exact: 1/3 = 33.33...%, so the answer is **66.67%** (or 66.66...%, which is 2/3 as a percentage).
