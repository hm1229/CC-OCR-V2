## Men in Top Leadership Positions at Large Nonprofits

According to the infographic, men dominate top leadership positions:

- **71%** of respondents said their organization's **CEO is male**
- **69%** of respondents said their organization's **board was "predominantly male"**
- **44%** of respondents said their organization **favors males** over equally qualified females for top positions

This is in stark contrast to the fact that **66%** of nonprofit staff positions are predominantly held by women, highlighting a significant **leadership gender gap**.
