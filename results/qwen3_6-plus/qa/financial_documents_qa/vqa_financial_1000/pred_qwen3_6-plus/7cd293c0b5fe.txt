The book *A History of the Southern Medical Association* is by **C. P. Loranz**.

As noted on the title page, C. P. Loranz was an officer of the Southern Medical Association and/or the Southern Medical Journal for forty-four of the fifty years covered in the history (1906–1956), mostly in an executive capacity.

The book was published by the **Southern Medical Association**, located at 2601 Highland Avenue, Birmingham 5, Alabama, and printed in September 1960 by the Birmingham Printing Company.

---

**Answer: C. P. Loranz**
