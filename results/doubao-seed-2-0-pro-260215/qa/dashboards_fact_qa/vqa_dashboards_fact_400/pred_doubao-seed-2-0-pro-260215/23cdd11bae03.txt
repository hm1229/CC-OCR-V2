To calculate this, we get the values from the *Average annual energy expenditures per household in the U.S.* section:
1.  Water heater = **$280**
2.  Refrigerator = **$153**
3.  Lighting (included in *OTHER (LIGHTING AND ELECTRICITY)* category) = **$827**
