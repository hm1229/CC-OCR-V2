# The Digital Property Boom - Key Findings

## Where Majority of Home Buyers Use Their Mobile Devices

According to the infographic, **at home (77%)** is where the majority of new home shoppers use their mobile devices for house buying.

The full breakdown of locations where new home shoppers use their mobile devices is:

1. **At Home** - 77%
2. **At Work** - 31%
3. **Waiting in Line** - 28%
4. **At a Restaurant** - 27%
5. **Other People's Homes** - 26%

So the vast majority of mobile home shopping activity occurs at home, more than double any other location.
