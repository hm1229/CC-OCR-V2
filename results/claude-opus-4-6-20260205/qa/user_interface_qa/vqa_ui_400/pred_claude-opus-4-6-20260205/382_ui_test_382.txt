## Shelf Life Comparison

**Dry dog food** has a longer shelf life than canned dog food when **unopened**:

- **Dry dog food**: **18-24 months** from date packaged
- **Canned dog food**: **12-15 months** from date packaged

However, once **opened**, the situation reverses dramatically:

- **Canned dog food**: keeps for **90 days** (stored in a cool, dry place)
- **Dry dog food**: keeps for only **3 days** (refrigerated)

So while dry dog food lasts longer unopened, **canned dog food lasts significantly longer once opened**.
