To determine the minimum number of host processors that can be connected to Microsoft Hyper - V, we refer to the "VMWARE VSPHERE VS. MICROSOFT HYPER - V" section of the infographic. Locate the row for "Max host processors" and then find the column for "Microsoft Hyper - V". The value in that cell is 8.
