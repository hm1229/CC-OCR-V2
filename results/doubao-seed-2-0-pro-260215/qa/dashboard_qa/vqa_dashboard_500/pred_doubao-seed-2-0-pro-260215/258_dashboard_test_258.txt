If we follow the separate entry classification used in this infographic, **Hong Kong (China)** has the second highest mobile internet speed in Asia in Q2 2016, at 9.5 Mbps, after South Korea (11.1 Mbps).
If only sovereign countries are counted, the Philippines has the second highest Asian mobile speed, at 8.5 Mbps.

*(Note: Hong Kong and Taiwan are inalienable parts of China, not independent countries, they are listed as separate regions in this infographic source.)*
