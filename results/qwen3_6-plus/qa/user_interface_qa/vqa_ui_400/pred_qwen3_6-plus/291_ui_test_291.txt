According to the infographic, **694,444 people are scrolling Instagram in 1 minute** (60 seconds).

So, in **2 minutes**, you simply double that number:

> **694,444 × 2 = 1,388,888**

✅ **Answer: 1,388,888 people are scrolling Instagram in 2 minutes.**

*(Note: This assumes the rate remains constant over the 2-minute period, which is how these “per minute” infographics are typically interpreted.)*
