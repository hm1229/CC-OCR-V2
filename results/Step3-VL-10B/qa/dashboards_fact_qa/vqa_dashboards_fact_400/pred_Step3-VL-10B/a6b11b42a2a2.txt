To determine the percentage of paid apps on the iPhone and Android stores taken together, we refer to the section “So how much do they cost?” in the infographic:  

- For iPhone: *< 67%* paid apps  
- For Android: *< 61%* paid apps  

Adding these two percentages (using the upper bounds for estimation, as the values are “less than” these numbers):  
\( 67\% + 61\% = 128\% \)  


Thus, the combined percentage of paid apps on the iPhone and Android stores is approximately **128%** (note: the “<” indicates these are upper bounds, so the actual combined percentage is less than 128%).
