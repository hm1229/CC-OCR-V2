## Things Needed for Experiment 7: Making Fake Snow

Based on the infographic, the materials needed for the **Making Fake Snow Experiment** are:

1. **Salt**
2. **Water**
3. **A Bowl**

The process involves:
- Putting some salt into the bowl
- Pouring a tiny bit of water into the bowl and mixing them together
- Fake snow will be ready **within minutes**! ❄️
