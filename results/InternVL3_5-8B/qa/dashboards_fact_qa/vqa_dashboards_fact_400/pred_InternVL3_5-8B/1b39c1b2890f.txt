The mindset of a business analyst in the data science industry should be that of a "Resilient project juggler." This role involves improving business processes and bridging the gap between business stakeholders and data scientists. A business analyst needs to be adept at managing multiple projects simultaneously, ensuring that they align with business objectives and deliver tangible results. They should possess strong interpersonal communication skills, the ability to understand and articulate business needs, and the resilience to navigate complex project landscapes effectively.
