To find the **percentage of free apps in the iPhone and Android stores, taken together**, we need to:

1. Look at the “Heavy Hitters” section of the infographic.
2. For each store, identify the percentage of **free apps** (not paid).
3. Add those percentages together.

---

From the infographic:

- **Apple App Store for iPhone**:  
  - Paid apps: 29% → So **Free apps = 100% - 29% = 71%**

- **Google Android Market**:  
  - Paid apps: 41% → So **Free apps = 100% - 41% = 59%**

*(Note: The infographic shows “Paid versus Free Apps in Store” with two pie charts per store — one labeled “Paid” and one “Free”. For iPhone, it says 29% paid → 71% free. For Android, 41% paid → 59% free.)*

---

Now, add them:

> **71% (iPhone free) + 59% (Android free) = 130%**

---

✅ **Final Answer: 130%**

*(This is the sum of the free app percentages from both stores — not an average or combined proportion, just as asked: “taken together”.)*
