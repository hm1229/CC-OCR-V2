## Third Highest Internet Users in Europe

According to the infographic, looking at the connected Europeans by country:

1. **Russia** - 68M / 13.1%
2. **Germany** - 67.5M / 13%
3. **UK** - 52.7M / 10.1%

The **UK** has the third highest number of internet users with **52.7 million** connected users, representing **10.1%** of connected Europeans.
