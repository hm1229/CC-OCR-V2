To calculate how much the **value of exports** is more than the **value of imports** in Australia-China trade in 2013:

From the infographic:

- **Total Exports (Australia to China)** = **$101.54 billion**
- **Total Imports (Australia from China)** = **$49.33 billion**

Now, subtract imports from exports:

> **$101.54b - $49.33b = $52.21 billion**

---

✅ **Answer: $52.21 billion**

This means Australia’s exports to China exceeded its imports from China by **$52.21 billion** in 2013.
