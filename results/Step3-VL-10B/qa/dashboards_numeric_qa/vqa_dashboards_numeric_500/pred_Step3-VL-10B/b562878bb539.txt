To determine the increase in the percentage of DNB Outbreak from 2007 to 2013 (note: the infographic shows data for 2007 and 2013, not 2016—likely a reference error in the question):  

- In 2007, the percentage of Scots pine sites within 1km of a DNB outbreak was 9%.  
- In 2013, this percentage rose to 61%.  

The increase is calculated as \( 61\% - 9\% = 52\% \).  

If the question intended 2013 (the year shown in the infographic) instead of 2016, the increase is 52 percentage points.
