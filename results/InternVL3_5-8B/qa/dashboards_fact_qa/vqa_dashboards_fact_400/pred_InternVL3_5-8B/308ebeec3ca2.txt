According to the infographic, 21% of participants accessed learning materials on their tablets. Additionally, 17% installed the app on both tablets and smartphones. To find the percentage of people who used only tablets, we need to subtract those who used both devices from the total percentage of tablet users:

\[ 21\% \text{ (tablet users)} - 17\% \text{ (users of both tablets and smartphones)} = 4\% \]

So, 4% of participants used only apps on their tablets.
