## Biggest Challenge in Technology Disruption

According to the infographic, **Job Losses Due to Outsourcing or Automation** is the biggest perceived downside of technology disruption, tied with **Greater Price Transparency, Lower Profit Margins** — both at **25%** each.

However, looking at the broader context of the infographic, **Security & Privacy** is highlighted as **the biggest concern for serious vulnerabilities** in the Internet of Things space. The infographic emphasizes that IoT devices are built quickly to get first to market and are made for convenience rather than security, with the top 10 IoT devices having multiple security flaws including:

- Insecure software & firmware
- Insecure web interfaces
- Lack of transport encryption
- Insufficient authentication
- Raised privacy concerns
- And several others

So while the perceived downsides of technology disruption are split among job losses (25%), price transparency (25%), threats to data/physical security (25%), inability of IT systems to keep up (25%), and others — **security and privacy** stands out as the overarching and most critical challenge.
