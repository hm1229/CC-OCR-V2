To determine the percentage of women over the age of 50 with a cancer incidence, we analyze the "Incidence by Age" section:  

- The infographic states that **only 18% of cancers in women occur before age 50**.  
- Since the total percentage of all age groups is 100%, the percentage of women *over* age 50 is \( 100\% - 18\% = 82\% \).  

We can also verify by summing the age - specific percentages for groups over 50:  
- 50 to 64 yrs: 27%  
- 65 to 74 yrs: 25%  
- Over 74 yrs: 30%  
- Total: \( 27\% + 25\% + 30\% = 82\% \)  


Thus, 82% of women over the age of 50 have a cancer incidence.
