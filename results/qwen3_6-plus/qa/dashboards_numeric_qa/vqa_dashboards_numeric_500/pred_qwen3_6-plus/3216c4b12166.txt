To calculate the percentage reduction in kids walking or cycling to school from 1969 to 2001:

- In **1969**, **42%** of kids biked/walked to school.
- In **2001**, that dropped to **16%**.

### Step 1: Find the absolute decrease
42% - 16% = **26 percentage points**

### Step 2: Calculate percentage reduction relative to the original (1969) value
> Percentage Reduction = (Decrease / Original Value) × 100  
> = (26 / 42) × 100 ≈ **61.9%**

---

✅ **Final Answer: Approximately 61.9%**
