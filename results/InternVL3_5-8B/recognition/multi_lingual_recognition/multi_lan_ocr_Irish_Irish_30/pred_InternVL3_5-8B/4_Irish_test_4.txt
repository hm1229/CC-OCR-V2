Le Biddy Jenkinson

Cóilín an Scéil

This is an unusual story about a vet. She is called one day to look at a prize cock, called Oisín, who has won many competitions and who usually likes to mate with all the hens. However, Oisín has not been in form lately and has no energy. Oisín is owned by Sally who is very proud of him and boasts about him often.

The locals laugh at her and have nicknamed her husband Coillín – the ‘chicken’ – because of the way she has been making him drowsy. The vet examines the hen and finds that she has been blocked up and Oisín has been given seeds that would make him drowsy. The vet suspects that Sally’s husbandband has tried to harm Oisín because he is sick and tired of all the jeering.
