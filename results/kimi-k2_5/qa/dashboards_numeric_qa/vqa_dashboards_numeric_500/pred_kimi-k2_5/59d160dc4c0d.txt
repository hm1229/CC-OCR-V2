Based on the infographic, the candidate for president is chosen at the **National Party Conventions**.

According to the section "HOW DO THE CANDIDATES WIN?", the process works as follows:
- The electorate votes for presidential nominees at **Caucuses and State Primaries**
- Then **party delegates** from each state go to the **National Party Conventions** to officially choose a party candidate for president

So the final selection of the party's presidential candidate happens at the National Party Conventions.
