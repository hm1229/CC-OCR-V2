Looking at the legend in the chart:

- **Blue** = Continuing to trade at full capacity  
- **Yellow** = Continuing to trade partially  
- **Orange** = Temporarily closed or paused trading  
- **Grey** = Permanently ceased trading  

✅ Therefore, **“permanently ceased trading” is depicted in grey**.

This is also visually consistent in the stacked bars — the grey segment appears only at the far right end of each bar, representing the smallest portion (as expected for permanent closures), and is labeled accordingly in the legend.

---

**Answer: Grey**
