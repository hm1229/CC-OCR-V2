## Developmental Benefits of Early Childhood Education

According to the infographic, there are three categories of developmental benefits:

### 1. Social and Emotional
- Students are surrounded by adults and peers, and can **develop relationships**
- The constant presence of these people offers a **sense of security**
- A feeling of security leads to **greater participation and cooperation**

### 2. Cognitive
- A developmental curriculum offers **age-appropriate learning**
- Students build on existing skills, **increasing their knowledge**
- Teachers help ensure students reach **important developmental milestones**

### 3. Language
- Between ages 1 and 5, **language develops quickly**
- Routine interactions with teachers and peers help **develop language skills**
- **More advanced skills emerge** as conversations and vocabulary progress
