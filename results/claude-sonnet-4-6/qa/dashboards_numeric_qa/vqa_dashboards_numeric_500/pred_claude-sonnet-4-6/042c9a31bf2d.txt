## Long-Term Benefits of Early Childhood Education

According to the infographic, the long-term benefits fall into two main categories:

### 🎓 Student Success
- Students are **29% more likely** to graduate from high school
- Students are **44% less likely** to repeat a grade
- Students **pass standardized tests more frequently**

### 👔 Adult Responsibility
- Adults age 40 are **29% more likely to be employed**
- Those same 40-year-olds have a **33% higher average income**
- 18-year-olds **without** early childhood education are **70% more likely** to be arrested for a violent crime

These benefits highlight how early childhood education creates a positive ripple effect that extends well into adulthood, impacting **academic achievement, financial stability, and law-abiding behavior**.
