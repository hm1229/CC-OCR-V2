According to the infographic, **1/3 of all mobile phone users** will access Instagram at least once a month in 2016.

This means that **approximately 66.7%** of all smartphone users are **not** using Instagram at least once a month in 2016.

### Calculation:
- If 1/3 use Instagram at least once a month, then:
- 1 - (1/3) = 2/3 = **66.7%** do not use it at least once a month.

✅ **Answer: 66.7%**
