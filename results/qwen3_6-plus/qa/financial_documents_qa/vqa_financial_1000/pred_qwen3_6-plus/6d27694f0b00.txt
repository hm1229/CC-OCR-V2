For **Barnyard Grass** in the **N.C. COLORADO DISTRICT**, the recommended control system includes:

> **Roneet preplant**

> **Option**:  
> Post with Betanal plus Betanal 475 with surfactant** when grass has 2 leaves or smaller

---

### Notes:
- The double asterisk (**) refers to:  
  **CITOWETT PLUS - 1 oz. to 10 gal. mix**

This means the optional post-emergence treatment should be applied when the barnyard grass is at the 2-leaf stage or smaller, using Betanal + Betanal 475 + CITOWETT PLUS as the surfactant.

✅ So, the full answer is:

> **Roneet preplant**  
> *Option*: Post with Betanal plus Betanal 475 with surfactant** (CITOWETT PLUS) when grass has 2 leaves or smaller.
