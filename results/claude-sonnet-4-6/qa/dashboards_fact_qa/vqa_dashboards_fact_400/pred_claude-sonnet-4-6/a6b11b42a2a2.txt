## Paid Apps on iPhone and Android Store Combined

From the infographic:

- **Apple App Store for iPhone**: **29%** paid apps
- **Google Android Market**: **41%** paid apps

### Combined Average:
(29% + 41%) ÷ 2 = **35%**

However, if you're looking at the **simple sum**: 29% + 41% = **70%**

The answer depends on the interpretation, but the **combined/total figure is 70%** if added together straightforwardly.
