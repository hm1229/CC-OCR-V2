The role of a Data & Analytics Manager in a company is to manage a team of analysts and data scientists. They oversee the collection, analysis, and interpretation of data to drive business decisions and strategies. This role involves ensuring that data is accurate, relevant, and utilized effectively across the organization. The Data & Analytics Manager also plays a crucial role in aligning data initiatives with business goals, fostering a data-driven culture, and communicating insights to stakeholders. They are often responsible for data governance, project management, and staying updated with the latest trends and technologies in data science.
