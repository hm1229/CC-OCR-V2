Based on the infographic, **malware attacks decreased** from 2010 to 2013.

- **2010**: 35% of email attacks were malware
- **2013**: 2% of email attacks were malware

That’s a significant drop — from 35% down to just 2%.

The infographic also notes under “EMAIL ATTACKS MOSTLY DOWN” that while overall email attacks decreased, the *types* of attacks shifted — with phishing and malicious files becoming more prominent relative to malware.
