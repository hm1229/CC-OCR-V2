I need to find which food crisis stage has ">30% of the population is acutely malnourished."

Looking at the infographic from top to bottom:

- **Food Security**: <5% acutely malnourished
- **Food Insecurity**: 5-10% acutely malnourished
- **Acute Food and Livelihood Crisis**: 10-15% acutely malnourished
- **Humanitarian Emergency**: 15-30% acutely malnourished
- **Famine**: >30% acutely malnourished

The answer is **Famine**. During the Famine stage, more than 30% of the population is acutely malnourished. This is the most severe stage, where people face complete lack of access to food and experience mass starvation, death, and destitution.
