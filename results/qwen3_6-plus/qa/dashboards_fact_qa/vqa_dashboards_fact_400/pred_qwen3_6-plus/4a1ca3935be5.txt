- Later, when the Lancastrians reformed and crossed the stream again, they were hit hard — and **Baron Audley (a Lancastrian commander) was killed**.
- Even after reforming with 4,000 more troops, **500 Lancastrians deserted** to the Yorkists — indicating morale collapse.
