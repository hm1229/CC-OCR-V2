The emergency benefits provided to working Canadians include:

1. **Emergency Care Benefit**: This is for individuals who must stay home due to COVID-19 and don't have paid sick leave. It may include those who can't access EI (Employment Insurance) leave, are caring for someone who is sick, or are taking care of a child or someone who is sick.

2. **Emergency Support Benefit**: This benefit provides up to $5 billion in support to workers who are not eligible for EI and who are facing unemployment and reduced hours.
