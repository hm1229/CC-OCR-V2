## Instagram is the #2 Most-Used Social Network

According to this infographic, **Instagram** is the answer. The infographic specifically states that:

> *"Instagram is the **#2 most-used social network** among teens ages 13-17 since 2015"*

### Supporting Stats:
- 📱 **Over 500 million** active users
- 📈 User base grew by **over 300%** in just two years
- 👥 Usage by online adults has **doubled since 2012**

This makes Instagram the most popular social media platform **after Facebook**, surpassing Twitter, YouTube, and WhatsApp in terms of active engagement and growth metrics highlighted in this infographic.
