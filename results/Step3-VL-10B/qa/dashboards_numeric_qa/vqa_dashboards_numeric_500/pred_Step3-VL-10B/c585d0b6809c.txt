To determine the percentage of the world's executives who are not women, we first find the percentage of women executives. From the "INCLUSION & PARTICIPATION" section, it states that women make up 8% of the world’s executives.  

To find the percentage of non - women executives, we subtract the percentage of women executives from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 8\% = 92\% \)  


So, 92% of the world's executives are not women.
