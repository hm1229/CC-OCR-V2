The three inventions introduced at the 1904 St. Louis World's Fair, as shown in the infographic, are:

1. **The Ice Cream Cone** – A waffle rolled into a conical shape, first used as an edible container for ice cream.
2. **Electricity** – The Palace of Electricity showcased the development of electricity for the masses, with Thomas Edison presenting it to the public.
3. **The Hot Dog** – Created by Bavarian sausage seller Anton Feuchtwanger, who asked his brother-in-law to create a long bun to hold a sausage.
