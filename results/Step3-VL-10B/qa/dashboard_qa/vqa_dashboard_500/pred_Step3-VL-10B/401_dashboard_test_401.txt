To determine the percentage of millennials in the U.S. who do not engage in social media activities daily, we first find the percentage that **does** engage daily. In the "SOCIAL MEDIA AND MOBILE ENGAGEMENT" section, it states: *"71% engage in social media daily"*.  

To find the percentage that does **not** engage daily, subtract this value from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 71\% = 29\% \)  


Thus, 29% of U.S. millennials do not engage in social media activities daily.
