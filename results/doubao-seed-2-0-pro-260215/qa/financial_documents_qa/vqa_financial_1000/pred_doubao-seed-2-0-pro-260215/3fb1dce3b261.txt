This is a **Production & Process Controls Audit Agenda for DePuy Orthopaedics Inc. (Warsaw, IN), for an audit scheduled 13-17 July 2009** that defines the objective and scope of this quality management system audit:
1.  **Audit Objective**: Assess compliance of selected Quality Management System areas to *21CFR820 (FDA medical device quality system regulation)* and *ISO 13485:2003 (medical device quality management system standard)*.
2.  **Audit Scope**:
    - It evaluates if the Production and Process Controls quality management subsystem is effectively implemented
    - It reviews the effectiveness of corrective actions identified in prior internal audits, where appropriate
    - It is *not a full quality system assessment*: other quality management system elements are out of scope unless identified during the above review, and the company may also flag specific in-scope areas to focus on as needed.
