From the infographic:

- In **2010**, spam made up **89%** of email attacks.
- In **2013**, spam made up **69%** of email attacks.

To calculate the **percentage decrease**:

> Decrease = 89% - 69% = **20 percentage points**

But if you’re asking for the **percent decrease relative to the 2010 value**:

> Percent Decrease = [(89 - 69) / 89] × 100 ≈ **(20 / 89) × 100 ≈ 22.47%**

---

✅ So, **spam mail decreased by approximately 22.47%** from 2010 to 2013 (relative to its 2010 level).

*(Note: The infographic shows a drop from 89% to 69%, which is a 20-percentage-point drop — but the relative percent decrease is ~22.5%.)*
