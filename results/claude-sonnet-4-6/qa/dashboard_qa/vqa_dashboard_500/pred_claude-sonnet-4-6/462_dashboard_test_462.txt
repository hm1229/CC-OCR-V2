## Generations Trying Out New Online Retailers

According to the infographic, shoppers from the following generations are trying out new online retailers:

| Generation | Percentage (Yes) |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Gen Z** | 32% |
| **Millennials** | 36% |

### Those Who Did **Not** Try New Online Retailers:
- **Baby Boomers**: 43%
- **Silent Generation**: 48%

So **Gen Z and Millennials** were the most likely to try new online retailers during the lockdown, while **Baby Boomers and the Silent Generation** largely did not adopt new online retailers.
