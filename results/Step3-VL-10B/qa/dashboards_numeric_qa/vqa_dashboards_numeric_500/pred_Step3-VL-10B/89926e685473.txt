To determine how many bottles out of 5 are not recycled, we analyze the information:  

- The infographic states, *“51 billion plastic bottles are used every year, only 1 in 5 are recycled”*.  
- If 1 out of 5 is recycled, then the number of bottles **not** recycled is \( 5 - 1 = 4 \).  


Thus, out of 5 bottles, 4 are not recycled.
