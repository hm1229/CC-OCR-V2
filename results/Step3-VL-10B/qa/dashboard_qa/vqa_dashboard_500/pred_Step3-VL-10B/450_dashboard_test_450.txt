To determine the percentage of Twitter users who do not buy from a brand they follow, we start with the information given: "37% of Twitter users will buy from a brand they follow!"  

Since the total percentage of users is 100%, we subtract the percentage of users who **do** buy from those they follow:  

\( 100\% - 37\% = 63\% \)  


Thus, 63% of Twitter users do not buy from a brand they follow.
