The majority of millennials in the U.S. choose online viewing for several reasons:

1. **Immediate Access**: Online platforms provide instant access to a vast array of content, allowing millennials to watch their favorite shows and movies at any time.

2. **Convenience of Viewing Location**: Millennials appreciate the flexibility of watching content from various locations, whether at home, in the office, or on the go.

3. **Multitasking**: They often enjoy engaging in multiple activities simultaneously, such as watching videos while working or socializing.

4. **Multichannel Options**: Online viewing offers a diverse range of content across different genres and interests, catering to varied preferences.

5. **Engagement with Social Media**: Millennials are highly active on social media, where they discover and share content, further driving their preference for online viewing.

6. **Cost-Effectiveness**: Many millennials opt for free streaming services or subscription models that offer affordable access to extensive content libraries.

These factors collectively contribute to the preference for online viewing among millennials.
