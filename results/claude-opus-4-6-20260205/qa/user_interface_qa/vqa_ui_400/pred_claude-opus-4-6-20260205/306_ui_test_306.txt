## Time-to-Payment for Apps Through App Store

According to the infographic, the **Time-to-payment** for apps through the **App Store** is **38 days**.

This can be seen in the "Time-to-market for apps" section, where the App Store shows:
- **Time-to-market**: 22 days
- **Time-to-payment**: 38 days

This compares to Direct Download (73 days) and Via Customer (116 days) for time-to-payment, making the App Store the fastest channel for receiving payment.
