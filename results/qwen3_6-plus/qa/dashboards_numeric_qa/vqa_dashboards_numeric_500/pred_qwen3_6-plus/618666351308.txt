To find the percentage of Malaysian online shoppers who **do not** shop online on a **daily basis**, we can subtract the percentage of those who shop **daily** from 100%.

From the chart:

- **Daily** shoppers = **0.4%**

Therefore:

> **100% - 0.4% = 99.6%**

---

✅ **Answer: 99.6%**

This means that **99.6%** of online shoppers in Malaysia (as of February 2017) do **not** shop online daily.

*(Note: The survey is based on online shoppers — not the entire Malaysian population — so this percentage applies to the group of people who already shop online.)*
