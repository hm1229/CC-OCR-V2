To determine how many years after McDonald's Microsoft was founded, follow these steps:  

1. **Find McDonald’s founding year**: In the infographic, under “McDonald’s”, it states: *“Founded in 1940, starting out as a barbeque restaurant…”*  
2. **Find Microsoft’s founding year**: Under “Microsoft”, it states: *“Founded in 1975”* (with Paul Allen inventing the company name that year).  
3. **Calculate the difference**: Subtract McDonald’s founding year from Microsoft’s: \( 1975 - 1940 = 35 \).
