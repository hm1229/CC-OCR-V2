\section*{Test Your Reading}

\subsection*{1. Complete the following statements:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(a)] They began \underline{heating} the iron and forging it again.
    \item[(b)] The farmer \underline{led} the lord to the storeroom.
    \item[(c)] Off rode the \underline{farmers} and the lord to get what he had earned.
    \item[(d)] The lord \underline{heated} it well and threw it in a tub of water.
    \item[(e)] The lord \underline{did not} want the coachman to know the shame.
\end{enumerate}
