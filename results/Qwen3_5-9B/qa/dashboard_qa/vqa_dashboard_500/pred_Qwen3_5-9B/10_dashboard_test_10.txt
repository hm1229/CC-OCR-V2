According to the infographic, under the section **“Scotland’s climate change plans”**, it states:

> “However, of the **109 ‘on track’ actions**, **103 do not have fixed timescales for delivery**.”

This implies that the remaining actions — those that *do* have fixed timescales — are:

**109 – 103 = 6**

✅ So, **6** of the 109 ‘on track’ actions have fixed timescales for delivery.
