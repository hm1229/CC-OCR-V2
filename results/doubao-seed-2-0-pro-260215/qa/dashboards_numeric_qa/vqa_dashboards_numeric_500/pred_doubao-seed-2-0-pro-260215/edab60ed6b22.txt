From this Halloween related infographic, the relevant characteristics of Halloween pumpkins are:
1. **Production and supply characteristics**:
    - The US produces *900 million pounds of pumpkins* specifically for Halloween, this amount is enough to make about 60 million pumpkin pies.
    - 80% of the total pumpkin supply in the US is available in October, the month when Halloween falls.
2. **Related use/custom characteristics**:
    - Carving pumpkins is one of the most common Halloween celebration activities: 48% of people celebrating Halloween will carve pumpkins.
    - Pumpkin is the most popular theme for pet Halloween costumes: more pets dress up as pumpkins than any other costume type for Halloween.
