To determine the percentage reduction in kids walking or cycling to school from 1969 to 2001, we look at the "Percentage of Kids Bike/Walk to School" section:  

- In 1969, 42% of kids bike/walk to school.  
- In 2001, 16% of kids bike/walk to school.  

Calculate the difference: \( 42\% - 16\% = 26\% \).  


Thus, the percentage of kids walking or cycling to school reduced by 26 percentage points from 1969 to 2001.
