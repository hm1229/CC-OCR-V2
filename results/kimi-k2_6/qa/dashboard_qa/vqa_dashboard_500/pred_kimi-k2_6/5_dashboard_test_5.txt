To find the percentage of Australians aged 55-59 years who use the internet according to the 2011 survey, I need to look at the "Internet usage in over 50s" section of the infographic.

In this section, there are curved bars with age groups and corresponding percentages on the right side.

Looking at the age groups and their percentages:
- 50-55 year olds: 85%
- 55-59 year olds: 90%
- 60-64 year olds: 82%
- 65-69 year olds: 76%
- 70-74 year olds: 65%
- 75+ year olds: 41%

The 55-59 year olds correspond to **90%**.
