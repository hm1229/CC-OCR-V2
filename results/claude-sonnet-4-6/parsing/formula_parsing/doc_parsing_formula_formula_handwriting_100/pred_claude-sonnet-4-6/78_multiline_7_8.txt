$$\frac{\partial^2 \lambda_k}{\partial w_{pq} \partial w_{rs}} = \sum_{j \neq k} \left[ \frac{\partial w_{kj}}{\partial w_{pq}} \frac{\partial \tau^k_{,j}}{\partial w_{rs}} + \frac{\partial w_{kj}}{\partial w_{rs}} \frac{\partial \tau^k_{,j}}{\partial w_{pq}} \right]$$

$$= \begin{cases} \dfrac{1}{\lambda_k - \lambda_q}, & p = k,\ q \neq k,\ r = q,\ s = k; \\[10pt] \dfrac{1}{\lambda_k - \lambda_s}, & r = k,\ s \neq k,\ p = s,\ q = k; \\[10pt] 0, & \text{otherwise.} \end{cases}$$
