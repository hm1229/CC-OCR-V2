According to the **Foodbank Hunger Report 2017** infographic, under the section **“How Many People Are Receiving Food Assistance?”**, it states:

> “This is because they feel:  
> - 42% Others need help more than they do  
> - **36% Embarrassed**  
> - 33% Ashamed”

---

✅ Therefore, **36%** of food insecure Australians feel **embarrassed** about seeking assistance from a charity.

*(Note: The report says “less than half (46%) have sought assistance from a charity,” and then lists reasons why others haven’t — including embarrassment. So the 36% refers to those who are food insecure but have not sought help due to feeling embarrassed.)*

---

**Final Answer: 36%**
