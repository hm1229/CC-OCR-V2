## Most Demanded Job in Australia

According to the **"High Demand Jobs"** section of the infographic, **Hospitality, retail and service managers** is the most demanded job in Australia, showing the longest positive bar extending to approximately **3000** on the chart.

This is followed by:
1. **Advertising and sales managers**
2. **ICT managers**
3. **Accountants, auditors and company secretaries**

> *Note: Negative values in the chart indicate a labor market surplus (more job seekers than available jobs), while positive values indicate high demand.*
