Based on the infographic provided, **first-year college students taking remedial courses pay on average an extra $3,000 for remedial coursework**.

This information is found in the red box located in the lower right section of the image, which states:

> **1 IN 4 COLLEGE FIRST-YEARS**

Additionally, the infographic notes that **first-year remedial college students and families spent $1.5 billion total** on tuition and living expenses (including $380 million in loans) for content and skills they should have learned in high school — but this is a *total aggregate cost*, not the *average per student*.

The **$3,000 figure** is explicitly labeled as the **average extra amount paid per student** for remedial coursework.

✅ So, the answer is: **$3,000**
