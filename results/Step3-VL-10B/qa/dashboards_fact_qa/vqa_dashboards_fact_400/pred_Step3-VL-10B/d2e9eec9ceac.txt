To determine the percentage of all smartphone users not using Instagram at least once a month in 2016, we start with the information from the "USER BASE" section:  

- It states that **1/3 (≈33.33%) of all mobile phone users will access Instagram at least once a month in 2016**.  

To find the percentage of users *not* using Instagram at least once a month, subtract this value from 100%:  

\( 100\% - 33.33\% = 66.67\% \)  


Thus, approximately 66.67% of all smartphone users do not use Instagram at least once a month in 2016.
