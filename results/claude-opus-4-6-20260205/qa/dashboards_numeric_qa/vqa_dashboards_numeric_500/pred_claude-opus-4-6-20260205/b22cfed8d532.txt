## What Should Be Eliminated in the Protein Category

According to the infographic, in the **Protein Group** section, you should **eliminate processed meats that contain chemicals or additives** — a distinction that is noted as **not made by MyPlate**.

The infographic recommends eating protein from a variety of sources including beans, legumes, nuts, eggs, wild-caught fish, and pasture-based dairy and meats, while specifically calling out the removal of chemically processed meats.
