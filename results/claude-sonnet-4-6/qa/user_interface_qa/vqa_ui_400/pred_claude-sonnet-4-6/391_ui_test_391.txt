## Storing Dry Dog Food Once Opened

According to the infographic, once opened, **dry dog food** should be stored at:

- **Temperature:** Below **80°F**
- **Location:** In a **cool, dry place**
- **Duration:** It keeps for up to **90 days**

This is in contrast to canned/wet dog food, which must be refrigerated at below 40°F and only keeps for **3 days** once opened.
