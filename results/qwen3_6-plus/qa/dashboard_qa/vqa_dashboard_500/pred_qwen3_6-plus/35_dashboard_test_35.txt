To find the number of reported COVID-19 cases **not from Chinese Mainland** as of February 21, 2020:

- Total reported cases in China: **75,569**
- Cases in Chinese mainland: **75,467** (which is 99% of total)

So,

> **75,569 - 75,467 = 102**

✅ Therefore, the number of reported cases **not from Chinese Mainland** was **102**.

*(Note: These likely include cases in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan — which are part of China but sometimes reported separately in epidemiological data.)*

---

**Answer: 102**
