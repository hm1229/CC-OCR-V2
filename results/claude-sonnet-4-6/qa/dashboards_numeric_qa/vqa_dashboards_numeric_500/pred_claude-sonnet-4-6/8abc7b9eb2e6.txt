## Malware Attack Trend (2010 to 2013)

Malware attacks **decreased** from **2010 to 2013**.

- **2010:** 0.35% malware attacks
- **2013:** 2% malware attacks

Wait — actually, looking at the data more carefully:

- **2010:** 0.35%
- **2013:** 2%

Malware attacks actually **increased** from **0.35% in 2010 to 2% in 2013**, making it one of the few email attack types that **grew** during this period, while spam, phishing, and malicious files all decreased.
