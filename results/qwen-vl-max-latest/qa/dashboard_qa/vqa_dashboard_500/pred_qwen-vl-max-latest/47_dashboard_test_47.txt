The mode of learning in which content is delivered 100% in oral or written formats is **Traditional**.

As shown in the infographic under "TYPES OF ONLINE EDUCATION," the Traditional mode is described as:
> "Course with no online technology used. Content is delivered in writing or orally."

This corresponds to 0% online content delivery, meaning all content is delivered through traditional (non-digital) methods such as lectures, textbooks, or written materials.
