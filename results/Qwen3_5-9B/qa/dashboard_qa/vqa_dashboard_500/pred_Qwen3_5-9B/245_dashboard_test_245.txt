The infographic states that **440,000 birds are killed by wind turbines each year** currently.

It also projects that **by 2030, that number will exceed 1 million**.

---

### To calculate the expected rise:

**Expected 2030 deaths** = >1,000,000  
**Current deaths** = 440,000

**Rise** = 1,000,000 - 440,000 = **560,000**

*(Note: Since the projection says “exceed 1 million,” the actual rise may be slightly higher than 560,000 — but based on the given figures, we use 1 million as the baseline for calculation.)*

---

✅ **Answer: The expected rise in bird deaths due to turbines by 2030 is approximately 560,000 birds per year.**
