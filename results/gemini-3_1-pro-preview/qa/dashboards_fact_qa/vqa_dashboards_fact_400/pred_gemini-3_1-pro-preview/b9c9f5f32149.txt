Based on the infographic, the two types of coronavirus tests are:

1.  **Swab test:** This test answers the question, **"Do I have it?"** It involves taking a swab sample from the throat and nose to see if you currently have the virus.
2.  **Antibody test:** This test answers the question, **"Have I had it?"** It involves a blood test (either a laboratory test or a finger-prick home test) to see if you have had the virus in the past.
