The document is an **audit agenda** for DePuy Orthopaedics, Inc. (Warsaw, IN) regarding their *Production & Process Controls* quality management system.  

Key details:  
- **Audit Objective**: Assess compliance with 21 CFR 820 and ISO 13485:2003 for selected areas of the Quality Management System.  
- **Audit Scope**: Evaluate the effectiveness of the production/process controls quality management sub - system; review prior corrective actions from internal audits; clarify that this audit is *not* a full assessment of the entire Quality System (only elements identified during the sub - system assessment are covered); and note the company can focus on specific areas of interest/concern.  

It is part of a confidential audit document for a July 13–17, 2009 audit.
