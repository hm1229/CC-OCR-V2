The memorandum was written to **ISRF Members** (International Sugar Research Foundation, Inc. Members).

This is clearly stated in the “To:” line of the memo:

> **To:** ISRF Members

The memo, dated December 11, 1975, and titled “Sugar Substitutes,” was issued by the International Sugar Research Foundation, Inc., and addressed internally to its own members to inform them about the preliminary findings of a committee reviewing the carcinogenicity of cyclamate.

---

*Note: While the memo discusses a U.S. government-sponsored committee (Temporary Committee for Review of Data on Carcinogenicity of Cyclamate), the memorandum itself is an internal communication from ISRF to its members — not to the government or the committee.*
