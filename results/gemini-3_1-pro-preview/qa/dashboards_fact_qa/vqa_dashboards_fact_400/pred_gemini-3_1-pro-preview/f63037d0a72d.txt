Based on the infographic, vegetable oil and nut oil are sources of **Vitamin E**.

You can find this by looking at the "OILS" section in the legend at the bottom right, which shows the icons for vegetable oil (a drop with a 'V') and nut oil (a drop with an 'N'). Then, locate the purple ring (corresponding to oils) in the circular chart. The only icons in that ring are located in the section for Vitamin E.
