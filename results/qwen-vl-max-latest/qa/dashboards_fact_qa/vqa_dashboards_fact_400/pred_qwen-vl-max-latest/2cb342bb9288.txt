The disease that rots the potato and destroys the plant is the **potato blight**. This is clearly indicated in the image, where it states: *"The potato blight is a disease that rots the potato & destroys the plant."* The blight was caused by the fungus-like organism *Phytophthora infestans*, which led to the catastrophic failure of potato crops in Ireland during the mid-19th century, contributing significantly to the Great Irish Famine (1845–1852).
