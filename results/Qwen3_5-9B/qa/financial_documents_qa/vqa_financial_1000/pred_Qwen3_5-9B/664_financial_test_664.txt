This form is known as a **Universal Credit Card Charge Form** — specifically, it’s a **Contractor Invoice Copy** issued by **Eastern Airlines**.

It’s used to acknowledge receipt of tickets or coupons for related charges (like flight services) and to record payment via credit card. The form includes fields for:

- Passenger name and routing
- Fare details
- Credit card information (card number, expiration date)
- Approval codes
- Date of issue

The “X” in the top-left corner likely indicates that payment is being made in full at the time of billing (as opposed to extended payment).

In short:  
> **Eastern Airlines Universal Credit Card Charge Form – Contractor Invoice Copy**

It’s essentially a receipt/invoice generated when a credit card is used to pay for airline tickets or related services.
