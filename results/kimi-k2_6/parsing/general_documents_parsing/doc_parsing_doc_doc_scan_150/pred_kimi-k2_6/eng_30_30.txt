\section*{III. CALCULATIONS:}

Calculating the volume of the given examined bodies based on the formula:
\[ V_b = a \cdot b \cdot c \]

$V_b$ - volume of examined bodies [cm$^3$], $a$ - width of examined bodies [cm], $b$ - length of examined bodies [cm], $c$ - height of examined bodies [cm].

\[ V_b = 2{,}5 \, [\text{cm}] \cdot 4{,}2 \, [\text{cm}] \cdot 1{,}5 \, [\text{cm}] = 15{,}75 \, [\text{cm}^3] \]

Calculating the material density of the given examined bodies based on the formula:
\[ \rho_b = \frac{m_b}{V_b} \]

$\rho_b$ - material density of given body [g/cm$^3$], $m_b$ - mass of given body [g], $V_b$ - volume of examined body.

\[ \rho_b (\text{ebonite}) = \frac{22{,}7}{15{,}75} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}44127 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1441{,}27 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

\[ \rho_b (\text{bakelite}) = \frac{19{,}8}{15{,}75} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}25714 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1257{,}14 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

\[ \rho_b (\text{aluminium (I)}) = \frac{42{,}6}{15{,}75} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}70476 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2704{,}76 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

Calculating the material density using aerometer based on the formula:
\[ \rho_b' = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{m_3 - m_2} \rho_w \]

$\rho_b'$ - material density of given body using aerometer [g/cm$^3$], $m_n$ - values of mass from experiment [g], $\rho_w$ - density of distilled water = 1 [g/cm$^3$].

\[ \rho_b' (\text{ebonite}) = \frac{110 - 87}{103 - 87} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{23}{16} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}4375 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1437{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

\[ \rho_b' (\text{bakelite}) = \frac{110 - 90}{105 - 90} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{20}{15} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}33333 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1333{,}33 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

\[ \rho_b' (\text{aluminium (I)}) = \frac{110 - 67}{83 - 67} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{43}{16} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}6875 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2687{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

\[ \rho_b' (\text{aluminium (II)}) = \frac{110 - 86}{94 - 86} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{24}{8} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 3 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 3000 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

Comparing calculated density values with table density values:

\[ \rho_b (\text{ebonite}) - \rho_{\text{tab}} (\text{ebonite}) = 1441{,}27 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] - 1150 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 291{,}27 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 22{,}78 \, [\%] \]

\[ \rho_b' (\text{ebonite}) - \rho_{\text{tab}} (\text{ebonite}) = 1437{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] - 1150 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 287{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 22{,}22 \, [\%] \]

\[ \rho_b (\text{bakelite}) - \rho_{\text{tab}} (\text{bakelite}) = 1257{,}14 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] - 1300 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 42{,}86 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 3{,}35 \, [\%] \]

\[ \rho_b' (\text{bakelite}) - \rho_{\text{tab}} (\text{bakelite}) = 1333{,}33 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] - 1300 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 33{,}33 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 2{,}53 \, [\%] \]

\[ \rho_b (\text{aluminium (I)}) - \rho_{\text{tab}} (\text{aluminium}) = 2704{,}76 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] - 2700 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 4{,}76 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 0{,}17 \, [\%] \]

\[ \rho_b' (\text{aluminium (I)}) - \rho_{\text{tab}} (\text{aluminium}) = 2687{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] - 2700 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 12{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 0{,}46 \, [\%] \]

\[ \rho_b' (\text{aluminium (II)}) - \rho_{\text{tab}} (\text{aluminium}) = 3000 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] - 2700 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 300 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] = 10{,}53 \, [\%] \]
