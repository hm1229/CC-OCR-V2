Based on the infographic, **Generation Z (Gen Z)** is the most demanding generation after Millennials.

This is highlighted in the **"Delivery expectations have decreased"** section, where it states:

- **Millennials** were the **most demanding**, with **19%** still expecting goods in **no more than 24 hours** after ordering.
- **Gen Z closely followed with 18%**, also expecting delivery within 24 hours.
