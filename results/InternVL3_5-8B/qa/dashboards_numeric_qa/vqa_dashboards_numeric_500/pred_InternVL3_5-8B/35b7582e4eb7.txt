The infographic highlights several key points regarding the challenges faced by LGBT youth:

1. **Homophobic Remarks by Teachers**: 30% of LGBT students reported that teachers were present when homophobic remarks were made.
2. **Verbal Harassment**: More than 8 out of 10 LGBT students have experienced verbal harassment in the past year.
3. **Frequency of Homophobic Slurs**: 65% of students heard homophobic slurs often or frequently.
4. **Skipping School**: 30% of students skipped a day of school in the past month because they felt unsafe.
5. **Unstable Environment**: Nearly 5% of LGBT youth live in an unstable environment, including facing homelessness.
6. **Lack of Action by School Officials**: 62% of LGBT students who reported harassment to school officials said nothing was done.

These statistics underscore the significant challenges and hostile environments that many LGBT students face, emphasizing the need for supportive measures and policies in schools.
