## Malaysian Users Shopping Online on a Yearly Basis

Based on the pie chart, to calculate the percentage of Malaysian users who shop online on a yearly basis, we need to combine two categories:

- **A few times per year**: 56.7%
- **Once a year**: 13.8%

**Total shopping on a yearly basis = 56.7% + 13.8% = 70.5%**

Therefore, **70.5%** of Malaysian online shoppers purchased over the internet on a yearly basis (either a few times per year or once a year) as of February 2017.
