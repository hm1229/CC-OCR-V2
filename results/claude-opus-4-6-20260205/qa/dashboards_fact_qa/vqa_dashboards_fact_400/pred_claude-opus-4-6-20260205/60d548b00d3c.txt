## Instagram Daily Usage Statistics

Based on the infographic, **59% of users log in to Instagram every day**, and **35% log in multiple times per day**.

These statistics highlight the high level of daily engagement on the platform, showing that more than half of Instagram's user base accesses the platform at least once daily, with over a third returning multiple times throughout the day.

Additionally, the infographic notes that Instagram had **over 500 million active users** at the time, meaning a significant portion of that 500 million+ user base was logging in daily.
