$$\text{Tr}[(A_m \otimes A_n) R_{AB}]$$

$$= \text{Tr}[(A_m \otimes A_n)(\rho_A \otimes \frac{1}{2}) E_{AB} + E_{AB}(\rho_A \otimes \frac{1}{2})]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2}[\text{Tr}((A_m \rho \otimes A_n) E_{AB}) + \text{Tr}((A_m \otimes A_n) E_{AB}(\rho \otimes \mathbb{1}))]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2}\left[\sum_{i,j} \text{Tr}(A_m \rho |i\rangle\langle j|) \text{Tr}(A_n \mathcal{E}_{AIB}(|i\rangle\langle j|)) + \sum_j \text{Tr}(A_m \mathcal{E}_{AIB}(|i\rangle\langle j|)) \text{Tr}(A_n \mathcal{E}_{AIB}(|i\rangle\langle j|))\right]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2}\left[\sum_{i,j} \langle i|(A_m \rho + \rho A_m)|i\rangle \text{Tr}(A_n \mathcal{E}_{AIB}(|i\rangle\langle j|))\right]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2}\left[\sum_{i,j} \langle i|(A_m \rho + \rho A_m)|i\rangle \text{Tr}\left(A_n \sum_k V_k |i\rangle\langle j| V_k^\dagger\right)\right]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2}\left[\sum_{i,j} \langle i|(A_m \rho + \rho A_m)|i\rangle\langle j| \sum_k V_k^\dagger A_n V_k |i\rangle\right]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2}\left[\sum_{i,j} \langle i|(A_m \rho + \rho A_m) \sum_k V_k^\dagger A_n V_k |i\rangle\right]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2} \text{Tr}\left[\sum_{i,j} \langle i|(A_m \rho + \rho A_m) \sum_k V_k^\dagger A_n V_k\right]$$

$$= \frac{1}{2} \text{Tr}[(A_m \rho + \rho A_m) \mathcal{E}_{AIB}^\dagger(A_n)]$$

$$= \langle A_m, A_n \rangle_{\text{seq}}$$
