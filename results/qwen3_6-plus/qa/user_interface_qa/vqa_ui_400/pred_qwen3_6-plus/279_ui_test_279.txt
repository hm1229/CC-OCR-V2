Based on the provided infographic from the UK Department of Health & Social Care:

Under the section **“Can I get tested now?”** for the **Laboratory test** (which is a type of Antibody test), it states:

> “This test is being offered to NHS and social care staff, and to NHS hospital inpatients and care home residents **based on their clinician’s advice**”

---

✅ Therefore, **a clinician’s advice** is required for conducting a laboratory antibody test.

*(Note: The “Swab test” — which detects active infection — does not require clinician advice for symptomatic individuals or essential workers; they can self-refer via the NHS website. But the question specifically asks about the “laboratory test,” which in this context refers to the lab-based antibody test.)*
