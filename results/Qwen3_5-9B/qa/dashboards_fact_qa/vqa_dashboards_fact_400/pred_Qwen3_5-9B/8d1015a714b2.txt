Based on the infographic, we can calculate the percentage of people who used **only** apps on smartphones.

The relevant data is:
- **79%** of learning materials were accessed via Apps by smartphone.
- **21%** on their tablets.
- **17%** installed the App on both tablets and smartphones.

To find those who used **only** smartphones, we subtract those who used both devices from the total smartphone users:

> **79% (smartphone users) – 17% (both devices) = 62%**

---

✅ **Answer: 62%** of people used apps **only** on smartphones.
