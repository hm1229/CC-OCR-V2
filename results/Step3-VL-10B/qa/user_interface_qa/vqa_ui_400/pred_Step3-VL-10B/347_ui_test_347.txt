To determine which side had more strength during **Operation Phantom Fury** (2004), we analyze the infographic:  

- **Insurgents**: Commanded by Al - Janabi/Hadid, with 4,000 men.  
- **Coalition**: Commanded by U.S. Natonski/Mattis, with 10,500 U.S. troops, 2,000 Iraqi (IRQ) troops, and 850 U.K. troops.  

The Coalition’s total force (10,500 + 2,000 + 850 = ~13,350) is significantly larger than the Insurgents’ 4,000. Thus, the **Coalition** had more strength during Operation Phantom Fury.
