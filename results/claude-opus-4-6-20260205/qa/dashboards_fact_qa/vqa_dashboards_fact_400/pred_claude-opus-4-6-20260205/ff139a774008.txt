## Concerns About Lack of Social Interactions During Mealtimes in USA/UK

According to the infographic, in **USA/UK**, **younger generations** reported higher concerns about the lack of social interactions during mealtimes, with **54% of Millennials** expressing this concern.

This contrasts with the **Asia** region, where **older generations** reported higher concerns about this issue (88% of Boomers and 73% of Gen X).
