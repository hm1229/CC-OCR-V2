According to the infographic, the majority of millennials in the U.S. choose online viewing primarily for the following reasons:

1. **Immediate access** – 56% cite this as a key reason.
2. **Multitasking** – 49% appreciate the ability to multitask while consuming media.
3. **Convenience of viewing location** – 44% value the flexibility of watching content from anywhere.

These factors highlight millennials' preference for flexibility, speed, and integration of media consumption into their daily routines.
