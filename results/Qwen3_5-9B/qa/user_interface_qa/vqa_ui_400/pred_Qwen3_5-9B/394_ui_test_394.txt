Based on the provided infographic, the biscuits used in WWI soldier rations were **Anzac biscuits**.

The section on "ANZAC BISCUITS" states:
> "Anzac biscuits are closely associated with the Australian and New Zealand Army Corps (ANZAC), established in WWI."
> "Bread substitute: The original biscuits used in WWI soldier rations were hard and savoury."

It also notes that the first known recipe was published in 1917 in 'The War Chest Cookery Book', and they were eggless because eggs were scarce during the war.
