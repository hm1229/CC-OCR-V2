$$\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a|K_g(t,x)-K_g(s,y)|^p dt\,dx\,ds\,dy$$

$$=\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a\left|\int_0^t\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}K(t-r,x)g(x-z)\,dz\,dW(r)\right.$$

$$\left.-\int_0^s\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}K(s-r,z)g(y-z)\,dz\,dW(r)\right|^p dt\,dx\,ds\,dy$$

$$\leq N(p)\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a\left|\int_0^s\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}(K(t-r,x)-K(s-r,x))g(y-z)\,dz\,dW(r)\right|^p$$

$$+N(p)\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a\left|\int_0^s\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}K(t-r,x)(g(x-z)-g(y-z))\,dz\,dW(r)\right|^p$$

$$+N(p)\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a\left|\int_s^t\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}K(t-r,z)g(x-z)\,dz\,dW(r)\right|^p dt\,dx\,ds\,dy$$

$$\leq N(p)\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a\left(\int_0^s\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}|(K(t-r,x)-K(s-r,x))g(y-z)\,dz|^2\,dr\right)^{\frac{p}{2}}$$

$$+N(p)\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a\left(\int_0^s\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}|K(t-r,x)(g(x-z)-g(y-z))\,dz|^2\,dr\right)^{\frac{p}{2}}$$

$$+N(p)\mathbb{E}\int_a\int_a\left(\int_s^t\int_{\mathbb{R}^d}|K(t-r,z)g(x-z)\,dz|^2\,dr\right)^{\frac{p}{2}}$$

$$=: I_1+I_2+I_3$$
