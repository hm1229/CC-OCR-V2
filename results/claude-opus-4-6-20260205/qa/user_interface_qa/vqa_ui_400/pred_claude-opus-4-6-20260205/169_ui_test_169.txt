## The Ferris Wheel

According to the infographic, the **Ferris wheel** is described as "now an amusement park fixture across the globe." It made its debut as the largest attraction at the **1893 Chicago World's Fair**. It was designed and constructed by **George Washington Gale Ferris Jr.**, and the original ferris wheel stood **80.4 metres tall** and rotated on **71 tonnes, 45.5 foot axle**.
