To determine how much more the "powerful" spend on themselves than on others, refer to the "WHY GIFT BUYING IS A POWER PLAY" section.  

- For the "Powerful" group:  
  - Amount spent on self: $13  
  - Amount spent on other: $7.10  

Calculate the difference: \( 13 - 7.10 = 5.9 \)  

Thus, the powerful spend $5.90 more on themselves than on others.
