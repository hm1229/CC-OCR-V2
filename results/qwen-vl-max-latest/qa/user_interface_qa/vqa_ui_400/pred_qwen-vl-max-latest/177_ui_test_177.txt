To find the combined food price change of fresh milk and cheese, we add their individual percentage increases:

- Fresh milk: **24.1%**
- Cheese: **51.3%**

Now, add them together:

$$
24.1\% + 51.3\% = 75.4\%
$$

### Answer:
The combined food price change for fresh milk and cheese is **75.4%** since 2006.
