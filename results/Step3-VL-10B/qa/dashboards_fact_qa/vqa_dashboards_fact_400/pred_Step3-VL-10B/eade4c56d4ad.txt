To determine how much less the recipient values a diamond ring as a gift compared to its actual price, we look at the "THE GIFT VALUATION GAP" section:  

- Gift Price of the diamond ring: $1,000  
- Value to Recipient of the diamond ring: $820  

Calculate the difference: \( 1000 - 820 = 180 \)  

The value perceived by the recipient is $180 less than the actual price.
