The decentralized section outlines four steps of app processing. Here's a summary of each step:

1. **Broadcasting Random ID**: The app broadcasts a random ID and collects the random IDs of other apps it interacts with, along with some data about the interaction (time and distance).

2. **Reporting Unwellness**: If a user reports they are unwell, they can choose to upload their ID to a central database.

3. **Downloading Database**: Users' phones regularly download the most up-to-date version of this database and look for IDs they have interacted with.

4. **Contact Notification**: If they see any IDs they have interacted with, their app calculates whether or not to inform them that they have been in contact with an infected person.
