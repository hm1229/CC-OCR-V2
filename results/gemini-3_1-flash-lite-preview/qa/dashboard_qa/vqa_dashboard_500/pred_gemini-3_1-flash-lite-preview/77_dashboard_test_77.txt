Based on the infographic provided, there are **six** ways Pinterest can help your business:

1.  **Creates Backlinks To Your Site:** Uploading pins and having them repinned creates links that help search engines formulate your page rankings.
2.  **Boosts Your Keyword Targeting:** Using keywords throughout your posts and content helps your page rank higher when those keywords are searched.
3.  **Boosts Your Social Signals:** Pins provide an opportunity to generate social signals, which are factored into Google and other search engine rankings.
4.  **Opportunity for New Content:** Posting new images and content puts you in a good position with both your viewers and search engines.
5.  **SEO Image Boost:** Optimizing your photos with keyword-rich names, descriptions, and your business name helps you rank in search engine image results.
6.  **General SEO/Ranking Improvement:** While not listed as a separate header, the infographic emphasizes that all these factors combined work together to improve your overall search engine rankings and the value of your backlinks.
