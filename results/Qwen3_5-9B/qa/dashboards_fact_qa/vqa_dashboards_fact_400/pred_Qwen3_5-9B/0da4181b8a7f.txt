Based on the infographic, under the section **“THE TIME SUCK”**, it states:

> This year, each person will spend **22.5 hours** on gift-buying and related tasks.

The breakdown includes:
- **Shopping**: 15 hours  
- **Waiting in checkout lines**: 3.5 hours  
- **Wrapping**: 3 hours  
- **Returning gifts**: 1 hour  

To find the total hours spent **wrapping gifts or waiting in checkout lines**, we add:

**3.5 (waiting) + 3 (wrapping) = 6.5 hours**

✅ **Answer: 6.5 hours**
