Looking at the section **“THE GIFT CARD VALUATION GAP”** in the infographic:

- The **unused 1-800-FLOWERS.COM gift card** has a **face value of $100**.
- Its **market value** (what someone would pay for it on the secondary market) is **$70**.

To find how much less the market value is than the actual card value:

> **$100 - $70 = $30**

---

✅ **Answer: $30**
