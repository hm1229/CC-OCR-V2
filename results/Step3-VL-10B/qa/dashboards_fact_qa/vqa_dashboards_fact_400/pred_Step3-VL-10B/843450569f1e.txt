To determine how much less the recipient values the iPad as a gift compared to its actual price, we find the values in the "THE GIFT VALUATION GAP" section:  

- Gift Price of iPad: $499  
- Value to Recipient of iPad: $409  

Subtract the recipient’s perceived value from the gift price:  
$499 - $409 = $90  

So, the recipient values the iPad as a gift $90 less than its actual price.
