To determine the total number of CAR refugees in **Congo** and **Chad**, we refer to the "Total population of CAR refugees" box on the right - hand side of the map:  

- For **Chad**: The value is 80,364.  
- For **Congo** (specifically the *Republic of Congo*, as distinguished from the Democratic Republic of the Congo in the box): The value is 11,000.  

Adding these two values together: \( 80,364 + 11,000 = 91,364 \).  


Thus, the total number of CAR refugees in Congo (Republic of Congo) and Chad is 91,364.
