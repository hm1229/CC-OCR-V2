Based on the image and general knowledge, the **sun** is the source of renewable energy.

Here's why:

- **Bulbs, CFL (Compact Fluorescent Light), and LED** are all **lighting technologies/devices** that *use* energy to produce light — they are not energy sources themselves.

- The **sun** is a **natural, renewable energy source** that provides solar energy. As shown in the infographic under "GREEN ENERGY," it recommends "use the sun, open windows" for natural lighting and heating.

The sun's energy is renewable because it is virtually inexhaustible and will continue to shine for billions of years, unlike fossil fuels which are finite resources.
