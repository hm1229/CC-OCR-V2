To find the combined food price change of fresh milk and cheese, we first locate their respective percentages in the "FOOD PRICE CHANGES SINCE 2006" section:  

- Fresh Milk: 24.1%  
- Cheese: 51.3%  

Adding these two values together: \( 24.1 + 51.3 = 75.4\% \)  


The combined food price change of fresh milk and cheese is 75.4%.
