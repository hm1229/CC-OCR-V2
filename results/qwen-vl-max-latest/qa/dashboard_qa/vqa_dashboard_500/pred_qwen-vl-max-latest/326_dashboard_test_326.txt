The chance of disability for senior citizens in Australia is significantly higher compared to the general population. According to the infographic:

- **2 in 5 people with disability are 65 years or older**, which means **40%** of individuals with disabilities in Australia are aged 65 and above.
- Additionally, **50.7%** of Australians aged 65 and over have some form of disability.

This highlights that the likelihood of living with a disability increases with age, particularly among seniors.
