To determine how many out of six Australians are not affected with hearing loss, we start with the information from the infographic:  

- It states “1 in 6 Australians are affected by hearing loss.”  

If 1 out of 6 is affected, then the number **not** affected is \( 6 - 1 = 5 \).  


Thus, out of six Australians, 5 are not affected with hearing loss.
