Maan-gaab lumaayiyo
Mid baa ceel gannuniyo
Kuu haysta gocoroo;
Mid baa kugu goblama oo
Gurxankiiisu damihayn
Haddii saxar ku gaadhoo;
Gaada wax bookiyo
Mid baad good la tahayyo;
Mid baad garab laaxaadliyo
Ruux guda abaaloo,
Loo galo wanaag iyo
Gaashaan la tahayyo;

Garaadlaay xogtadaadii
Cidi gaadhi waydee
Dadkan kugu gababsiye
Kumaa helay guntaadoo
Gacan-qaad la siyaa?
Miyeey gabi habaabeen?
Maadiga wax gabayoo
Hadba gee is-mariyoo,
Goobba midab la joogoo
Gallibaxa habeemkii?

Garaad-daran nafdaydaay!
In kastoon gucleeyo,
Garmaamada haldaagiyoo
Gammankaaba ka jittoo
Cirka sare galaal-baxo,
Adigay la gooshoo

Iga gaabinayncoon,
Goobtaan is-taagaaba
Adiga wax galluubane,
