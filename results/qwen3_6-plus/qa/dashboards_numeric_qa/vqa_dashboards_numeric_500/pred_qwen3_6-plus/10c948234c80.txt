To calculate the **expected rise in bird deaths due to wind turbines by 2030** from the current figure:

- Current annual deaths (as shown in infographic): **440,000 birds**
- Projected deaths by 2030: **over 1,000,000 birds**

> Note: The infographic says “By 2030, that number will exceed 1 million.” So we’ll use **1,000,000** as the projected figure for calculation purposes.

---

### Rise = Projected (2030) – Current

**1,000,000 – 440,000 = 560,000**

---

✅ **Answer: 560,000**

This is the expected *increase* in bird deaths per year due to wind turbines by 2030 compared to the current rate.
