The data indicates that Democrats and Independents are more likely to have purchased solar panels and all-electric vehicles compared to Republicans. Specifically:

- **Solar Panels (Photovoltaic - PV):** Both Democrats and Independents show higher likelihoods of having purchased solar panels.
- **All-Electric Vehicles:** Democrats and Independents also show a higher likelihood of having purchased all-electric vehicles.

Republicans are less likely to have made these purchases compared to the other groups.
