## Easy Snacking Swaps

According to the infographic, the healthier swap suggestions are:

| Instead of... | Have... |
|--------------|---------|
| **Cereal** (156) | **All-Bran** (151) |
| **Bananas** (118) | **Oranges** (202) |
| **Potato Chips** (91) | **Popcorn** (154) |

The numbers represent the **Satiety Index** scores — so the recommended swaps have **higher satiety values**, meaning they keep you fuller for longer after eating 240 calories of that food.

> 🍊 **Oranges** are the standout swap, with a satiety score of **202**, making them significantly more filling than bananas.
