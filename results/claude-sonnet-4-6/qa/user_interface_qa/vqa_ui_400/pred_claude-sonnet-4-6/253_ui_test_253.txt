## Majority Mobile Device Usage for Home Buying

According to the infographic, **At Home** is where the majority of new home shoppers use their mobile devices, with **77%** of home buyers using their mobile devices there.

This is significantly higher than other locations:
- 🏠 **At Home** - **77%**
- 💼 At Work - 31%
- ⏳ Waiting in Line - 28%
- 🍽️ At a Restaurant - 27%
- 🏘️ Other People's Homes - 26%
