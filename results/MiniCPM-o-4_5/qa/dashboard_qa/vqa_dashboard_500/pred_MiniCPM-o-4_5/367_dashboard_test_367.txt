That would be the sum of "Somewhat" and "No, not at all" — but actually, since "Somewhat" implies partial agreement, the strict "do not agree" is likely the "No, not at all" portion. So: 24% + 4% = 28%

Alternatively, if the question intends only the “No, not at all” part, it would be 4%. But given the phrasing “do not agree”, it’s more reasonable to include both negative responses.
