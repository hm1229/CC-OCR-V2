To find the percentage of Grade 5 children in South Africa who were able to read at the basic reading level, we can start by looking at the given statistic:

"43% of our Grade 5 children were unable to read at even the basic reading level..."

This means that 43% could not reach the basic level. Therefore, the percentage who *were* able to read at the basic level is the remainder of the 100%.

100% - 43% = 57%

So, 57% of Grade 5 children were able to read at the basic level.

Answer: 57%
