The two main types of coronavirus (COVID - 19) tests presented in the image are:  

1. **Swab test** – Used to answer “Do I have it?” (determines if you currently have the virus).  
2. **Antibody test** – Used to answer “Have I had it?” (determines if you have had the virus in the past, even if you’re not currently infected).  

(Note: The swab test itself has sub - types like laboratory test and home test, but the two overarching categories of tests for COVID - 19 in this infographic are the swab test and the antibody test.)
