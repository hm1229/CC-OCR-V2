According to the infographic, 28% of the money spent by The Humane Society in 2008 was allocated to "Campaigns & Investigation." This section highlights the organization's efforts in investigating, exposing, and helping prosecute animal cruelty cases. These activities include puppy mills, foie gras farming, dog fighting, and other forms of animal abuse. The funds are used to support legal actions, gather evidence, and raise public awareness to combat these issues effectively.
