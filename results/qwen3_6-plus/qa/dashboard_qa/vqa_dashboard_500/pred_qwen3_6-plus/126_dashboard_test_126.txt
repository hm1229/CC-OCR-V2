To find how much more the **total civilian casualties per month** were in **2012** compared to **2011**, we subtract the 2011 monthly average from the 2012 monthly average:

- **2012**: 2,882 total civilian casualties per month  
- **2011**: 2,510 total civilian casualties per month  

**Difference** = 2,882 - 2,510 = **372**

---

✅ **Answer: 372 more civilian casualties per month in 2012 compared to 2011.**
