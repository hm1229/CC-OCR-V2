The red card (labeled **04**) states:

> **LACK OF ACTIONABLE DATA**  
> *Do your providers have access to data at the point of care that is accurate and easily actionable?*

---

### Summary:
The reason stated on the red card is **“Lack of Actionable Data”** — meaning providers may not have timely, accurate, or usable data when they need it most (at the point of care), which hinders effective decision-making and population health management.

This is one of the five signs suggesting it’s time to reassess your Population Health Strategy.
