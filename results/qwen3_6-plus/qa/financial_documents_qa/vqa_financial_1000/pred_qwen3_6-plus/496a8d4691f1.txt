The convention city mentioned on the Avis Reservation Card is **Monterey**.

This is indicated in the field labeled “(convention city)” next to the convention name “I.A.R.W.” (International Association of Refrigerated Warehouses).

Additionally, the vertical text on the right side of the card confirms the event location:
> INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF REFRIGERATED WAREHOUSES  
> 88th ANNUAL MEETING  
> DEL MONTE HYATT HOUSE  
> **MONTEREY, CALIFORNIA**

✅ So, the convention city is **Monterey**.
