\section*{III. CALCULATIONS:}

Calculating the volume of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

\[
V_b = a \cdot b \cdot c
\]

$V_b$ - volume of examined bodies [cm$^3$], $a$ - width of examined bodies [cm], $b$ - length of examined bodies [cm], $c$ - height of examined bodies [cm].

\[
V_b = 2{,}5 \text{ [cm]} \cdot 4{,}2 \text{ [cm]} \cdot 1{,}5 \text{ [cm]} = 15{,}75 \text{ [cm}^3\text{]}
\]

Calculating the material density of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

\[
g_b = \frac{m_b}{V_b}
\]

$g_b$ - material density of given body [g/cm$^3$], $m_b$ - mass of given body [g], $V_b$ - volume of examined body.

\[
g_b \text{ (ebonite)} = \frac{22{,}7}{15{,}75} \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1{,}44127 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1441{,}27 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]}
\]

\[
g_b \text{ (bakelite)} = \frac{19{,}8}{15{,}75} \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1{,}25714 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1257{,}14 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]}
\]

\[
g_b \text{ (aluminium (10))} = \frac{42{,}6}{15{,}75} \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 2{,}70476 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 2704{,}76 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]}
\]

Calculating the material density using aerometer based on the formula:

\[
g_b' = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{m_3 - m_2} \cdot g_w
\]

$g_b'$ - material density of given body using aerometer [g/cm$^3$], $m_n$ - values of mass from experiment [g], $g_w$ - density of distilled water $= 1$ [g/cm$^3$].

\[
g_b' \text{ (ebonite)} = \frac{110 - 67}{103 - 67} \text{ [g/g]} \cdot 1 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = \frac{23}{16} \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1{,}4375 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1437{,}5 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]}
\]

\[
g_b' \text{ (bakelite)} = \frac{110 - 90}{105 - 90} \text{ [g/g]} \cdot 1 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = \frac{20}{15} \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1{,}33333 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 1333{,}33 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]}
\]

\[
g_b' \text{ (aluminium (10))} = \frac{110 - 67}{83 - 67} \text{ [g/g]} \cdot 1 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = \frac{43}{16} \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 2{,}6875 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 2687{,}5 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]}
\]

\[
g_b' \text{ (aluminium (11))} = \frac{110 - 86}{94 - 86} \text{ [g/g]} \cdot 1 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = \frac{24}{8} \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 3 \text{ [g/cm}^3\text{]} = 3000 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]}
\]

Comparing calculated density values with table density values:

\[
g_b \text{ (ebonite)} - g_{\text{tab}} \text{ (ebonite)} = 1441{,}27 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} - 1150 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 291{,}27 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 22{,}48 \text{ [\%]}
\]

\[
g_b' \text{ (ebonite)} - g_{\text{tab}} \text{ (ebonite)} = 1437{,}5 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} - 1150 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 287{,}5 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 22{,}22 \text{ [\%]}
\]

\[
g_b \text{ (bakelite)} - g_{\text{tab}} \text{ (bakelite)} = 1257{,}14 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} - 1300 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 42{,}86 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 3{,}35 \text{ [\%]}
\]

\[
g_b' \text{ (bakelite)} - g_{\text{tab}} \text{ (bakelite)} = 1333{,}33 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} - 1300 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 33{,}33 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 2{,}53 \text{ [\%]}
\]

\[
g_b \text{ (aluminium(10))} - g_{\text{tab}} \text{ (aluminium)} = 2704{,}76 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} - 2700 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 4{,}76 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 0{,}17 \text{ [\%]}
\]

\[
g_b' \text{ (aluminium (10))} - g_{\text{tab}} \text{ (aluminium)} = 2687{,}5 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} - 2700 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 12{,}5 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 0{,}46 \text{ [\%]}
\]

\[
g_b' \text{ (aluminium (11))} - g_{\text{tab}} \text{ (aluminium)} = 3000 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} - 2700 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 300 \text{ [kg/m}^3\text{]} = 10{,}53 \text{ [\%]}
\]
