Based on the infographic provided, here is the information regarding the shelf life of **dry dog food**:

Under the section **“BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD”**, the right side shows a bag of dry dog food (purple bag with a dog face logo).

The details listed are:

- **Shelf Life (Unopened):** 12–15 months from date packaged.
- **Once Opened, Store At:** <80° F
- **Once Opened, Keeps For:** 90 days (store in a cool, dry place)

---

✅ So, to directly answer your question:

> The **shelf life for unopened dry dog food** is **12–15 months** from the date it was packaged.  
> Once opened, it should be used within **90 days** if stored properly (<80°F in a cool, dry place).

*(Note: This differs from canned/wet food, which has a shorter shelf life once opened — only 3 days refrigerated.)*
