Based on the image, the emergency benefits provided to working Canadians are:

## Emergency Care Benefit
- **Up to $900 bi-weekly** for up to **15 weeks**
- For those who must stay home due to COVID-19 and don't have paid sick leave
- Includes individuals who are sick, those who can't access EI and are sick, or those taking care of a child or someone who is sick

## Emergency Support Benefit
- **$5 billion** in total support
- For workers who are not eligible for EI and who are facing unemployment and reduced hours

These benefits are part of Canada's $27 billion in direct support for Canadian workers and businesses, plus $55 billion in business liquidity support through tax deferrals.
