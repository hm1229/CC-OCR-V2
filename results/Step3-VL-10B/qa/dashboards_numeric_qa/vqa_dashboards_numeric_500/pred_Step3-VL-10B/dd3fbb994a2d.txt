To determine the VMware version with the highest number of guest machines, refer to the **"VMWARE VSPHERE VS. MICROSOFT HYPER-V"** table. Locate the row for *Max guests per host*:  

- VMware STANDARD: 512  
- VMware ENTERPRISE: 512  
- VMware ENT. PLUS: 512 (implied, as the table shows consistency for these VMware tiers)  

Among VMware’s offerings, the maximum number of guests per host is **512**, shared across VMware Standard, Enterprise, and Enterprise Plus editions. This is higher than Microsoft Hyper - V’s maximum (384 for Hyper - V Standard/Enterprise/DC).
