I need to find the percentage of millennials who do NOT want their daily tasks to be automated.

From the infographic, in the section "I wish more of the day-to-day tasks I do were automated":
- 56% of Millennials wish more of their day-to-day tasks were automated

To find those who do NOT want this:
100% - 56% = **44%**

So, 44% of millennials do not want their daily tasks to be automated.
