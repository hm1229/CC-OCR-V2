\section*{SECTION A: READING SKILLS \hfill (12+10=22 Marks) \hfill 50 Periods}
\begin{itemize}
    \item The section will have two unseen passages with the maximum word limit of 750 words. The passages can have continuous and non-continuous text inspired from the themes in prescribed books. Please refer to the Main Course Book (MCB) for types of non-continuous texts. (For example--Unit 1 has lists, tables, cues, message, telephone conversation etc.).
    \item Objective Type Questions (including Multiple Choice Questions), Very Short Answer Type Questions (one word/ one phrase / one sentence) and Short Answer Type Questions (30-40 words each) will be asked to test interpretation, analysis, inference, evaluation and vocabulary in context.
\end{itemize}

\section*{SECTION B: WRITING SKILLS \hfill 22 Marks \hfill 50 Periods}
This section will have a variety of short and long writing tasks.
\begin{itemize}
    \item Email to school authorities (Application for leave/ change of subject /change of section/ bus-timings or similar topics) in maximum 50 words \hfill \textbf{3 Marks}
    \item Factual Description of a person/object in maximum 100 words \hfill \textbf{4 Marks}
    \item One out of two formal letters, in maximum 120 words, thematically aligned to topics in MCB. \hfill \textbf{7 Marks}
    \item One out of two articles based on verbal cues, in maximum 150 words, thematically aligned to MCB topics. \hfill \textbf{8 Marks}
\end{itemize}

\section*{SECTION C: GRAMMAR \hfill 10 Marks \hfill 20 periods}
Grammar items will be taught and assessed over a period of time.
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Tenses
    \item Modals
    \item Subject - verb concord
    \item Reported speech
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Commands and requests
        \item Statements
        \item Questions
    \end{itemize}
\end{enumerate}
