To determine the total number of CAR refugees in **Congo** and **Cameroon**, we refer to the "Total population of CAR refugees" box on the right - hand side of the map:  

- For **Cameroon**: The value is 98,504.  
- For **Congo**: Here, “Congo” refers to the *Republic of Congo* (not the Democratic Republic of the Congo, which is abbreviated as DRC). The value for the Republic of Congo is 11,000.  

Adding these two values together: \( 98,504 + 11,000 = 109,504 \).  


Thus, the total number of CAR refugees in Congo (Republic of Congo) and Cameroon is 109,504.
