Providing a disposable surgical mask is part of **Step 1: ISOLATE**.

According to the infographic:

> **1. ISOLATE**  
> Isolate the person from others and provide a disposable surgical mask, if available, for the person to wear.

This step applies when there is a **suspected or confirmed case of COVID-19 at work**.

---

✅ So, the answer is: **Step 1 — ISOLATE**
