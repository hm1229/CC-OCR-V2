To calculate the total awarded by *NIHR BRC* and *King's*:
1.  £320,000 was awarded by **NIHR BRC** to 8 translational COVID-19 research studies
2.  £320,000 was awarded by **King's Together's Rapid COVID-19 call** for 19 projects run in partnership with Guy's and St Thomas'.

Added together, the total awarded by these two bodies is **£640,000 (GBP)**.
