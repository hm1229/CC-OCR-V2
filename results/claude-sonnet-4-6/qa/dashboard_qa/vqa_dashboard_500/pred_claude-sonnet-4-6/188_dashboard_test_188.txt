## Biggest Challenge in Technology Disruption

According to the infographic, **Competition from New Rivals** is the biggest perceived downside of technology disruption, with the highest percentage at approximately **42%**.

This is followed by:
- **Regulatory Challenges** (~35%)
- **Inability of IT Systems to Keep Up with Change** (~32%)
- **Threats to Data or Physical Security** (~28%)
- **Disaster Price Transparency/Lower Profit Margins** (~26%)
- **Job Losses Due to Outsourcing or Automation** (~25%)
