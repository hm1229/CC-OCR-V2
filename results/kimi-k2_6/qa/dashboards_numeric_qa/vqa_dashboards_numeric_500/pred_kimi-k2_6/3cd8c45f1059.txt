Based on the infographic, shoppers from **Gen Z** and **Millennials** are trying out new online retailers.

Specifically:
- **32% of Gen Z**
- **35% of Millennials**

These are the generations that said they had tried new online retailers because of the lockdown and intend to continue shopping with them. In contrast, 43% of Baby Boomers and 48% of the Silent Generation did **not** try new online retailers.
