To expose children to **1 million written words in a year**, **just 15 minutes of reading each day** is required. This information is highlighted in the infographic under the section titled *"JUST 15 MINUTES A DAY"*, which states:

> "15 minutes of reading with your children each day can expose them to 1 million written words in a year."  
> — Anderson, Wilson, & Fielding, 1988

So, the daily requirement is **15 minutes of reading**.
