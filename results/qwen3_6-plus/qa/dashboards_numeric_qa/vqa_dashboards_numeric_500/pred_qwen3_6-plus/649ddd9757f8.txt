According to the infographic, **most people believe the driverless car manufacturer is at fault** if a driverless car hits a person.

Specifically:
- **73.72%** say **“The driverless car manufacturer”** is at fault.
- 13.76% say “Those inside the car” are at fault.
- 12.52% say “The pedestrian” is at fault.

This overwhelming majority (nearly 3 out of 4 respondents) places responsibility on the company that built the autonomous vehicle — likely reflecting the view that since the car operates without human control, the manufacturer bears liability for its actions or failures.
