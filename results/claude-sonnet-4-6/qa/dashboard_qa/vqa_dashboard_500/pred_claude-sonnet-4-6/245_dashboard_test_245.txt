## Expected Rise in Bird Deaths Due to Wind Turbines

- **Current figure:** 440,000 birds killed per year
- **Expected figure by 2030:** Over **1,000,000** (1 million)

### Rise = 1,000,000 - 440,000 = **560,000+ birds**

This represents an increase of more than **127%** from the current annual death toll caused by wind turbines.
