## Shortest Length in Long Cycling Routes

Looking at the **Long Routes** section, the cycling routes and their lengths are:

1. **Ring of the Reeks Cycle**, MacGillycuddy Reeks, Co. Kerry - **37KM / 23 MILES**
2. **Burren Cycling Route, Long Loop**, Co. Clare - **100KM / 64 MILES**
3. **Inishowen Scenic Cycle**, Co. Donegal - **100 KM / 100 MILES**
4. **Ring of Kerry Scenic Cycle**, Co. Kerry - **200KM / 125 MILES**

The shortest length one can cycle in the long cycling routes is **37KM / 23 MILES** on the **Ring of the Reeks Cycle** in MacGillycuddy Reeks, Co. Kerry.
