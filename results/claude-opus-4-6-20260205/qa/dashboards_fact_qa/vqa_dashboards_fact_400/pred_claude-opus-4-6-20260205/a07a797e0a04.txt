# Battle of Blore Heath - Troop Strength

The **Lancastrians** had more strength during the Battle of Blore Heath, with **8,000 - 14,000 men**, compared to the **Yorkists** who had only **3,000 - 5,000 men**.

Despite this significant numerical advantage, the Yorkists were able to use tactical advantages (such as feigning defeat to bait a charge by Lancastrian cavalry, then slaughtering them from a superior uphill position) to overcome the Lancastrians' greater numbers.
