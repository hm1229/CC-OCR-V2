To determine the percentage of adults who didn’t support the cigarette ban, we first find the percentage of adults who **did** support it. In the “Will you support the ban?” section, it states that 80% of adults support the ban.  

To find the percentage of adults who didn’t support it, subtract the support percentage from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 80\% = 20\% \)  


Thus, 20% of adults didn’t support the cigarette ban.
