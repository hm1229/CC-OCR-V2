The antibody test that involves blood testing by a medical professional is the laboratory test. This type of test is designed to determine if a person has had a previous infection with the virus, as it detects antibodies in the blood. The image specifies that this test is being offered to NHS and social care staff, and to NHS hospital and care home patients and residents based on their clinician's advice. It's important to note that while this test can indicate past infection, it does not provide information about current infection status or immunity level, and social distancing measures must still be followed.
