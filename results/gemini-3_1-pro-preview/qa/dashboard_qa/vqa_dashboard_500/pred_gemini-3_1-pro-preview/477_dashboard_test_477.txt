Based on the image provided, under the section titled "WORKING CANADIANS," there are two emergency benefits listed:

1.  **Emergency Care Benefit:** This is for individuals who must stay home due to COVID-19 and do not have paid sick leave. This includes people who cannot access EI and are sick, or those taking care of a child or someone who is sick. It provides **up to $900 bi-weekly for 15 weeks**.
2.  **Emergency Support Benefit:** This benefit provides support to workers who are not eligible for EI and who are facing unemployment and reduced hours. The total support for this benefit is **$5 billion**.
