Once opened, canned dog food can be stored for a longer time compared to dry dog food. According to the infographic:

- **Dry Dog Food**: Once opened, it should be stored at a temperature below 40°F and can keep for 3 days if refrigerated.
- **Canned Dog Food**: Once opened, it should be stored at a temperature below 80°F and can keep for 90 days if stored in a cool, dry place.

Therefore, canned dog food has a significantly longer shelf life once it is opened.
