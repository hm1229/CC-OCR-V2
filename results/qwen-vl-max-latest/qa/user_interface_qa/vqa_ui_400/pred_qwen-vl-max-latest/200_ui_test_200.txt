The two aspects considered in the **gift gender gap** analysis are:

1. **Who does all the shopping** – This aspect looks at whether women do all the holiday gift shopping, with 75% of women agreeing and 49% of men agreeing.
2. **Whether couples share the responsibility** – This aspect examines if both partners share the gift-buying responsibility, with 33% of men and 20% of women agreeing.

These insights highlight differing perceptions between men and women regarding who handles gift-buying responsibilities during the holidays.
