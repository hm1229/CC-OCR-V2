According to the infographic, **most people (52.4%) research the company** while preparing for interviews.

This is the highest percentage among the listed preparation methods:

- **Research the company**: 52.4%
- Relax to ease nerves: 33.9%
- Practice interview questions: 10.5%
- Videotape themselves: 3.2%

So, researching the company is the most common interview preparation technique reported in this 2013 survey.

*(Source: FindEmployment / www.employmentsurvey.net)*
