To find the **combined unemployment rate** of tertiary and national education levels, we simply add their individual rates as shown in the bar chart:

- **Tertiary**: 11.2%
- **National**: 4.2%

**Combined = 11.2% + 4.2% = 15.4%**

> ✅ **Answer: 15.4%**

*(Note: This is a simple arithmetic sum — not a weighted average or any other statistical combination — since the question asks for the “combined” rate, implying addition.)*
