## Internet Access Gap in Education - Key Findings

Based on the infographic, here are the detailed insights:

### What Students Do Online (Top 5 Activities)
1. **95%** - Research or search for information online
2. **79%** - Access or download assignments from an online site
3. **76%** - Submit assignments online
4. **40%** - Develop, share, or post their work on a website, wiki, or blog
5. **39%** - Participate in online discussions

### Access to Internet/Digital Technologies

**At Home:**
- All or almost all: 3%
- Most: 54%
- Only some: 28%
- Hardly any: 2%

**In School:**
- All or almost all: 54%
- Most: 27%
- Only some: 15%
- Hardly any: 5%

### Access to Digital Tools by Socioeconomic Status

**At Home:**
- Below poverty line/Low: 3%
- Lower middle: 7%
- Middle: 19%
- Upper middle/Upper: **52%**

**In School:**
- Below poverty line/Low: 45%
- Lower middle: 49%
- Middle: 56%
- Upper middle/Upper: **67%**

### Answer to the Question

**67%** of students in schools belong to the upper middle/upper class category who have access to digital tools, according to the "Access to Digital Tools by Socioeconomic Status" section.
