# MATHEMATICS RULES

## Trigonometry

### 1) Trigonometry Ratios:

$$\sin\theta = \frac{\text{Opp}}{\text{Hyp}}, \quad \cos\theta = \frac{\text{Adj}}{\text{Hyp}}$$

$$\tan\theta = \frac{\text{Opp}}{\text{Adj}}, \quad \cot\theta = \frac{\text{Adj}}{\text{Opp}}$$

$$\sec\theta = \frac{\text{Hyp}}{\text{Adj}}, \quad \csc\theta = \frac{\text{Hyp}}{\text{Opp}}$$

### 2) Imp. Results:

i) $\sin\theta \cdot \csc\theta = 1$ \quad ii) $\cos\theta \cdot \sec\theta = 1$

iii) $\tan\theta \cdot \cot\theta = 1$ \quad iv) $\tan\theta = \frac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta}$

v) $\cot\theta = \frac{\cos\theta}{\sin\theta}$ \quad vi) $\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta = 1$

vii) $\sec^2\theta = 1 + \tan^2\theta$ \quad viii) $\csc^2\theta = 1 + \cot^2\theta$

ix) $\pi = 180°$ \quad x) length of arc $s = r\theta$

xi) Area of sector $= \frac{1}{2}r\theta$

### 3) Trigonometry Ratios of Standard Angles:

$$\begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
\text{Ratios} & 0° & 30° & 45° & 60° & 90° & 180° & 270° & 360° \\
\hline
\sin & 0 & \frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} & \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} & 1 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\
\hline

### 4) Allied Angles:

1) $\sin(-\theta) = -\sin\theta$ \quad 2) $\sin(90°-\theta) = \cos\theta$

$\cos(-\theta) = \cos\theta$ \quad $\cos(90°-\theta) = \sin\theta$

$\tan(-\theta) = -\tan\theta$ \quad $\tan(90°-\theta) = \cot\theta$

$\cot(-\theta) = -\cot\theta$ \quad $\cot(90°-\theta) = \tan\theta$

$\sec(-\theta) = \sec\theta$ \quad $\sec(90°-\theta) = \csc\theta$

$\csc(-\theta) = -\csc\theta$ \quad $\csc(90°-\theta) = \sec\theta$

### 5) Compound Angles:

i) $\sin(A+B) = \sin A \cos B + \cos A \sin B$

ii) $\sin(A-B) = \sin A \cos B - \cos A \sin B$

iii) $\cos(A+B) = \cos A \cos B - \sin A \sin B$

iv) $\cos(A-B) = \cos A \cos B + \sin A \sin B$

$$\text{v)} \quad \tan(A+B) = \frac{\tan A + \tan B}{1 - \tan A \tan B}$$

$$\text{vi)} \quad \tan(A-B) = \frac{\tan A - \tan B}{1 + \tan A \tan B}$$

### 6) Multiple Angle and Sub Multiple Angle:

$\sin 2A = 2\sin A \cos A$

i) $\sin A = 2\sin\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)\cos\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)$

$\cos 2A = \cos^2 A - \sin^2 A$

ii) $\cos A = \cos^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) - \sin^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)$

iii) $\cos A = 1 - 2\sin^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)$

iv) $\cos A = 2\cos^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) - 1$

$$\text{v)} \quad \cos A = \frac{1 - \tan^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}{1 + \tan^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}$$

$$\text{vi)} \quad \sin A = \frac{2\tan\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}{1 + \tan^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}$$

$$\text{vii)} \quad \tan A = \frac{2\tan\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}{1 - \tan^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}$$

viii) $1 - \cos A = 2\sin^2\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)$

### 7) Triple Angle Formulae:

i) $\sin 3\theta = 3\sin\theta - 4\sin^3\theta$ \quad ii) $\cos 3\theta = 4\cos^3\theta - 3\cos\theta$

$$\text{iii)} \quad \tan 3\theta = \frac{3\tan\theta - \tan^3\theta}{1 - 3\tan^2\theta}$$

$$\text{iv)} \quad \cos^4\theta = \frac{\cos 3\theta + 3\cos\theta}{4} \quad \text{v)} \quad \sin^4\theta = \frac{3\sin\theta - \sin 3\theta}{4}$$

### 8) Factorization Formulae:

$$\text{i.} \quad \sin C + \sin D = 2\sin\left(\frac{C+D}{2}\right)\cos\left(\frac{C-D}{2}\right)$$

$$\text{ii.} \quad \sin C - \sin D = 2\sin\left(\frac{C+D}{2}\right)\sin\left(\frac{C-D}{2}\right)$$

$$\text{iii.} \quad \cos C + \cos D = 2\cos\left(\frac{C+D}{2}\right)\cos\left(\frac{C-D}{2}\right)$$

$$\text{iv.} \quad \cos C - \cos D = 2\sin\left(\frac{C+D}{2}\right)\sin\left(\frac{C-D}{2}\right) \text{ if } C > D$$

$$= 2\sin\left(\frac{C+D}{2}\right)\sin\left(\frac{D-C}{2}\right) \text{ if } D > C$$

### 9) Defactorization formulae:

i. $2\sin A\cos B = \sin(A+B) + \sin(A-B)$

ii. $2\cos A\sin B = \sin(A+B) - \sin(A-B)$

iii. $2\cos A\cos B = \cos(A+B) + \cos(A-B)$

iv. $2\sin A\sin B = \cos(A-B) - \cos(A+B)$

## Co-Ordinate Geometry (2D)

1. Distance Formula: $\sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}$

2. Section Formula: 
$$P(x, y) = \left(\frac{mx_2 + nx_1}{m+n}, \frac{my_2 + ny_1}{m+n}\right) \quad \text{Internally}$$

$$Q(x, y) = \left(\frac{mx_2 - nx_1}{m-n}, \frac{my_2 - ny_1}{m-n}\right) \quad \text{Externally}$$

3. Mid point Formula: $\left(\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2}\right)$

4. Centroid of triangle: $G\left(\frac{x_1 + x_2 + x_3}{3}, \frac{y_1 + y_2 + y_3}{3}\right)$

5. Area of triangle $\Delta = \frac{1}{2}\begin{vmatrix} x_1 & (y_1 - y_3) + x_2 & (y_2 - y_1) \\ & + x_3 & (y_1 - y_2) \end{vmatrix}$

6. Slope of straight line: $m = \tan\theta$

7. One point Form: $y - y_1 = m(x - x_1)$

8. Slope Intercept from: $y = mx + C$

9. Two point Form: $\frac{y - y_1}{x - x_1} = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}$

10. Intercept Form: $\frac{x}{a} + \frac{y}{b} = 1$

11. Normal Form: $x\cos\alpha + y\sin\alpha = p$

12. Slope of line $ax + by + c = 0$ is $\frac{-a}{b}$

13. Intersection of two lines: $L_1 + \lambda L_2 = 0$

14. Angle between two s.t. lines: $\tan\theta = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{1 + m_1 m_2}$

15. If the lines are parallel: $m_1 = m_2$

\quad If the lines are $\perp$: $m_1 \cdot m_2 = -1$

16. Length of $\perp$ form: $\left|\frac{ax_1 + by_1 + c}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}\right|$

17. Bisector Equations: $\frac{a_1 x + b_1 y + c_1}{\sqrt{a_1^2 + b_1^2}} = \pm \frac{a_2 x + b_2 y + c_2}{\sqrt{a_2^2 + b_2^2}}$

18. Distance bet$^n$ parallel lines: $\frac{|c_1 - c_2|}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}$

19. Homogeneous Equation: $ax^2 - 2hxy + by^2 = 0$

$$\tan\theta = \frac{2\sqrt{h^2 - ab}}{a + b}$$

20. General Equation: $ax^2 + 2hxy + by^2 + 2gx + 2fy + c = 0$

\quad represents a pair of lines

If $abc + 2fgh - af^2 - bg^2 - ch^2 = 0$

Point of intersection $(x, y) = \left(\frac{hf - bg}{ab - h^2}, \frac{hg - af}{ab - h^2}\right)$
