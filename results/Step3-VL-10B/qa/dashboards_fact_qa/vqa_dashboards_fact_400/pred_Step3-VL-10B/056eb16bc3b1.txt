To determine the percentage of employees who feel trust is the most important component of an employee - boss relationship, we refer to the "Trust and Reputation" section of the infographic. It states, "1 IN 5 employees feel trust is the most important component of an employee - boss relationship". 

Since 1 in 5 is equivalent to 20% (because \( \frac{1}{5} = 0.2 = 20\% \)), the percentage is 20%.
