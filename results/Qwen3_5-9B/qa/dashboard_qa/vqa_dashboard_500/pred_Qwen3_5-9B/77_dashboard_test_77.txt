Based on the infographic titled **“Unique Ways Pinterest Can Help Your Business”**, there are **five** key ways Pinterest can help businesses:

1. **Creates Backlinks To Your Site**  
   → Pins create backlinks when users repin content, which improves search engine rankings.

2. **Boosts Your Keyword Targeting**  
   → Proper keyword use in pins and descriptions helps search engines rank your content higher.

3. **Boosts Your Social Signals**  
   → Pins generate social signals (likes, shares, repins) that influence Google and other search engine rankings.

4. **Opportunity for New Content**  
   → Repinning and creating new pins allows you to refresh content and stay relevant to your audience.

5. **SEO Image Boost**  
   → Pinterest is image-heavy; optimizing images with keywords and descriptions can improve visibility without being obvious.
