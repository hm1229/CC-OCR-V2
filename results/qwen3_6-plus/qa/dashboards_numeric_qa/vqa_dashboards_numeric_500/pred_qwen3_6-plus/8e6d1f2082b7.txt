According to the infographic, the **industrial sector** accounts for **4%** of American water use.

This is shown in the section titled “POWER, FARMING, AND THE PUBLIC ARE TOP CONSUMERS,” where water usage is broken down by sector:

- Thermoelectric Power: 49%
- Irrigation and Livestock: 32%
- Public and Domestic Supply: 12%
- **Industrial: 4%**
- Mining and Aquaculture: 3%

✅ So, the percentage of water consumption by the industrial sector is **4%**.
