## Free Apps in iPhone and Android Stores Combined

From the infographic:

- **Apple App Store for iPhone**: Free apps = **71%**
- **Google Android Market**: Free apps = **60%**

### Combined Average:
(71% + 60%) / 2 = **65.5%**

> If simply **added together**: 71% + 60% = **131%** (but this is a combined total, not a meaningful average)

The **average percentage of free apps** across both stores is **65.5%**.
