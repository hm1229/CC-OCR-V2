To calculate the **increase in percentage from overall consumers to millennials** when it comes to trying new things while shopping online:

From the infographic:

- **Overall**: 39% of respondents said lockdown encouraged them to try and buy goods online they had not considered before.
- **Millennials**: 52% said the same.

So, the increase is:

> **52% - 39% = 13%**

---

✅ **Answer: 13%**
