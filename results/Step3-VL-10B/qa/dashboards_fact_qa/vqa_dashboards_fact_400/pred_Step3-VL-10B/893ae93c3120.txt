To determine the total number of CAR refugees in Congo and DR Congo, we refer to the "Total population of CAR refugees" box on the right - hand side of the map:  

- For **DR Congo (Democratic Republic of the Congo)**, the number is 56,000.  
- For **Congo (Republic of the Congo)**, the number is 11,000.  

Adding these two values together: \( 56,000 + 11,000 = 67,000 \).  


Thus, the total number of CAR refugees in Congo and DR Congo is 67,000.
