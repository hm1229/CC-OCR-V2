To determine what young people are most concerned about regarding startups apart from funding or economic climate, we look at the "Top three concerns of young people considering starting a business today" section.  

- The first concern is "Accessing start up funding" (48%).  
- The second is "The current economic climate" (43%).  
- The third (and the one apart from the first two) is "Risks of startups" (41%).  

Thus, apart from funding and economic climate, the most concerned issue is **Risks of startups** (41%).
