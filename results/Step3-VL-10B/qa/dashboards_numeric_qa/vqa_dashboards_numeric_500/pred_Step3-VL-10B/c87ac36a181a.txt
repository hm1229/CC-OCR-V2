To determine the percentage of SMBs using Facebook versus Google+ for their business, refer to the **Social Media** section of the infographic:  

- Most valuable social media outlet for businesses:  
  - Facebook: 37%  
  - Google+: 18%  

If the question asks for the *difference* in usage between Facebook and Google+:  
\( 37\% - 18\% = 19\% \)  

Thus, 37% of SMBs use Facebook, 18% use Google+, and Facebook usage is 19 percentage points higher than Google+ usage.
