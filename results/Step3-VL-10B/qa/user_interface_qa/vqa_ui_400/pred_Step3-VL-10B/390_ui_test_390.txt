To determine how long canned dog food can be refrigerated after opening, refer to the "BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD" section of the infographic.  

- For **canned dog food** (the right - hand storage block in that section):  
  - Once opened, store at <80°F (refrigeration typically falls within this range, as refrigerators operate below 80°F).  
  - Once opened, it keeps for **90 days** when stored in a cool, dry place (which includes refrigeration).  


Thus, canned dog food can be refrigerated after opening for 90 days.
