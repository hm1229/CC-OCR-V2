The **Louisiana Purchase** is regarded as one of the greatest real estate deals in history. In 1803, President Thomas Jefferson led the acquisition of the Louisiana Territory from France for $15 million, which doubled the size of the United States overnight. The territory covered approximately 828,000 square miles and included land from the Mississippi River to the Rocky Mountains and from the Gulf of Mexico to the Canadian border. This purchase is considered a monumental deal due to its vast size and strategic importance, especially the Port of New Orleans.
