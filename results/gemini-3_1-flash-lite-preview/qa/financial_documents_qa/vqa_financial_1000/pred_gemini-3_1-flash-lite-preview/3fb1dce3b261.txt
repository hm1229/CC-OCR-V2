This document is an **Audit Agenda** for a "Production & Process Controls" audit conducted at DePuy Orthopaedics, Inc. in Warsaw, IN, from July 13–17, 2009.

The document outlines the following:

*   **Audit Objective:** To assess the company's compliance with 21CFR820 (FDA medical device regulations) and ISO 13485:2003 standards regarding their Quality Management System.
*   **Audit Scope:** 
    *   Evaluating the implementation of the production and process controls subsystem.
    *   Reviewing the effectiveness of previously identified corrective actions.
    *   Clarifying that this is a targeted audit rather than a full assessment of the entire Quality System.
    *   Allowing the company to identify specific areas of concern for the auditors to focus on.

The document is marked as "Confidential" and is noted as page 2 of 5.
