## Sun ☀️

According to the infographic, the **sun** is highlighted as a source of renewable/green energy, under the **"Green Energy"** category with the tip **"Use the sun, open windows."**

The sun is a **renewable energy source**, while bulbs, CFLs, and LEDs are simply types of lighting fixtures that *consume* energy rather than generate it. However, the infographic also suggests **switching to fluorescent (CFL)** bulbs as a more energy-efficient lighting option.
