## Longest Short Route

Looking at the **Short Routes** section, the longest cycling route is the **Inishmore Entire Island Cycle, Aran Islands, Co. Galway**, with a route length of **57 KM / 35.5 MILES**.

The other short routes for comparison:
- Great Western Greenway: 42KM / 26 MILES
- Gap of Dunloe & Black Valley Cycle: 30KM / 20 MILES
- Loop Head Heritage Trail: 30 TO 85KM / 30 TO 50 MILES

Note: The Loop Head Heritage Trail has a variable range (30-85 KM), so at its maximum it could actually be the longest at **85 KM / 50 MILES**.
