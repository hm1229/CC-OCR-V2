### First Problem: Factor the difference of two squares  

Factor the difference of two squares.  

\[
(16x^4 - 81)
\]  

\begin{itemize}  
    \item \((2x + 3)^2(2x - 3)^2\)  
    \item \((4x^2 + 9)(4x^2 + 9)\)  
    \item \((4x^2 + 9)(2x + 3)(2x - 3)\)  
    \item \((4x^2 + 9)(4x^2 - 9)\)  
\end{itemize}  


### Second Problem: Question 8  

Factor using the formula for the sum or difference of two cubes.  

\[
27x^3 + 64
\]  

\begin{itemize}  
    \item \((3x + 4)(9x^2 + 12x + 16)\)  
    \item \((3x + 4)(9x^2 + 16)\)  
    \item \((3x + 4)(9x^2 - 12x + 16)\)  
    \item \((3x - 4)(9x^2 + 12x + 16)\)  
\end{itemize}
