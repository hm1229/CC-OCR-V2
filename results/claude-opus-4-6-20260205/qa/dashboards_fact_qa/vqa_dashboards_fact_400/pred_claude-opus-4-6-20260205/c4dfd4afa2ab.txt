## Longest Time for the Longest Route in Ireland

Looking at the **Very Long Routes** section of the infographic:

- **Kingfisher Cycling Trail, Entire Loop Route, Ireland and Northern Ireland**: 270 KM / 220 MILES with an average duration of **16 HOURS**
- **Wild Atlantic Way Route Map and Guide, Scenic Cycle, West of Ireland**: 7,700KM / 1,700 MILES with an average duration of **61+ HOURS**

The longest route is the **Wild Atlantic Way Route Map and Guide, Scenic Cycle, West of Ireland**, and the longest time taken to complete it is **61+ hours**.
