To find the combined food price change for **fruit** and **vegetables** since 2006, we add their individual percentage increases:

- **Fruit**: 30%
- **Vegetables**: 27.7%

$$
30\% + 27.7\% = 57.7\%
$$

### ✅ Answer: **57.7%**  
This is the total food price change for fruit and vegetables combined since 2006.
