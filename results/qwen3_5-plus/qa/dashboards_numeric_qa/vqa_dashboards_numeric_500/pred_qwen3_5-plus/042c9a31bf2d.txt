Based on the infographic, the long-term benefits of early childhood education are divided into two categories: **Student Success** and **Adult Responsibility**.

**Student Success:**
*   **Graduation:** Students are **29% more likely** to graduate from high school.
*   **Grade Retention:** Students are **44% less likely** to repeat a grade.
*   **Testing:** Students pass standardized tests more frequently.

**Adult Responsibility:**
*   **Employment:** Adults age 40 who had early childhood education are **more likely to be employed**.
*   **Income:** These 40-year-olds have a **33% higher average income**.
*   **Crime:** 18-year-olds *without* early childhood education are 70% more likely to be arrested for a violent crime (implying those with the education are significantly less likely to be arrested).
