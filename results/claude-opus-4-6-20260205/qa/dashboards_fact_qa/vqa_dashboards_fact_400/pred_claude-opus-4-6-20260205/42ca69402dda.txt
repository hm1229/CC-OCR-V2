## Answers from the Infographic: "The Digital Property Boom"

According to the "House Buying On-The-Go" section of the infographic, **31% of new home shoppers use their mobile devices at work**.

The breakdown of locations where new home shoppers use their mobile devices is:
- **77%** - At Home
- **31%** - At Work
- **28%** - Waiting in Line
- **27%** - At A Restaurant
- **26%** - Other People's Homes
