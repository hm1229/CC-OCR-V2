From the infographic, under the section **“What Education Do They Have?”**, we see the educational breakdown for **Business Analysts**:

- **Bachelor’s**: 30%
- **Master’s**: 56%
- **Doctoral**: 11%

To find the percentage of business analysts who **don’t** hold a doctoral degree, subtract the doctoral percentage from 100%:

> **100% - 11% = 89%**

---

✅ **Answer: 89%**
