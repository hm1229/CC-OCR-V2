- **Suicide** accounts for **23.3%** of deaths among 15–24 year olds each year.

These are separate categories under “Leading Causes of Death.” The infographic does not suggest that car accidents cause suicide — rather, it shows that both are leading causes of death in young people, with suicide being the top cause.

So, to clarify:  
> ❌ **0% of suicides are caused by car accidents.**  
> ✅ **Car accidents and suicide are both leading causes of death, but one does not cause the other.**
