The infographic compares **COVID-19 reports for February 2020 in China and the United States**.

Specifically:

- **China**: As of **February 21, 2020**
  - 75,569 reported cases
  - 75,467 (99%) in Chinese mainland
  - 2,239 deaths

- **United States**: As of **February 24, 2020**
  - 14 cases diagnosed through US health care system
  - 39 cases among repatriated US citizens
  - 0 deaths
  - 0 critically ill case-patients
  - No evidence of community-wide transmission

Additionally, it compares **Influenza in the US** (as of February 15, 2020) to highlight the severity of the flu season at that time — with millions of ill patients, hospitalizations, and deaths — contrasting it with the limited spread of COVID-19 outside China as of late February 2020.

So, the countries being compared for **COVID-19** are **China and the United States**.
