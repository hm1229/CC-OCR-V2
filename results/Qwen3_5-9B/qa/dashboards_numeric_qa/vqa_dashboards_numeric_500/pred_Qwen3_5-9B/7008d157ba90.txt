Based on the provided infographic from Ireland’s Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), **84.4%** of Ireland’s bathing waters are classified as having **“Good status”**.

This is shown in the pie chart under “WATER QUALITY %” at the top of the infographic:

- **Blue (Good status)**: 84.4%
- **Green (Sufficient Status)**: 12.6%
- **Orange (Poor Status - Non compliant)**: 3%

Additionally, it notes that **97%** of Ireland’s designated bathing areas meet EU Environment & Health Standards — which includes both “Good” and “Sufficient” status.

So, to directly answer your question:  
> **84.4%** of Ireland’s bathing waters are of good water quality standard.
