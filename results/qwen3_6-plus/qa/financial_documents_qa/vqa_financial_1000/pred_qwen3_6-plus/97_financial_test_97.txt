The sample menu for **Afternoon** is:

> **1 glass milk, hard boiled egg, or piece of cheese**

This is listed under the “Afternoon” heading in the Sample Menu section of the document. It offers a choice among three options — not all three together — as indicated by the word “or”.

---

✅ So, the afternoon snack can be *any one* of:
- 1 glass of milk  
- 1 hard-boiled egg  
- 1 piece of cheese

This aligns with the low-carbohydrate, high-protein/fat focus of the diet plan for children aged 3 to 6 years.
