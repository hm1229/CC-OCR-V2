To determine the percentage of employers bound to step up sanitization measures post - COVID - 19, we refer to the section “THE MAJORITY OF BUSINESSES PLAN TO IMPLEMENT MEASURES TO PROTECT EMPLOYEES AND CUSTOMERS ONCE ONSITE WORK RESUMES”. 

- The 79.0% figure relates to providing personal protective equipment (face masks, gloves, etc.). 
- The 91.2% figure relates to insisting employees stay home if they show signs of illness. 
- The 87.0% figure specifically states that businesses were likely or very likely to increase sanitization in the workplace. 

Thus, the correct percentage is 87.0%.
