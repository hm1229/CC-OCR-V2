## Prescription Pills Leading to Most Deaths in the US

According to the infographic, **prescription pills** (specifically **opioids**, e.g., prescription painkillers or heroin) are responsible for the most deaths in the United States.

The infographic highlights:
- A **500% increase** in overdose deaths involving opioids
- **47,055 deaths** attributed to this cause
- This is compared to **cocaine** and **heroin** deaths, which are listed as lower figures

This is part of the **"Prescription Pills Are Overrunning the People"** section, covering the period **2000-2016**, which emphasizes the **opioid epidemic** as a major crisis in America.
