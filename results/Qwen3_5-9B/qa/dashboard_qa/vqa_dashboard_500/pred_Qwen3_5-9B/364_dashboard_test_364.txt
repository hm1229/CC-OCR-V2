Looking at the options:

- **Binatog** – boiled white corn kernel with coconut shavings → *vegetarian*
- **Mani** – peanuts → *vegetarian*
- **Chicharon** – pork rinds → *non-vegetarian* (made from pork)

✅ So, the only non-veg snack among the three is **Chicharon**.

*(Note: While “Chicharon” can sometimes refer to pork rinds, in the Philippines it’s almost always pork-based — hence non-veg.)*

**Answer: Chicharon**
