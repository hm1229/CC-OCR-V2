The role of a **Data and Analytics Manager** in a company is to **manage a team of analysts and data scientists**. This involves overseeing their work, guiding projects, and ensuring that data-driven insights are effectively delivered to support business decisions.

Key aspects of the role include:
- **Leadership and project management**: Leading teams and managing data science initiatives.
- **Interpersonal communication**: Collaborating with stakeholders across departments.
- **Technical expertise**: Utilizing skills in database systems (SQL and NoSQL), data mining, and predictive modeling to guide analytical strategies.

The mindset associated with this role is described as a "Data Wizard's Cheerleader," indicating a supportive and leadership-oriented approach to empowering data professionals within the organization.
