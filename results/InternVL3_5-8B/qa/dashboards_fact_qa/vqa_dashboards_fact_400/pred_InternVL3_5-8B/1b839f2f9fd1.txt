According to the survey, 60% of small business owners and marketers plan to increase the use of Twitter as part of their online marketing tactics in 2014. This indicates a significant interest in leveraging Twitter for marketing purposes, despite the challenges of time constraints. The survey highlights that while there is a readiness to ramp up online tactics, time management remains a critical issue, with 79% spending less than 2 hours per week on Twitter. This suggests that although Twitter is recognized as a valuable tool, the limited time dedicated to it may hinder its full potential.
