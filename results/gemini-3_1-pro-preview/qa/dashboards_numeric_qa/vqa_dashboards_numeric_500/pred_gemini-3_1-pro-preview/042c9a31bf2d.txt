Based on the infographic, the long-term benefits of early childhood education are categorized into two areas:

**Student Success:**
*   **Higher graduation rates:** Students are 29% more likely to graduate from high school.
*   **Lower grade retention:** Students are 44% less likely to repeat a grade.
*   **Better test performance:** Students pass standardized tests more frequently.

**Adult Responsibility:**
*   **Higher employment rates:** Adults (age 40) are more likely to be employed.
*   **Higher earnings:** These adults have a 33% higher average income.
*   **Lower crime rates:** 18-year-olds without early childhood education are 70% more likely to be arrested for a violent crime, implying that those with it are significantly less likely to be arrested.
