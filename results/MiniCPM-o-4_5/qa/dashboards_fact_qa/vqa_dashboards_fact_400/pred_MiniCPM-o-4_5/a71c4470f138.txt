The question asks: What percentage of Suicides are caused by pedestrian accidents?

This is a trick question. The percentages listed are for the leading causes of death among 15-24 year olds — they are not percentages *of suicides*. Pedestrian accidents are a separate category (2.9% of total deaths), not a cause *within* the suicide category.

Therefore, pedestrian accidents do not cause any percentage of suicides — they are a different cause of death entirely.

Answer: 0%
