## Brazil

According to the infographic, **Brazil** had the most killings of transgender people in Latin America. The data shows that:

- **79%** of killings of transgender people worldwide occurred in **Latin America**
- **50%** of those killings that occurred in Latin America happened in **Brazil**

This means Brazil accounted for half of all transgender killings in the Latin American region, making it the country with the highest number of such killings.
