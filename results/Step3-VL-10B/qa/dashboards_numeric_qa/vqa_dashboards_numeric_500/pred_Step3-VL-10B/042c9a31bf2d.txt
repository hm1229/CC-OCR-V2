The long - term benefits of early childhood education include:  

### Student Success  
- Students with early childhood education are **29% more likely to graduate from high school**.  
- They are **44% less likely to repeat a grade**.  
- They **pass standardized tests more frequently**.    

### Adult Responsibility (when reaching adulthood)  
- Adults age 40 who had early childhood education are **more likely to be employed**.  
- These 40 - year - olds also have a **33% higher average income**.  
- 18 - year - olds *without* early childhood education are **70% more likely to be arrested for a violent crime** (implying early education reduces this risk).
