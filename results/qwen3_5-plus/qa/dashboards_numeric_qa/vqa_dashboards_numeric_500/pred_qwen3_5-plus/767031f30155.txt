Based on the infographic, there are two main emergency benefits provided to working Canadians:

1.  **Emergency Care Benefit:**
    *   **Who it's for:** Individuals who must stay home due to COVID-19 and do not have paid sick leave. This includes people who are sick, cannot access Employment Insurance (EI), or are taking care of a sick child or family member.
    *   **Value:** Up to **$900 bi-weekly** for up to **15 weeks**.

2.  **Emergency Support Benefit:**
    *   **Who it's for:** Workers who are not eligible for EI and are facing unemployment or reduced hours.
    *   **Value:** The plan allocates up to **$5 billion** in total support for this benefit.
