## South Korea's Broadband Penetration Rate

Based on the infographic, South Korea's broadband penetration rate can be identified from the **Country Broadband Penetration Rate** section, where **South Korea is listed at 27.4%** (ranked 9th).

Additionally, for **Mobile Broadband Penetration Rate**, **Europe has 46.3%** as the top region, but South Korea is not separately listed in that section.

However, it's worth noting that South Korea is highlighted prominently throughout the infographic:

- **#1 in average connection speed** at **14.4 megabits per second**
- **First country to complete the conversion from dial-up to broadband** in 2005
- **116,650,000** broadband subscribers (ranked #1 globally)
- Has **5 cities** in the top 100 fastest broadband connections in the world

So the **country broadband penetration rate for South Korea is 27.4%**.
