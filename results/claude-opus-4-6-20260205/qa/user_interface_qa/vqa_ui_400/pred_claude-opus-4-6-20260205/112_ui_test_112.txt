## Disney+ vs AppleTV+ Viewership Comparison

According to the infographic, **14%** say they've watched Disney+ since its launch on 24th March, which is **3x more** (3 times more) than those who have watched AppleTV+ (**4%**) since its launch in November 2019.

This means Disney+ had a **10 percentage point increase** in viewership compared to AppleTV+ (14% - 4% = 10%), or put another way, Disney+ attracted **3.5 times** the viewership of AppleTV+ (14% ÷ 4% = 3.5x).
