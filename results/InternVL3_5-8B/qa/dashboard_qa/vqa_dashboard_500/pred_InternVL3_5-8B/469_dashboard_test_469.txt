According to the infographic, shoppers from the Baby Boomers and Silent Generation are not trying out new online retailers. Specifically:

- **Baby Boomers**: 43% did not try out new online retailers.
- **Silent Generation**: 48% did not try out new online retailers.

In contrast, Gen Z and Millennials showed a higher inclination to try new online retailers, with 32% and 35% respectively indicating they had done so.
