The Indian poet who was a friend of W.B. Yeats was **Rabindranath Tagore**.

As stated in the infographic under the “Our Peoples” section:

> “W.B. Yeats and the celebrated Indian poet Rabindranath Tagore were friends. Busts of Tagore stand in St. Stephen’s Green in Dublin and in Sligo, while a bust of Yeats will be unveiled in New Delhi this year.”

Tagore (1861–1941) was not only a poet but also a playwright, novelist, and composer — and the first non-European to win the Nobel Prize in Literature (1913). His friendship with Yeats helped introduce Indian literature and philosophy to Western audiences.

✅ So, the answer is: **Rabindranath Tagore**.
