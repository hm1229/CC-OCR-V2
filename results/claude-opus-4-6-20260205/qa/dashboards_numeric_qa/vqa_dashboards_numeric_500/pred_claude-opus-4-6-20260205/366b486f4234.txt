## Most Ideal Valentine's Day Scenarios for Age Group 18-29

According to the Hotels.com Romance Survey (2012), respondents aged **18-29** preferred the following scenarios, ranked by percentage:

1. **Exploring a New City** - **33%** (highest preference)
2. **Snuggling by the Fire** - **33%** (tied for highest)
3. **Spending the Night in a Hotel Suite** - **22%**
4. **Dinner at a Romantic Restaurant** - **11%** (lowest preference)

So the 18-29 age group most favored **Exploring a New City** and **Snuggling by the Fire** equally (both at 33%), while **Dinner at a Romantic Restaurant** was the least popular choice at just 11%. This younger demographic showed a clear preference for adventurous or cozy intimate experiences over traditional dining.
