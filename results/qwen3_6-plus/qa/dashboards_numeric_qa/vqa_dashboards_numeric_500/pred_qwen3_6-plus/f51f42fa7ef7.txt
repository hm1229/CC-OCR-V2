Looking at the section **“Shoppers are trying out new online retailers”**:

- **Gen Z**: 32% said **YES** (they tried new online retailers and intend to continue)
- **Millennials**: 35% said **YES**

To find the difference:

> **35% - 32% = 3%**

---

✅ **Answer: The difference is 3 percentage points**, with Millennials slightly more likely than Gen Z to have tried and intend to continue with new online retailers.

*(Note: The infographic also shows that 43% of Baby Boomers and 48% of Silent Generation said “NO”, but the question specifically asks about Gen Z vs. Millennials.)*
