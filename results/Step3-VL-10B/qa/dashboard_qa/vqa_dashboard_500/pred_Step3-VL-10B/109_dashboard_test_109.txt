To determine the percentage of a household's energy bill that is not for lighting, we first find the percentage **for** lighting. In the "Lighting" section of the infographic, it states: *"Lighting accounts for 7% of a typical household's energy bill."*  

To find the percentage **not** for lighting, subtract the lighting percentage from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 7\% = 93\% \)  


Thus, 93% of a household's energy bill is not for lighting.
