The **54 mm Pinnacle acetabular shell** is measured over the Porocoat porous coating.

This is explicitly stated in the caption under the rightmost diagram in Figure 17:

> *A 54 mm Pinnacle acetabular shell is 54 mm in diameter as measured over the Porocoat Porous Coating.*

This distinguishes it from the trial shell and grater, which are measured without accounting for the porous coating layer.
