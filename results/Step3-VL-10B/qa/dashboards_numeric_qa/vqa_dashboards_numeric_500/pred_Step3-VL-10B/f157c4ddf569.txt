To determine which book used vocabulary familiar to six - year - olds, we refer to the “FUN FACTS ABOUT CHILDREN'S CLASSICS” section. For *Cat in the Hat* (by Dr. Suess, 1957), it states: “At the behest of the school publisher, the book was written using only a 236 - word vocabulary that every 6 - year - old should know.” 

Thus, the book is *Cat in the Hat*.
