In 2003, **57.2%** of academic leaders believed that online education was the same or even superior to face-to-face learning.

By 2005 (two years later), this number had increased to **77%**.

To find how many more percentage points this represents:

$$
77\% - 57.2\% = 19.8\%
$$

So, **19.8% more** academic leaders in 2005 believed online education was the same or superior compared to 2003.
