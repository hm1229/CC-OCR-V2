The two good news highlighted as good practices are:

1. **Read to your children**: This emphasizes the importance of reading to children as a significant factor influencing their early educational success. Being read to at home before starting school introduces children to books and fosters a love for reading.

2. **Be a reading role model**: This highlights the impact of adults who read for pleasure on children. Children are more likely to value reading as a worthwhile activity if they see adults enjoying books.

These practices are supported by research from the National Commission on Reading (1985) and Csíkszentmihályi (1991).
