Once opened, **dry dog food** can be stored for a longer time compared to canned dog food.

According to the infographic:

- **Canned dog food**: Once opened, it should be refrigerated and kept for **3 days**.
- **Dry dog food**: Once opened, it can be stored in a cool, dry place for up to **90 days**.

Therefore, **dry dog food** has a significantly longer storage life after opening.
