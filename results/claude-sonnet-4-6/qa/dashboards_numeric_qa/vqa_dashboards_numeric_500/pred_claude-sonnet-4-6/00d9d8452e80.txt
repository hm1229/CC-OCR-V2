## The Pitch

The area between the two sets of wickets on a cricket ground is called the **"pitch"** (also sometimes referred to as the **"strip"** or **"track"**).

As mentioned in this infographic, **a good Test match pitch starts with 32% moisture content**. The pitch is a carefully prepared surface, typically:

- **22 yards (20.12 metres)** long
- Made of closely mown and rolled **clay/grass**
- Critical to the outcome of the match, as its condition affects how the ball behaves for both **batters and bowlers**
