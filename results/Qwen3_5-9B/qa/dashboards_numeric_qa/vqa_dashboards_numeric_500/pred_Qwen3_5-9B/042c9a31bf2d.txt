Based on the infographic, the **long-term benefits** of early childhood education are categorized into two main areas:

### 1. Student Success
*   **Higher High School Graduation Rate:** Students with early childhood education are **29% more likely** to graduate from high school.
*   **Lower Grade Repetition:** They are **44% less likely** to repeat a grade.
*   **Better Test Performance:** They pass standardized tests more frequently.

### 2. Adult Responsibility
*   **Higher Employment Likelihood:** Adults age 40 who had early childhood education are **more likely to be employed**.
*   **Higher Income:** These same 40-year-olds have a **33% higher average income**.
*   **Lower Crime Rates:** 18-year-olds *without* early childhood education are **70% more likely** to be arrested for a violent crime (implying those *with* it are significantly less likely).

In summary, early childhood education leads to better academic outcomes in school and greater economic stability and lower crime rates in adulthood.
