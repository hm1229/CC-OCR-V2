To determine the percentage of U.S. students with "hardly any" access to digital technologies **at school**, refer to the "ACCESS TO INTERNET & DIGITAL TECHNOLOGIES" section of the infographic.  

- Locate the "In School" column (right - hand side of that section).  
- Identify the "Hardly any" category in the legend (the lightest - colored bar for "In School").  
- Check the numerical label for that bar: it shows **5%**.  


Thus, 5% of U.S. students have hardly any access to digital technologies at school.
