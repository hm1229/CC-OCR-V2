According to the text, **table salt contains 40 percent sodium**.

The article explains that table salt is sodium chloride, and while sodium is an element, table salt is a compound. Of the two elements in sodium chloride (sodium and chlorine), sodium makes up 40% by weight.

> “Table salt contains 40 percent sodium and is one of many sodium sources in the diet.”

This is why even small amounts of salt can contribute significantly to dietary sodium intake.
