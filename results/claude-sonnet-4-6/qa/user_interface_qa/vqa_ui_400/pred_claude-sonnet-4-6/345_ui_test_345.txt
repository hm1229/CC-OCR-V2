## Battle of Blore Heath (1459) - Strength Comparison

**Lancastrians** had more strength with:
- **Men: 8,000 - 14,000**

Compared to the **Yorkists** who had:
- **Men: 3,000 - 5,000**

The **Lancastrians had roughly 2-3 times more men** than the Yorkists. However, despite their numerical superiority, the Lancastrians were **defeated** by the Yorkists in this battle.
