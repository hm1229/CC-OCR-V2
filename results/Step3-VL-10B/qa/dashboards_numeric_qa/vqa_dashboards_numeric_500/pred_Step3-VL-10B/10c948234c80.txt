To determine the expected rise in bird deaths due to wind turbines by 2030, we analyze the data from the infographic:  

- **Current annual bird deaths from wind turbines**: 440,000 (stated as “440,000 birds are killed by wind turbines each year”).  
- **Projected annual bird deaths from wind turbines by 2030**: Exceeds 1,000,000 (stated as “By 2030, that number will exceed 1 MILLION”).  

To find the rise, subtract the current figure from the projected figure:  
\( 1,000,000 - 440,000 = 560,000 \)
