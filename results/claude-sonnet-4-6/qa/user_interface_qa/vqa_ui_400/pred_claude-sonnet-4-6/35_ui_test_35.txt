## Calculating Household Expenditure

Based on the **Average Annual Energy Expenditures per Household** data in the infographic, I'll add up the relevant categories:

| Category | Annual Cost |
|----------|-------------|
| **Other (Lighting & Electricity)** | $827 |
| **Refrigerators** | $153 |
| **Water Heating** | $280 |
| **Total** | **$1,260** |

So a household with **lighting, a refrigerator, and a water heater** would incur an average annual expenditure of **$1,260**.
