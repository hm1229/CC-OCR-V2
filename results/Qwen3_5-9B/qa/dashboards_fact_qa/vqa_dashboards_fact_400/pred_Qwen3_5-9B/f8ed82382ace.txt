Based on the infographic, under the section **“REASONS GIVEN FOR NOT HAVING A BUSINESS WEBSITE”**, the following percentages are listed:

- **9%** say it’s **“Too complex”**
- **4%** say the **“Business will never need one”**

Adding these together:

**9% + 4% = 13%**

✅ So, **13%** of respondents think that having a website is too complex and/or that their business will never need one.
