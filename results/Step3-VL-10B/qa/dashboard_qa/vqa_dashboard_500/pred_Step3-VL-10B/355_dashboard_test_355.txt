To determine the number of barbecue items using various parts of a chicken, we analyze the **INIHAW (Barbecued)** section:  

1. **Isaw** – (chicken intestines) ✔️  
2. **Betamax** – (dried pork/chicken blood) ✔️ (includes chicken blood, a chicken part)  
3. **Adidas** – (chicken feet) ✔️  
4. **Helmet** – (chicken heads) ✔️  

Items like *Pork BBQ* (pork) and *Pig skin* (pig) do not use chicken parts.  

Thus, there are **4** barbecue items prepared using various parts of a chicken.
