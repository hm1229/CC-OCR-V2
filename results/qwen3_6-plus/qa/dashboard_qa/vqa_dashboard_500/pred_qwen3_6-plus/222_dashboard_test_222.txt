To find the **difference between total cases and active cases in India** as of August 11, 2020 (from the provided table):

- **Total cases in India** = **22,68,675**
- **Active cases in India** = **6,39,929**

Now subtract:

> **22,68,675 - 6,39,929 = 16,28,746**

---

✅ **Answer: 16,28,746**

This difference represents the number of **recovered + deceased** cases in India at that time.

*(Note: In standard COVID-19 reporting, Total Cases = Active + Recovered + Deceased. So this difference is not just recovered — it’s recovered plus deaths.)*

From the table:
- Deaths in India = 45,257
- So, Recovered = Total - Active - Deaths = 22,68,675 - 6,39,929 - 45,257 = **15,83,489**

But since the question only asks for **Total - Active**, we stick with:

> **16,28,746**

---

✅ Final Answer: **16,28,746**
