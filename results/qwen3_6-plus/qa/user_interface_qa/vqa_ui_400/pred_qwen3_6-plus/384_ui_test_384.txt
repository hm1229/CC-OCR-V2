Based on the infographic’s section **“BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD”**, here’s the comparison:

### 🥫 **Canned Dog Food (once opened):**
- Store at: **< 40°F** (refrigerated)
- Keeps for: **3 days**

### 🐶 **Dry Dog Food (once opened):**
- Store at: **< 80°F** (cool, dry place)
- Keeps for: **90 days**

---

✅ **Answer: Dry dog food can be stored for a much longer time once opened — up to 90 days, compared to only 3 days for canned dog food.**

This is because dry kibble has low moisture content and is less prone to bacterial growth when stored properly, while opened canned food is moist and perishable, requiring refrigeration and quick consumption.

*(Note: Always follow manufacturer instructions and check for signs of spoilage regardless of type.)*
