Based on the infographic, **online recruitment platforms** are the most popular job search method at **26%**, making them the "best" or most widely used technique according to the data.

However, it's worth noting that **41% of respondents say they use ALL these methods** combined, suggesting that using multiple approaches together is actually the most effective strategy. The infographic shows that job seekers use a mix of:

- Online recruitment platforms (26%)
- Personal research & contacts (11%)
- Recruitment agencies (9%)
- Traditional media (7%)
- Social media (6%)

So while online platforms lead individually, a diversified approach appears to be the true "best" method according to this 2013 data.
