This is a **DISCIPLINE REVIEW LETTER** from the U.S. Department of Health & Human Services, Public Health Service, Food and Drug Administration (FDA).

Specifically, it is a regulatory correspondence regarding:
- **NDA 21-073/S-026** (a supplemental new drug application)
- For **Actos® (pioglitazone HCI)** tablets in 15 mg, 30 mg, and 45 mg doses
- Submitted by **Takeda Global Research & Development Center, Inc.**

The letter addresses the FDA's review of clinical data and proposed labeling revisions, specifically discussing the **PROactive trial** and the appropriate placement of information in the drug labeling (moving information from Clinical Studies to Adverse Reactions section). It explains why secondary endpoints from a failed primary endpoint trial cannot be included in the labeling.

The document is marked as **"Confidential – Subject to Protective Order"** and was received by the Regulatory Affairs Department on **February 19, 2007**.
