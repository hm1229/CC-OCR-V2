Based on the infographic, **VMware Enterprise** allows for the highest number of guest machines (virtual SMP guests) with a maximum of **32**.

Here's the relevant section from the "VMware vSphere vs. Microsoft Hyper-V" comparison table:

| Feature | VMware Standard | VMware Enterprise | Microsoft Ent. Plus | Microsoft Standard | Microsoft Ent. | Microsoft DC |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|---------------------|--------------------|----------------|--------------|
| Max virtual SMP (guest) | 8 | **32** | 32 | 4 | 4 | 4 |

So, **VMware Enterprise** supports up to **32** virtual SMP guest machines, which is the highest among the VMware options listed.
