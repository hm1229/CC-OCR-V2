Based on the infographic, **Limerence** is described as having "A primitive instinct."

Limerence is phase 2 of the three stages of love, characterized by:
- Infatuation
- An increase in dopamine, which motivates you to pursue a reward
- Decreased serotonin, which can sometimes cause obsession
- Testosterone decreases in men and increases in women (which may help men focus sexual interest)
