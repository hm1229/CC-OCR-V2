To find the combined unemployment rate of tertiary and national education levels, we first identify their respective unemployment rates from the "Unemployment Rates by Education Level (%)" bar chart:  

- Tertiary education level: 11.2%  
- National average: 4.2%  

Adding these two values together: \( 11.2 + 4.2 = 15.4 \)  


The combined unemployment rate of tertiary and national education levels is 15.4%.
