## South Korea 🇰🇷

According to the infographic, **South Korea** has the highest broadband speed in Asia at **27 Mbps** in Q2 2016, making it the "Speed Demon" — described as "the winner by a mile."

This is significantly higher than the next top performers:
- **Japan**: 19.5 Mbps
- **Taiwan**: 15.6 Mbps
- **Singapore**: 17.2 Mbps
- **Hong Kong**: 17.1 Mbps
