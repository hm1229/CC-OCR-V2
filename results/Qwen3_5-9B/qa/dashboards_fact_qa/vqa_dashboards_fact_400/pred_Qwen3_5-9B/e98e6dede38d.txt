According to the survey conducted in 2012, **26%** of consumers in South Africa used a **smartphone or smartphone app** for internet banking.

This is shown in the “DEVICES USED” section of the infographic, where:

- Personal laptop or computer: 97%
- **Cellphone or smartphone app: 26%**
- Public computer: 14%
- Tablet: 10%

*(Note: The 97% for laptops/computers likely includes desktops and laptops combined, and may overlap with other device categories — it’s not meant to sum to 100%.)*

✅ So, the answer is: **26%**
