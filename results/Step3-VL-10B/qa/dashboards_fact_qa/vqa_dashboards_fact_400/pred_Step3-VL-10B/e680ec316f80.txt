To determine which platform takes longer to master among iPhone, Java ME, and Windows Mobile, we analyze the **"Learning Curve"** section of the infographic.  

- The learning curve shows the time (in months) required for developers to master each platform.  
- The x - axis represents “Months,” and the y - axis lists platforms.  
- For the three platforms in question:  
  - **iPhone**: Its learning curve reaches a certain milestone at a relatively early month (shorter time).  
  - **Java ME**: Its learning curve extends further than iPhone but not as far as Windows Mobile.  
  - **Windows Mobile**: Its learning curve is the longest among the three, meaning it takes the most time to master.  


Thus, **Windows Mobile** takes longer time to master compared to iPhone and Java ME.
