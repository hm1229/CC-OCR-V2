### Clarification:
While “fishballs” and “calamares” are also fried street foods shown in the same section, they are distinct items:
- **Fishballs** = made from fish paste
- **Calamares** = fried squid rings
- **Kwek-kwek / Tokneneng** = battered and deep-fried quail eggs (sometimes chicken eggs), often orange-colored due to annatto dye

In some regions or contexts, “tokneneng” may refer specifically to the version made with chicken eggs, while “kwek-kwek” refers to quail egg version — but in this infographic, they’re presented as synonymous.
