For Barnyard Grass in the N.C. COLORADO DISTRICT, the *option* (post-emergent control, separate from the standard Roneet preplant treatment) is:
> Post application with Betanal plus Betanal 475, with **CITOWETT PLUS surfactant (1 oz. per 10 gal. mix)**, applied *when the grass has 2 leaves or is smaller*.
