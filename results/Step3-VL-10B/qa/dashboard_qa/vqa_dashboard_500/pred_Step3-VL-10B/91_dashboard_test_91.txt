To determine the percentage of respondents who stopped hiring new staff, we analyze the section on staff hiring in the infographic.  

- The data shows **8% of respondents are “continuing to hire new staff”**.  
- To find the percentage of those who *stopped* hiring new staff, we subtract the “continuing to hire” percentage from 100% (since all respondents fall into one of these categories):  
  \( 100\% - 8\% = 92\% \).  


Thus, 92% of respondents stopped hiring new staff.
