According to the infographic **“Reading Like a Sleuth”**, **close reading** promotes critical thinking.

Specifically, under the section **“Understanding Close Reading”**, it states:

> ▶ It promotes **CRITICAL THINKING**, conversation, and understanding.

---

### Why?
Close reading encourages students to:
- Reread texts
- Focus on details
- Ask questions
- Pay attention to language and syntax
- Uncover deeper meaning

These habits of mind are foundational to critical thinking — analyzing, evaluating, and interpreting text rather than just skimming or accepting surface-level meaning.

---

✅ So, the direct answer is:  
> **Close reading** promotes critical thinking.

*(As stated in the infographic.)*
