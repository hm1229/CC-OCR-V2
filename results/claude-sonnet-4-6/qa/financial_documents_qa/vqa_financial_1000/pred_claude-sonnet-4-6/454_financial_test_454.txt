This is a **Western Union Telegram**, as indicated by the header at the top of the document. It was sent on **December 30, 1961**, from Houston, Texas, addressed to **Dr. Roger Adams** at the **Department of Chemistry, University of Illinois, Urbana, Illinois**.

The telegram was sent by **Mark Edwin Andrews**, informing Dr. Adams that his father had "died quietly Friday morning" and expressing appreciation for the "great help and inspiration" Dr. Adams provided as a **Trustee of the Welch Foundation**.
