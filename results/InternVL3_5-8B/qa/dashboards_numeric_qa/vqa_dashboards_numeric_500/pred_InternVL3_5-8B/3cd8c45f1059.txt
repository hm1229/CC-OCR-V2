According to the infographic, shoppers from Gen Z and Millennials are trying out new online retailers. Specifically:

- **Gen Z**: 32% said yes to trying out new online retailers.
- **Millennials**: 35% said yes to trying out new online retailers.

In contrast, Baby Boomers and the Silent Generation showed less interest:

- **Baby Boomers**: 43% said no to trying out new online retailers.
- **Silent Generation**: 48% said no to trying out new online retailers.

This indicates a higher inclination among younger generations to explore new online shopping options during the pandemic.
