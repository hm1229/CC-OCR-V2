I need to find the number of unemployed people with disabilities in the UK based on the infographic.

From the "6 million" section, it states:
- There are over **6 million** disabled people of working age in the UK
- Only about **3.2 million** are currently in employment

To find the number of unemployed disabled people:
6 million - 3.2 million = **2.8 million**

Therefore, approximately **2.8 million** disabled people of working age in the UK are unemployed.
