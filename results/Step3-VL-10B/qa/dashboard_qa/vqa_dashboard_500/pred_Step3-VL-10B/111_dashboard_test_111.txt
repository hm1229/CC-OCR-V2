To find the inverse percentage of people who use their phone while driving, first identify the percentage of drivers who do use their phones while driving. From the infographic, it states that **60% of drivers use phones while driving**.  

The "inverse percentage" (meaning the percentage of drivers who *do not* use their phones while driving) is calculated as:  
\( 100\% - 60\% = 40\% \)  


So, the inverse percentage of people who use their phone while driving is 40%.
