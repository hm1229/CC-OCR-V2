## Phases of Matter in Chemistry

According to **The Map of Chemistry**, the different phases of matter are:

1. 🧊 **Solid**
2. 💧 **Liquid**
3. 💨 **Gas**
4. ✨ **Crystal**

### Additional Notes from the Map:
- The phases section also mentions **mixtures** (like cake and air as examples)
- There are references to **low pressure/high temperature** and **high pressure/low temperature** conditions affecting phases
- A **unit cell** is shown in relation to crystal structure

These phases are shown in the **"Phases"** section of the map, which falls under the broader **Rules of Chemistry** category.
