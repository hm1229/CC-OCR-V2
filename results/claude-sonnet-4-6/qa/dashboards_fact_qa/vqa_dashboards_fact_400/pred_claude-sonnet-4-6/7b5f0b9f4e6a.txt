## Most Expensive Stadium to Build

Based on the infographic, **Cape Town Stadium** was the most expensive to build among the five featured stadiums, with a cost of **US $776.4m**.

Here's a comparison of all five stadium costs:

| Stadium | Cost |
|---------|------|
| **Cape Town Stadium** | **$776.4m** |
| Moses Mabhida Stadium | $642.5m |
| Nelson Mandela Bay | $281.1m |
| Peter Mokaba | $160.6m |
| Mbombela | $147.2m |
