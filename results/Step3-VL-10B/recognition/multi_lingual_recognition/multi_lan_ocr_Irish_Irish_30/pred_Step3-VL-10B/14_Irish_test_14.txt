STAIR An Béal Bocht

Nuair a tháinig sé amach i ndeiireadh na dála tógadh ceann de i gcéin is i gcóngar. Scrióbdh leírmheasanna air go forleathan,¹⁰ iad uile nach mór moltach. Ba mhó ná sásta a bhi a n-túdar ar ndóigh:  
I am rather pleased at the reception given to my book, ‘An Béal Bocht’. It is gratifying to know that an important work of literature receives in this country the recognition that is its due. Scholars, students, men-about-town, clerics, T.D.s, ladies of fashion, and even the better-class corner-boys have vied with one another in grabbing the copies as they pour from the giant presses. How long will the strictly limited edition of 50,000 copies last? A week? A month? Who can tell? Sufficient it to say that you cannot order your copy too soon. Paper difficulties make it doubtful whether another edition of 50,000 will be possible, in our own generation at any rate . . . About the book itself, it is indeed, fine stuff. It is the first thing of the kind ever published. You can look out any day now for a deluge of imitations. Refuse all substitutes. Every genuine copy bears the name, ‘Myles na gCopalein’. (CLn 12/12/1941)

É.’. Bhi sé de dhánaiocht sa Nuallánach bunús an leírmheas seo a chur i gcló in 2 Aibreán 1942. B’fhollas gur bhaithn é ardsasamh as agus gur dhíol sé cuid den chomhar leis an leírmheastóir¹³ ina fhonóta ‘léannta’ agus lena chuid siceanna:  
Is fada mé ag feitheimh leis an leírmheas so thios. Bhi gach dóchas beagnach caillte leis agam. Acht anois tá sé ar fáil ó ‘D. Litt. Celt.’ is ulig.  

Ag Féachaint Cliathánach  
Ní amhras ná gur bhaist ughdar an leabhar seo thuas a leabhar féin maith go leor: is feara dúinn gan tabhairt fá n-a bhréagnughadh, acht d’fhág sé aon nid mahain gan luadh – do b’ é ba chóir do a rádho droch-ségal ar an droch-shaoighal, breacaithe ndroch-Ghaedhlig!  
Ná measadh én nár léigheas-a An Béal Bocht, mar do léigheas, agus san le mó-chúram, gach aon fhocal de – mar phionós! Do chairteas im oige róinnt bhliadhanta thar leag, agus tharla dham, trath, i gcuirsaí an tsaoghal, siubhal trnasna tanús (sic) (monarcha súdaireachta) agus lá a eile trasna monarchach ceimice – do bhi bóthar príobháideach in ngach áit aca: fágaím le h-uadhacht go raibh orm rith trasna ar mo dhicheall (sic) agus greim daingean agam ar pholláiribh mo choimnne, * sara dtuitinn leis an mbalaithe. Ní mór ná gur bh’ é an dáelta céadna agam é agus mé ag léigheamh id taoith na muice bréine sin insan Bhéal Bocht!
