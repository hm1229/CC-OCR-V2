This is **Ban Ki-moon's *Global Education First Initiative***, it has 3 core priority pillars (official initiatives), plus a supporting financing push to deliver these goals:
---
### 1. Put every child in school
It targets getting the 61 million out-of-school primary-aged children (32 million of whom are girls, over 28 million in conflict/poor affected regions) access to school, via:
- Eliminating cost barriers to school attendance
- Providing nutritional/health/social support for vulnerable children
- Closing the gender gap at all education levels
- Building 4 million more classrooms, especially in rural/conflict zones
- Doubling (or more) the percentage of humanitarian aid allocated to education

### 2. Improve the quality of learning
It addresses the issue that 250 million children cannot read, write or count well even after at least 4 years of schooling, via:
- Adding 2 million well-trained teachers with effective student performance evaluation measures
- Improving traditional and digital learning materials in students' home languages
- Increasing early childhood education programs by 45% to prepare children for primary school
- Aligning school education with required workforce skills to improve adult learning outcomes

### 3. Foster global citizenship
It is designed to build shared values to address interconnected global challenges, via:
- Promoting peace, tolerance and respect for cross-cultural diversity through education
- Cultivating community participation, active civic engagement and environmental stewardship
- Training teachers to use innovative, participatory and non-formal teaching and learning methods
---
### Supporting initiative: Close the education financial gap
To fund the above goals, the initiative also pushes to:
- Generate sufficient education funding via global advocacy
- Add *$24 billion per year* to global investment in low-income country school systems
- Allocate at least 5-6% of national GDP to education
- Ensure effective, accountable use of education resources aligned to national education plans
