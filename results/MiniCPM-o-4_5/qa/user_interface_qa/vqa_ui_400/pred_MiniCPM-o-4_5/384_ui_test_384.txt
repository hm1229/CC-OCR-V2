Looking at the section "BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD":

- For canned dog food (represented by the can icon): "ONCE OPENED, KEEPS FOR: 3 DAYS (REFRIGERATED)"
- For dry dog food (represented by the bag icon): "ONCE OPENED, STORE AT: <80°F ... ONCE OPENED, KEEPS FOR: 90 DAYS (STORE IN A COOL, DRY PLACE)"

Comparing the two, dry dog food can be stored for 90 days once opened, while canned dog food lasts only 3 days once opened.
