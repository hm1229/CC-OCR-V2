Based on the infographic, **canned dog food** has a higher protein content than dry pet food.

Here’s the breakdown from the section “HOW TODAY’S PET FOODS MEASURE UP (OR DON’T)”:

- **Dry Pet Food**: Protein range is **18–32%**
- **Canned Food**: Protein range is **28–50%**

Even at the low end of canned food (28%), it exceeds the high end of dry food (32% is the max for dry, but 28% is already higher than 18–32%’s average). More importantly, the *maximum* protein in canned food (50%) is significantly higher than the maximum in dry food (32%).

✅ So, **canned food** generally offers a higher protein content.
