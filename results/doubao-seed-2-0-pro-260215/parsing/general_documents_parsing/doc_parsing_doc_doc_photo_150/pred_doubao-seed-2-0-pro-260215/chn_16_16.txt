\subsection*{7.6 Limitation on our right to terminate the contract}
The contract termination right agreed in the preceding paragraph shall be extinguished if it is not exercised within 30 days from the date we know of the cause for termination. If more than 2 years have elapsed from the date of establishment of this contract, we shall not terminate the contract; if an insured event occurs, we shall bear the liability for paying insurance benefits.

\subsection*{7.7 Age and gender error}
When applying for insurance, the applicant shall fill in the date of birth and gender of the insured consistent with valid identity documents on the insurance application form. If there is an error, it shall be handled in accordance with the following methods:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\arabic*)]
\item If the age of the insured declared by the applicant is untrue, and his/her actual age does not meet the insurable age limit we stipulated, we have the right to terminate the insurance liability for the insured, but refund the cash value of this contract corresponding to the insured at the time of termination of the insurance liability to the applicant. For insured events that occur before the termination of the insurance liability for the insured, we shall not bear the liability for paying insurance benefits. The above-mentioned right to terminate the insurance liability for the insured is subject to the provisions of "Limitation on our right to terminate the contract";
\item If the age or gender of the insured declared by the applicant is untrue, so that the insurance premium actually paid by the applicant is less than the insurance premium payable corresponding to the insured, we have the right to correct and require the applicant to pay the supplementary insurance premium. If an insured event has occurred, the insurance benefit shall be paid in proportion to the ratio of the actually paid insurance premium to the payable insurance premium corresponding to the insured;
\item If the age or gender of the insured declared by the applicant is untrue, so that the insurance premium actually paid by the applicant is more than the insurance premium payable corresponding to the insured, we will refund the overpaid insurance premium to the applicant without interest.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{7.8 Change of the insured}
If the applicant needs to add insureds due to changes in group members, he/she shall notify us in writing. After our review and consent, we will charge the corresponding insurance premium. We shall bear the insurance liability for the newly added insured from 0:00 on the start date of the insurance liability for the newly added insured as agreed by both parties.
If the applicant needs to reduce the number of insureds, he/she shall notify us in writing, and we shall handle the reduced insureds in the following ways respectively:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\arabic*)]
\item If the applicant applies to us for continuing to bear the insurance liability for the reduced insured in accordance with the agreement of this contract, and we agree to the application after review, the insurance liability for the insured shall continue to be valid;
\item If the applicant applies to us for terminating the insurance liability for the reduced insured, our insurance liability for the insured shall be terminated from the time we receive the written notice from the applicant. If the insurance liability termination date specified by the applicant in the notice is later than the date when the notice is delivered to us, our insurance liability for the insured shall be terminated from 0:00 on the insurance liability termination date specified in the notice. Unless otherwise agreed, we will refund the cash value of this contract corresponding to the insured at the time of termination of the insurance liability to the applicant.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{7.9 Unpaid amount}
When we pay insurance benefits, refund cash value or refund insurance premiums, if the applicant has unpaid insurance premiums or other unpaid amounts, we will make the payment after deducting the above-mentioned unpaid amounts and the payable interest accrued.

\subsection*{7.10 Change of contract content}
Within the validity period of this contract, the applicant and we may change the relevant contents of this contract after consultation. To change this contract, we shall issue an endorsement on the insurance policy, or attach a rider, or the applicant and we shall conclude a written change agreement.
