Based on the infographic's section titled "Ways we search for jobs...", there isn't one single "best" method, but rather a hierarchy of popularity:

*   **Online recruitment platforms** are the most popular single method, used by **26%** of people.
*   However, the infographic highlights that the most common approach is actually a combination of everything: **41%** of people say they use **ALL these methods** (including personal research, recruitment agencies, traditional media, and social media).

So, while online platforms are the top individual tool, using a mix of all available techniques is the most prevalent strategy.
