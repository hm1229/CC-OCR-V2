## Expected Rise in Bird Deaths Due to Wind Turbines

**Current figure:** 440,000 birds killed per year

**Expected figure by 2030:** More than 1,000,000 (1 million) birds per year

**Expected rise:** More than **560,000** additional bird deaths per year (1,000,000 - 440,000 = 560,000)

This represents an increase of more than **127%** from the current figure.
