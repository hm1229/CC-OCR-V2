The infographic mentions **seven** components of a "biologically appropriate raw food" diet for dogs, as proposed by Australian veterinarian Ian Billinghurst. These are:

1. Muscle meat (on bone)  
2. Bones (whole or ground)  
3. Organ meats (liver, kidney)  
4. Raw eggs  
5. Veggies (broccoli, spinach, celery)  
6. Apples or other fruits  
7. Some dairy, such as yogurt  

So, **7** items are listed as part of this raw food diet.
