\textbf{III. CALCULATIONS:}

Calculating the volume of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

\[
V_b = a \cdot b \cdot c
\]

\[
V_b \text{- volume of examined bodies } [\mathrm{cm}^3],\ 
a \text{- width of examined bodies } [\mathrm{cm}],\ 
b \text{- length of examined bodies } [\mathrm{cm}],\ 
c \text{- height of examined bodies } [\mathrm{cm}].
\]

\[
V_b = 2{,}5\ [\mathrm{cm}] \cdot 4{,}2\ [\mathrm{cm}] \cdot 1{,}5\ [\mathrm{cm}] = 15{,}75\ [\mathrm{cm}^3]
\]

Calculating the material density of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

\[
\delta_b = \frac{m_b}{V_b}
\]

\[
\delta_b \text{- material density of given body } [\mathrm{g/cm}^3],\ 
m_b \text{- mass of given body } [\mathrm{g}],\ 
V_b \text{- volume of examined body}
\]

\[
\delta_b \text{ (ebonite)} = \frac{22{,}7}{15{,}75}\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}44127\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1441{,}27\ [\mathrm{kg/m}^3]
\]

\[
\delta_b \text{ (bakelite)} = \frac{19{,}8}{15{,}75}\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}25714\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1257{,}14\ [\mathrm{kg/m}^3]
\]

\[
\delta_b \text{ (aluminium (10))} = \frac{42{,}6}{15{,}75}\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}70476\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 2704{,}76\ [\mathrm{kg/m}^3]
\]

Calculating the material density using areometers based on the formula:

\[
\delta_b' = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{m_3 - m_2}\,\delta_w
\]

\[
\delta_b' \text{- material density of given body using areometer } [\mathrm{g/cm}^3],\ 
m_n \text{- values of mass from experiment } [\mathrm{g}],\ 
\delta_w \text{- density of distilled water } [\mathrm{g/cm}^3].
\]

\[
\delta_b' \text{ (ebonite)} = \frac{110 - 87}{103 - 87}\ [\mathrm{g}] \cdot 1\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3]
= \frac{23}{16}\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}4375\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1437{,}5\ [\mathrm{kg/m}^3]
\]

\[
\delta_b' \text{ (bakelite)} = \frac{110 - 90}{105 - 90}\ [\mathrm{g}] \cdot 1\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3]
= \frac{20}{15}\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}33333\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 1333{,}33\ [\mathrm{kg/m}^3]
\]

\[
\delta_b' \text{ (aluminium (10))} = \frac{110 - 67}{83 - 67}\ [\mathrm{g}] \cdot 1\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3]
= \frac{43}{16}\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}6875\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 2687{,}5\ [\mathrm{kg/m}^3]
\]

\[
\delta_b' \text{ (aluminium (11))} = \frac{110 - 86}{94 - 86}\ [\mathrm{g}] \cdot 1\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3]
= \frac{24}{8}\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 3\ [\mathrm{g/cm}^3] = 3000\ [\mathrm{kg/m}^3]
\]

\[
