## Most Common Cause of Shark Attack Incidents

According to the **"What was victim doing?"** section of the infographic, **Surfing/Skiing** was the most common activity during shark attack incidents, with **609 reported cases**.

This was followed by:
1. 🏄 **Surfing/Skiing** - 609
2. 🏊 **Swimming** - 499
3. 🎣 **Fishing** - 150
4. 🤿 **All Other** - 128
5. 🔱 **Spearfishing** - 85

This suggests that **surfers and skiers** are most frequently involved in shark incidents, likely because their activity on the water surface can mimic the movement of prey from a shark's perspective.
