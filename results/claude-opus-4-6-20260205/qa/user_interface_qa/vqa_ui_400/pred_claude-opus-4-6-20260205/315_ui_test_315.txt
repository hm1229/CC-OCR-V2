## Support Provided by Torfaen Council for Children with Additional Learning Needs

According to the infographic, Torfaen County Borough Council provided **1,000 days of support** for children with additional learning needs during the Covid-19 crisis (from March to 12 June 2020).

Additionally, to support **digitally excluded pupils at home**, the council made available:

- **1,200 laptops**
- **550 MiFi devices**

These measures were part of the council's broader response to ensure that children with additional learning needs continued to receive support and could access education remotely during the pandemic.
