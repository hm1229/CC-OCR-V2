Looking at the **Decentralised** section on the left side of the infographic:

There are **4 numbered steps** shown:

- **Step 1**: The app broadcasts a random ID and collects random IDs of other apps it interacts with, plus interaction data (time and distance).
- **Step 2**: If a user reports being unwell, they can choose to upload their ID to a central database.
- **Step 3**: Users’ phones regularly download the latest version of the database and look for IDs they’ve interacted with.
- **Step 4**: If they see any matching ID(s), their app calculates whether to inform them they’ve been in contact with an infected person.

✅ So, the answer is: **4 steps**.
