\section*{III. CALCULATIONS:}

Calculating the volume of the given examined bodies based on the formula:
\[ V_b = a \cdot b \cdot c \]
Where:
$V_b$ - volume of examined bodies [$\text{cm}^3$], $a$ - width of examined bodies [$\text{cm}$], $b$ - length of examined bodies [$\text{cm}$], $c$ - height of examined bodies [$\text{cm}$].

\[ V_b = 2{,}5 \, [\text{cm}] \cdot 4{,}2 \, [\text{cm}] \cdot 1{,}5 \, [\text{cm}] = 15{,}75 \, [\text{cm}^3] \]

\vspace{1em}
Calculating the material density of the given examined bodies based on the formula:
\[ \rho_b = \frac{m_b}{V_b} \]
Where:
$\rho_b$ - material density of given body [$\text{g/cm}^3$], $m_b$ - mass of given body [$\text{g}$], $V_b$ - volume of examined body.

\[ \rho_b \, (\text{ebonite}) = \frac{22{,}7}{15{,}75} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}44127 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1441{,}27 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]
\[ \rho_b \, (\text{bakelite}) = \frac{19{,}8}{15{,}75} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}25714 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1257{,}14 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]
\[ \rho_b \, (\text{aluminium (10)}) = \frac{42{,}6}{15{,}75} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}70476 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2704{,}76 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]

\vspace{1em}

\[ \rho_b' = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{m_3 - m_2} \rho_w \]

\[ \rho_b' \, (\text{ebonite}) = \frac{110 - 87}{103 - 87} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{23}{16} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}4375 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1437{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]
\[ \rho_b' \, (\text{bakelite}) = \frac{110 - 90}{105 - 90} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{20}{15} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}33333 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1333{,}33 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]
\[ \rho_b' \, (\text{aluminium (10)}) = \frac{110 - 67}{83 - 67} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{43}{16} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}6875 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2687{,}5 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]
\[ \rho_b' \, (\text{aluminium (11)}) = \frac{110 - 86}{94 - 86} \, [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{24}{8} \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 3 \, [\text{g/cm}^3] = 3000 \, [\text{kg/m}^3] \]
