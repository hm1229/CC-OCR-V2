Based on the infographic, shoppers from **Gen Z** and **Millennials** are more likely to be trying out new online retailers.

The data shows:
*   **32% of Gen Z** said they had tried new online retailers because of the lockdown.
*   **35% of Millennials** said the same.

In contrast, only 43% of Baby Boomers and 48% of the Silent Generation reported *not* trying new retailers (meaning less than half of them did).
