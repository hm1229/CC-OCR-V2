According to the infographic, **most people believe the driverless car manufacturer is at fault** if a driverless car hits a person. Specifically, **73.72%** of respondents think the manufacturer is responsible, which is significantly higher than the other options:

- **13.76%** believe those inside the car are at fault.
- **12.52%** believe the pedestrian is at fault.

This indicates a strong public perception that manufacturers bear primary responsibility for the safety and behavior of autonomous vehicles.
