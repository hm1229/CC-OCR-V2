Looking at the infographic, we need to find which percentage refers to **income obtained from a business run out of a home** — that is, the share of household income generated by the home-based business.

Let’s examine the relevant stats:

- **35%** → “generate revenue above $125,000 annually” — this is about *revenue*, not necessarily *income* or *share of household income*.
  
- **36%** → “share of total receipts are reported as net income” — this refers to how much of business *receipts* become *net income*, not the portion of *household income* coming from the business.

- **50%** → “estimated homepreneur’s household income generated from their home-based businesses” — ✅ This directly answers the question: **half of the household’s total income comes from the home-based business**.

---

### Correct Answer: **50%**

This is the percentage of a homepreneur’s *household income* that is generated from their home-based business.
