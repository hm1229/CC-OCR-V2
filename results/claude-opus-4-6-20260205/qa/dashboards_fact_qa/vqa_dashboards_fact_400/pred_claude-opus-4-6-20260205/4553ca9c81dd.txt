## Two Thomas Edison Inventions Introduced at World Expos:

1. **The Phonograph** - introduced at the **1878 Paris World Expo**, where Edison used the shiny shaft to record and reproduce sound with his newly invented phonograph.

2. **Electricity** - At the **1904 St. Louis World's Fair**, the Palace of Electricity was a main attraction, and Thomas Edison, the inventor of electricity, oversaw its presentation to the public.
