Qorqodaha cantuugada tirshana, calafba hay siinnin.
VII
Cocobada midkaa taagan een, ceeb ka korahaynnin
Ceelkuu istaagaba midkii, gabadh cayaarsilya
Caloolyowna kugu beera iyo, cillo kugu raagta
Cadraddii xishootiyo marwaba, daba caraabaaya
Casarka iyo duhurkii intaa, cago budhlaynaaya
Kii dumarka ciil bada Rabboow, calafba hay siinnin.
VIII
Mid caqligu naxasobayoo, cudur ka gunxaayo
Cuyub lyo magaal lito oon, xoogga kugu caawin
Canaadnimo mid ay hayso oo, laabray cududdisa
Ciirsili'i mid ay dhaanto meel, ciyow ka yeedhaayo
Cumuuq-cumuq-midkii lagu salliday, cayn la garan waayo
'Cakudaal' intaa kugu hayoo, cunaya jiidhkaaga
Cuqdad kli la yuurura Rabboow, calafba-hay-siinnin.
