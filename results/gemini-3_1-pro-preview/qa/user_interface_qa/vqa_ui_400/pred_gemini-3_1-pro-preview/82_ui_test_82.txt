Based on the infographic:

*   In **2003**, **57.2%** of academic leaders believed online education was the same or superior to face-to-face learning.
*   Two years later (in **2005**), that number increased to **77%**.

To find how many more percentage points this is:
77% - 57.2% = **19.8%**

So, **19.8%** more academic leaders found online education same or superior in 2005 compared to 2003.
