From the infographic:

- In **2003**, **57.2%** of academic leaders believed online education was the same or even superior to face-to-face learning.
- **Two years later (i.e., in 2005)**, that number increased to **77%**.

To find how many more percentage points in 2005 compared to 2003:

**77% - 57.2% = 19.8%**

✅ **Answer: 19.8%**

So, **19.8% more** academic leaders in 2005 found online education same or superior to face-to-face learning compared to 2003.
