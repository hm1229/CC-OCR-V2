Looking at the section: "Consumers are experimenting, purchasing items they would not usually purchase online:" 

It states: "39% of respondents said the lockdown had encouraged them to try and buy goods online they had not considered before. This increased to 61% with Generation Z and over half (52%) of Millennials."

So, overall is 39%, and for Millennials it's 52%.

The increase is 52% - 39% = 13%.
