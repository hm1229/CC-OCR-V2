The steps to derive the quadratic formula are shown below.

\begin{tabular}{|l|}
\hline
Step 1: $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ \\
\hline
Step 2: $ax^2 + bx = -c$ \\
\hline
Step 3: $x^2 + \dfrac{b}{a}x = -\dfrac{c}{a}$ \\
\hline
Step 4: $x^2 + \dfrac{b}{a}x + \dfrac{b^2}{4a^2} = -\dfrac{c}{a} + \dfrac{b^2}{4a^2}$ \\
\hline
Step 5: $x^2 + \dfrac{b}{a}x + \dfrac{b^2}{4a^2} = -\dfrac{4ac}{4a^2} + \dfrac{b^2}{4a^2}$ \\
\hline
Step 6: $\left(x + \dfrac{b}{2a}\right)^2 = \dfrac{b^2 - 4ac}{4a^2}$ \\
\hline
Step 7: $\left(x + \dfrac{b}{2a}\right) = \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{b^2 - 4ac}{4a^2}} = \pm\dfrac{\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{4a}$ \\
\hline
Step 8: $x = -\dfrac{b}{2a} \pm \dfrac{\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$ \\
\hline
Step 9: $x = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$ \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
