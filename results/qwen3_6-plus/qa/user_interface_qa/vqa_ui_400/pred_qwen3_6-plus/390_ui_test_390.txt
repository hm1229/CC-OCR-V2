According to the infographic, **once opened, canned dog food should be refrigerated and kept for 3 days**.

This is shown in the “BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD” section under the canned food illustration:

> **ONCE OPENED, KEEPS FOR:  
> 3 DAYS (REFRIGERATED)**

It also notes that once opened, it should be stored at **<40°F**.

---

✅ So, the answer is: **3 days** when refrigerated after opening.
