Based on the infographic, **Coca-Cola** has received an honorable mention for upgrading to a bottle derived from plants.

The section titled "CHANGING THE WAY THE WORLD THINKS ABOUT PLASTIC BOTTLES" explains that Coca-Cola reduces its carbon footprint by using a plant-based bottle that's composed of sugarcane converted into monoethylene glycol (MEG). The alternative packaging is lighter than the average recyclable bottle but functions just like traditional plastic.
