Looking at the section titled "WHAT WE WILL SPEND ON GIFT CARDS THIS SEASON: $91 BILLION", there is a pie chart showing reasons why people don't use gift cards:

- 51% Didn't have time
- 41% Forgot
- 8% Other

The question asks for the percentage of people who either forget or have other reasons.

So, we add the percentages for "Forgot" and "Other":

41% + 8% = 49%

Answer: 49%
