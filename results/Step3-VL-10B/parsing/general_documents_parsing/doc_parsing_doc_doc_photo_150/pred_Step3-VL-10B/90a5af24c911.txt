    \item If one zero of the quadratic polynomial \( x^2 + 3x + k \) is \( 2 \), then the value of \( k \) is \\
    (a) \( 10 \) \quad (b) \( -10 \) \quad (c) \( 5 \) \quad (d) \( -5 \)

    \item If the zeroes of the quadratic polynomial \( x^2 + (a + 1)x + b \) are \( 2 \) and \( -3 \), then \\
    (a) \( a = -7, b = -1 \) \quad (b) \( a = 5, b = -1 \) \quad (c) \( a = 2, b = -6 \) \quad (d) \( a = 0, b = -6 \)
