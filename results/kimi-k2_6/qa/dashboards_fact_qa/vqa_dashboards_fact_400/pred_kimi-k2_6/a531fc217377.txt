Based on the infographic, the role of a **Data and Analytics Manager** (also referred to as "Data Science Team Leader") is to:

**Manage a team of analysts and data scientists**

Their mindset is described as a **"Data Wizards' Cheerleader"**

The skills and talents required for this role include:
- Database systems (SQL and NO SQL based)
- Leadership & project management
- Interpersonal communication
- Data mining & predictive modeling

The languages associated with this role are SQL, R, SAS, Python, Matlab, and Java.

According to the salary section, this position earns a national average salary of **$118,709** in the US, making it one of the higher-paying roles in the data science industry.
