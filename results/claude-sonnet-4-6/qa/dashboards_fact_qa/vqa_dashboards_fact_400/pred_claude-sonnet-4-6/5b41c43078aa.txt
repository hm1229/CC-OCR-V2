## The 2 Good News Highlighted as Good Practices

### 1. 📚 Read to Your Children
A significant factor influencing a child's early educational success is an **introduction to books and being read to at home** prior to beginning school.
*(National Commission on Reading, 1985)*

---

### 2. 🌟 Be a Reading Role Model
Children who know adults who **read for pleasure** take it for granted that reading is a valuable and worthwhile activity.
*(Csikszentmihalyi, 1991)*
