According to the infographic, **22.5%** of businesses expect that 10% or more of their workforce will **continue to work remotely once the pandemic is over**.

The other figures refer to:
- **16.6%** – proportion of businesses with 10%+ remote workforce **prior to February 1, 2020**
- **32.6%** – proportion of businesses with 10%+ remote workforce **on May 29, 2020** (during the pandemic)
