I need to find what percentage of smartphone users are NOT using Instagram at least once a month in 2016.

Therefore, the percentage that will NOT use Instagram at least once a month = 100% - 33.33...% = **66.67%** or approximately **66.7%** (or exactly **66.66%** if rounding to two decimal places, or simply **67%** if rounding to the nearest whole number).
