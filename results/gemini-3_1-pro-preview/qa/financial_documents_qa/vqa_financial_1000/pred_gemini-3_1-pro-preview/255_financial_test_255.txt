Based on the document provided, the amount a retailer pays for racks (fixtures) depends on their weekly RJR volume:

*   **If RJR Volume is less than 80 cartons per week:** The retailer pays **100%** of the cost.
*   **If RJR Volume is 80+ cartons per week:** There is a **standard Cost Share Program** where other manufacturers offset the fixture cost.
*   **If RJR Volume is 100+ cartons per week:** RJR will bear **all costs** for fixtures (the retailer pays nothing), provided certain conditions regarding horizontal loading, exclusive advertising, and EDLP parity are met.
