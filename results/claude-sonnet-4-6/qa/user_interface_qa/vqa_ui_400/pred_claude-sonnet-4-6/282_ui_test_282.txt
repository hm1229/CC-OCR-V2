## Swab Test

According to the infographic, the **Swab Test** ("Do I have it?") indicates if a person:

- ✅ Needs to **continue to self isolate**
- ✅ Can **return to work**
- ✅ Needs to be **re-tested**

This test works by taking a **swab sample from the throat and nose**, and is available to anyone experiencing symptoms through the NHS website, with priority testing for essential workers.
