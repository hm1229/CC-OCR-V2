## Key Statistics on Male Dominance in Top-Level Management at Nonprofits

Based on the infographic, the following statistics highlight male dominance in top-level management positions:

- **71%** of respondents from large nonprofits said their organization's **CEO is male**
- **69%** of respondents at large nonprofits said their organization's **board was "predominantly male"**
- **44%** of respondents from large nonprofits said their organization **favors males** over equally qualified females for top positions

So while **66%** of nonprofit staff are predominantly women, the leadership positions — CEO roles, board seats, and top positions — are **mostly held by men**.
