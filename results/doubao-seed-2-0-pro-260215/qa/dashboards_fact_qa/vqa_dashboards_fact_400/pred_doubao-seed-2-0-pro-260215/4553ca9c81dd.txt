The two Thomas Edison inventions introduced at World Expos, per the infographic, are:
1.  **The Phonograph**: His sound recording/reproduction device, first publicly introduced at the *1878 Paris World Expo*.
2.  **Accessible mass electricity (practical incandescent light bulb)**: Edison oversaw the public presentation of this, as the main attraction of the Palace of Electricity at the *1904 St. Louis World's Fair*.
