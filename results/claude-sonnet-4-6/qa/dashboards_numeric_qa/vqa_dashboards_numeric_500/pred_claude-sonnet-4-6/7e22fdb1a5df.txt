## Instagram Has the Highest Brand Engagement

According to the infographic, **Instagram** has the highest brand engagement among the three platforms:

| Platform | Brand Engagement |
|----------|-----------------|
| 📷 **Instagram** | **4.21%** |
| 👍 Facebook | 0.07% |
| 🐦 Twitter | 0.03% |

Instagram's brand engagement rate of **4.21%** is significantly higher than both Facebook (0.07%) and Twitter (0.03%), making it by far the most effective platform for brand-audience interactions.
