## Cape Town Stadium

Cape Town Stadium was the most expensive to build among the five stadiums mentioned, with a cost of **$776.4m**.

Here's a comparison of the costs:

| Stadium | Cost |
|---------|------|
| **Cape Town Stadium** | **$776.4m** |
| Moses Mabhida Stadium | $642.5m |
| Nelson Mandela Bay | $281.1m |
| Peter Mokaba | $160.6m |
| Mbombela | $147.2m |
