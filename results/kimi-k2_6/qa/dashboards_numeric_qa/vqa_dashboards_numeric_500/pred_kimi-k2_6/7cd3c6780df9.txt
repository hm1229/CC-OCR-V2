Based on the infographic, **Generation Z** is more likely to try new things while shopping online.

According to the section "Consumers are experimenting, purchasing items they would not usually purchase online":
- **61% of Gen Z** have been encouraged to try and buy goods online they hadn't considered before
- **52% of Millennials** have done the same

So Generation Z (61%) is more likely to try new things than Millennials (52%).
