To determine the increase in US companies’ use of social networks for marketing purposes in 2017 from the present, we analyze the relevant section of the infographic:  

- Current usage: 88.2% of US companies use social networks for marketing.  
- Projected usage in 2017: 89.4%.  

Calculate the difference: \( 89.4\% - 88.2\% = 1.2\% \).  

Thus, US companies are estimated to increase their use of social networks for marketing purposes by **1.2 percentage points** by 2017.
