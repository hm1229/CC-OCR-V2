Maan-gaab lumaayiyo
Mid baa ceel ganuniyoo
Kuu haysta gochoroo;
Mid baa kugu goblama oo
Gurxankiisu damihayn
Haddii saxar ku gaadhuo;
Gaadaa wax boobiyo
Mid baad good la tahayoo;
Mid baad garab laxaadliyoo
Ruux guda abaaloo,
Loo galoo wanaag iyo
Gaashaan la tahayoo;

Garaadlay xogtaadii
Cidi gaadhi waydee
Dadkan kugu gabaabsiyey
Kumaa helay guntaadoo
Gacan-qaad la siiyaa?
Miyey gabi habaabeen?
Maadigaa wax gabayoo
Hadba geed is-mariyoo,
Goobba midab la joogoo
Gallibaxa habenki?

Garaad-daran naftaydaay!
In kastoon gucleeyoo,
Garmaamada halldhaagiyo
Gammaankaba ka jiitoo
Cirka sare galaab-baxo,
Adigay la gooshoo

Iga gaabinaynoo,
Goobtaan is-taagaba
Adigaa galluubane,
Ma gashaygu baadqoo
