Based on the image, the emergency benefits provided to working Canadians are:

## 1. Emergency Care Benefit
- **Up to $900 bi-weekly**
- **For up to 15 weeks**
- For those who must stay home due to COVID-19 and don't have paid sick leave
- Includes individuals who are sick, those who can't access EI, or those taking care of a child or someone who is sick

## 2. Emergency Support Benefit
- **$5 billion** in total support
- For workers who are not eligible for EI and who are facing unemployment and reduced hours

Additionally, the plan includes:
- **$27 billion** in direct support for Canadian workers and businesses
- **$55 billion** in support for business liquidity through tax deferrals
