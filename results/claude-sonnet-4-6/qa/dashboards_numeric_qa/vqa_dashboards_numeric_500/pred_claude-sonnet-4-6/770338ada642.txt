## Eastern Wild Turkey

According to the map, the **Eastern Wild Turkey** (represented in **teal/cyan color**) is found in the **largest area** of the country, covering most of the **eastern half of the United States**, including the Midwest, Southeast, and Northeast regions.

It is notably highlighted in states like:
- **Alabama** (~400,000 birds)
- **Missouri** (~317,000 birds)
- And numerous other eastern states

This makes it the most **widely distributed** turkey subspecies across the United States as shown in the infographic.
