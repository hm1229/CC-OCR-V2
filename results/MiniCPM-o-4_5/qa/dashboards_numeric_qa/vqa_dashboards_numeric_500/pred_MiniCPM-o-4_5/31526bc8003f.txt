Looking at the section titled "BUSINESS SURVIVAL MODE" in the infographic:

It states: "19.3% of businesses reported that they can continue to operate at their current level of revenue and expenditures for less than 6 months before considering further staffing actions, closure or bankruptcy."

This means 19.3% can operate for less than 6 months — so the percentage that **cannot** operate for less than 6 months is the complement.

Total = 100%

So, 100% - 19.3% = 80.7%

Answer: 80.7%
