The Annual Meeting of The Harvey Society in 1977 starts at **4:30 p.m.**

This is stated in the first paragraph of the announcement:

> “The Annual Meeting of The Harvey Society will be held on Thursday, May 12 at **4:30 p.m.** in Caspary Hall (Room 1B) of the Rockefeller University.”

✅ So, the meeting begins at **4:30 p.m.**
