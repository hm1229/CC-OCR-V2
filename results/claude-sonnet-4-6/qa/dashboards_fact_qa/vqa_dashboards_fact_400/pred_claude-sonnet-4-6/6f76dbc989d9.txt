## Time-to-Market for Apps via Direct Download

According to the infographic, the **Time-to-market for apps via Direct Download is **73 days**.

This is broken down as:
- **Time-to-market**: 36 days
- **Time-to-payment**: 21 days (shown separately)

The total of **73 days** makes Direct Download the middle option between:
- **App Store**: 58 days
- **Via Customer**: 112 days
