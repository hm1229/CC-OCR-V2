Looking at the "OTHER CAUSES" section of the infographic:

- DROWNING: 2,000 annual deaths
- ACCIDENTAL SHOOTINGS: 600 annual deaths
- FALLS: 25,000 annual deaths (listed in the top 5 causes)

Comparing these numbers: 25,000 (falls) > 2,000 (drowning) > 600 (accidental shootings)

Therefore, more people died from falls than from either drowning or accidental shootings.
