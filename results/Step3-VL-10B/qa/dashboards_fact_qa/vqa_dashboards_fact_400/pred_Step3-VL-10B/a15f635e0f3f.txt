To determine the difference, first identify the relevant percentages:  

- In 2003, 57.2% of academic leaders believed online education was the same or even superior to face - to - face learning.  
- “Just two years later” (which is 2005, since 2003 + 2 = 2005), the percentage increased to 77%.  

Now calculate the difference: \( 77\% - 57.2\% = 19.8\% \).  


So, 19.8 percentage points more academic leaders in 2005 found online education same or even superior to face - to - face learning compared to 2003.
