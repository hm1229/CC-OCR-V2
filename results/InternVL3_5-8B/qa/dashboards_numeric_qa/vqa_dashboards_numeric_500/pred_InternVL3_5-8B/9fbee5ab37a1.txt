The two types of entrepreneurs identified in the infographic are:

1. **Opportunity-Driven Entrepreneurs**: These entrepreneurs operate in markets with strong enabling conditions such as New Zealand, Canada, the United Kingdom, UAE, and Portugal. These markets foster more opportunity-driven entrepreneurs.

2. **Necessity-Driven Entrepreneurs**: These entrepreneurs are found in markets such as Ghana, Brazil, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Mexico, which have fewer supporting conditions. These markets tend to have more necessity-driven entrepreneurs.
