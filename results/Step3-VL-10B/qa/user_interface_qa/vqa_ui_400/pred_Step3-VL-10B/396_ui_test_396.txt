To determine which type of dog food has a higher protein content, we analyze the "HOW TODAY’S PET FOODS MEASURE UP" section of the infographic:  

- **Dry pet food (Woof! for Dogs)**: The protein content range is marked as **18–32%** (refer to the arrow labeled “18–32%” corresponding to protein).  
- **Canned food (Le Dog)**: The protein content range is marked as **28–50%** (refer to the arrow labeled “28–50%” corresponding to protein).  

Since 28–50% is a higher range than 18–32%, **canned food has a higher protein content** than dry pet food.
