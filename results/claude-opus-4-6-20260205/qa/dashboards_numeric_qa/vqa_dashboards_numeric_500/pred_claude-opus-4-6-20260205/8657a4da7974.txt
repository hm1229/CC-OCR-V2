According to the infographic, Pinterest can help businesses in **5 unique ways**:

1. **Creates Backlinks To Your Site** - Backlinks are created when you upload pins and when users repin your content, generating more outside links to your website.

2. **Boosts Your Keyword Targeting** - Search engines scan through Pinterest, and if your keywords are being searched, your page ranking goes up, increasing the value of your backlinks.

3. **Boosts Your Social Signals** - Social signals are factored into Google and other search engine rankings, and pins help generate new social signals to boost rankings.

4. **Opportunity for New Content** - Pinterest allows you to post new images and content to your audience, which search engines reward.

5. **SEO Image Boost** - Since search engines look for images (not just webpages), optimizing photos on Pinterest gives an opportunity to utilize keywords through keyword-rich names, full descriptions, and business names.
