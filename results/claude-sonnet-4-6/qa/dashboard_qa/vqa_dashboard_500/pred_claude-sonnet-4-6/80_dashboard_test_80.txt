## Difference Between Total Exports and Total Imports

Based on the **Australia-China Trade** data:

- **Total Exports:** $101.54b
- **Total Imports:** $49.33b

### Difference:
**$101.54b - $49.33b = $52.21 billion**

Australia's exports to China exceeded imports from China by **$52.21 billion** in 2013, indicating a significant **trade surplus** in Australia's favor.
