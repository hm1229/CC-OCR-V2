## Combined Percentage of Polytechnic and College Graduates Experiencing Joblessness

According to the infographic:

- **Polytechnic Graduates:** 75%
- **College Graduates:** 30%

**Combined Total: 75% + 30% = 105%**

*(These are individual percentages representing graduates who experience joblessness for over a year after graduation, not mutually exclusive groups, so they are added separately.)*
