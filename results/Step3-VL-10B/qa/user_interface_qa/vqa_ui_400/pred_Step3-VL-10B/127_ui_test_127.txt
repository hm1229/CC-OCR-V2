To determine the percentage of Americans who do not use the internet, we first find the percentage of Americans who **do** use the internet. In the "Quick Findings" section, under "Personal Computers," it states that 75% of Americans use the internet.  

To find the percentage of Americans who do not use the internet, subtract the usage percentage from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 75\% = 25\% \)  


Thus, 25% of Americans do not use the internet.
