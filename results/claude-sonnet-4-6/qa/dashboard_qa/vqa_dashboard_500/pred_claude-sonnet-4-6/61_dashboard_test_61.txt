## Total Financial Support

Based on the infographic for **Canada's COVID-19 Economic Response Plan**:

| Support Type | Amount |
|-------------|--------|
| Direct support for Canadian Workers and Businesses | **$27 Billion** |
| Support in business liquidity through tax deferrals | **$55 Billion** |
| **Total** | **$82 Billion** |

The combined total financial support was **$82 Billion**.
