Looking at the data provided in the infographic:

- In **2010**, malware attacks accounted for **.35%** of email attacks.
- In **2013**, malware attacks accounted for **2%** of email attacks.

### Comparison:
**2% > 0.35%**

→ So, **malware attacks increased** from 2010 to 2013.

✅ **Answer: Increased**

*(Note: While overall email attacks like spam decreased significantly — from 89% to 69% — malware specifically rose as a percentage of total email attacks during this period.)*
