Based on the infographic, there are **4 steps** of app processing given in the **Decentralised** section, numbered **1 through 4**:

1. The app broadcasts a random ID and collects random IDs
2. If a user reports they are unwell, they can upload their ID to a central database
3. Users' phones regularly download the most up-to-date version of the database
4. If they see any ID(s) they have interacted with, their app calculates whether they've been in contact with an infected person
