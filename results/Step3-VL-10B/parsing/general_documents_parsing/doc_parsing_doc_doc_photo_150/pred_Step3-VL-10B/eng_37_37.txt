**Part (a): Define and explain break even analysis in units and provide the formula in units (1 mark)**

Break-even analysis in units determines the number of units (sales) needed to cover total costs (fixed + variable) so that profit is zero. The formula is: Break-even quantity = Total Fixed Costs / (Selling Price per Unit - Variable Cost per Unit). For services, "units" are sessions or students, so we need to define what a unit is (e.g., for Talk It Up, a unit could be a session; for online, a unit could be a student).
