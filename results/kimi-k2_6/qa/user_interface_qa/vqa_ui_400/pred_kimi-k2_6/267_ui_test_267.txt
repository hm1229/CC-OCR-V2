Based on the image, the two types of coronavirus tests are:

1. **Swab test** - This test determines if you currently have the virus ("Do I have it?")

2. **Antibody test** - This test determines if you have had the virus in the past ("Have I had it?")

The antibody test is further divided into two subtypes:
- Laboratory test (blood test taken by a medical professional)
- Home test ('finger-prick' test taken at home)
