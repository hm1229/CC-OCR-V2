To determine the percentage of Instagram users living inside the U.S., we refer to the "USER BASE" section of the infographic. It states: *"OVER 75% of Instagram users LIVE OUTSIDE OF THE U.S."*  

To find the percentage living **inside** the U.S., subtract the outside percentage from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 75\% = 25\% \)  


Thus, approximately 25% of Instagram users live inside the U.S.
