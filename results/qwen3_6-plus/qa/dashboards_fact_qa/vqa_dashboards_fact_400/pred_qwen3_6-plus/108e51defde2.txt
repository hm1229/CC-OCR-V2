The **zipper** was invented by **Whitcomb Judson**, and it made its first public appearance at the **1893 Chicago World’s Fair**.

According to the infographic:
> “That nifty metal thing that fastens your trousers made its first appearance at the Chicago World Fair in 1893. Designed by Whitcomb Judson, the revolutionary idea came to him after he saw a stiff-backed friend struggling to do up his shoes.”

So:
- **Inventor**: Whitcomb Judson  
- **Year introduced to public**: **1893** (at the Chicago World’s Fair)

*(Note: While Judson patented an early version called the “clasp locker” in 1891, the 1893 World’s Fair marks its debut to the general public — which is what the infographic highlights.)*
