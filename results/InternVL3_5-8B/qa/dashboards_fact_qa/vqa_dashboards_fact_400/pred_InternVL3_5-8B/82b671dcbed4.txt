The number of IDPs (Internally Displaced Persons) in the Central African Republic since January 2013 is indicated on the map with a red square symbol. The specific numbers are as follows:

- Paoua: 4,859
- Bossanga: 10,748
- Bangui: 935,000
- Batalimo: 56,309

These numbers represent the count of IDPs in these locations within the Central African Republic.
