To determine how many VMware versions have a maximum of 160 host processors, we examine the **"VMWARE VSPHERE VS. MICROSOFT HYPER-V"** section of the infographic, specifically the row for *Max host processors*:  

- **VMware Standard**: 160  
- **VMware Enterprise**: 160  
- **VMware Ent. Plus**: 160  

Microsoft Hyper - V versions have lower values (8 for Standard, Enterprise, and DC).  

Thus, there are 3 VMware versions (Standard, Enterprise, Ent. Plus) with a maximum of 160 host processors.
