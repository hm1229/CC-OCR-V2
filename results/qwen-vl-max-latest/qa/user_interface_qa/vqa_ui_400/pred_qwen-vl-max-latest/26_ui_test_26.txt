Based on the infographic titled "Best Job Search Techniques of 2013," **online recruitment platforms** are highlighted as the most popular method, with **26%** of job seekers using them. This is the highest percentage among all listed methods.

Other methods include:
- Personal research & contacts: 11%
- Recruitment agency: 9%
- Traditional media (e.g., newspapers): 7%
- Social media: 6%

Additionally, the infographic notes that **41% of people use all these methods**, indicating a multi-channel approach is common.

While online platforms are the most used, the infographic also emphasizes that **personal research and networking** (like contacting people directly) are highly effective, especially in accessing the "hidden job market" — roles not publicly advertised.

So, while **online recruitment platforms** are the most popular, combining multiple techniques (especially personal connections and online tools) appears to be the most effective strategy overall.
