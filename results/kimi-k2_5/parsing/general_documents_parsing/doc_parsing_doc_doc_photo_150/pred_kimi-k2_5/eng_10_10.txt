\noindent \textbf{1.} The cost of 1 bicycle is \rupee~1475. Find the cost of 23 bicycles.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{2.} A man makes 87 toys in 1 day. How many toys does he make in four weeks?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{3.} Buttons are packed in packets of 576 each. How many buttons are there in 48 packets?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{4.} The consumption of petrol in a scooter is 54 km per litre. How much distance will it cover in 18 litres of petrol?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{5.} The price of 17 table fans is \rupee~6545. What is the cost of 1 table fan? What is the cost of 8 table fans?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{6.} Three planes can carry 1185 passengers. How many passengers can be carried by 8 planes?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{7.} 2250 clips are equally packed in 18 packets. How many clips are there in each packet?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{8.} A car covers 2025 km in 25 hours. How much distance does it cover in 1 hour?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{9.} An off-set printing machine prints 37600 forms in 16 hours. How many forms does it print in 1 hour?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{10.} A factory produces 6816 motor horns in 12 days. How many horns does it produce in 1 day?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{11.} If 13 buses can carry 715 passengers, how many passengers can be carried by 1 bus?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{12.} A car runs 285 km on 15 litres of petrol. How many kilometres can it run on 22 litres of petrol?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{13.} The annual rent of a house is \rupee~58500. What is its rent for 17 months?

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{14.} A worker earned \rupee~1080 in 8 days. How much did he earn in 3 days?
