I need to find how many people are **not** disabled, given that 1 in 5 people have a disability.

From the infographic:
- **1 in 5 people** have some form of disability

If we consider **5 people**:
- People with disability: **1**
- People **without** disability: **5 - 1 = 4**

Therefore, **4 out of 5 people** are not disabled in Australia.
