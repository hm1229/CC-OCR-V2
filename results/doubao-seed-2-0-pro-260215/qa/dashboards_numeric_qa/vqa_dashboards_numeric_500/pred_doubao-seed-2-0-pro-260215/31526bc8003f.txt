To get this figure, we look at the **BUSINESS SURVIVAL MODE** section:
19.3% of businesses *can only continue operating at their current revenue/expenditure level for less than 6 months* before they need to consider staffing cuts, closure, or bankruptcy -- this is the share of businesses that **cannot operate for 6 months or longer** at their current levels.

If the question refers to the share that cannot survive *more than 6 months* (can only survive <6 months), the answer is **19.3%**.

If you are asking for the share that *can operate for 6 months or longer* (can survive more than <6 months), it is 100% - 19.3% = **80.7%**.
