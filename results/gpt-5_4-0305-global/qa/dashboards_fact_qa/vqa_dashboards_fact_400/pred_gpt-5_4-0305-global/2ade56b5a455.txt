The infographic does not state the percentage of all U.S. students in schools who are below the poverty line.

It does show, under “Access to Digital Tools by Socioeconomic Status,” that for students **at school**, teachers report access levels for the **“Below poverty line/Low”** group as:
- **45%** all or almost all
- **48%** most
- **58%** only some
- **67%** hardly any

But that is not the overall percentage of U.S. students who are below the poverty line.
