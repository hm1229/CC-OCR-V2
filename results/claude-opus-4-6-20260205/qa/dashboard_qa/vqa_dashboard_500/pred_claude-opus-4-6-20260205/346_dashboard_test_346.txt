## Fourth-Highest Social Media Platform for News

Based on the infographic's section "Social media makes a strong showing as an online news source," the rankings are:

1. **Facebook** - 59.5%
2. **Twitter** - 19.9%
3. **YouTube** - 12.7%
4. **Google+** - **11.6%**

So **Google+** is the fourth-highest social media platform used for reading news at **11.6%**.
