## Key Statistics from "The Digital Property Boom" Infographic

Based on the infographic, **21%** of home buyers read reviews on their mobiles.

This ranks 6th among the ways house hunters use their mobiles:

1. Read general home information - **51%**
2. Get directions to visit a home - **48%**
3. Compare prices - **44%**
4. Compare features - **35%**
5. Search a listing company's inventory - **35%**
6. **Read reviews - 21%**
7. Research mortgage financing - **20%**
8. Watch an online video about a home - **16%**
