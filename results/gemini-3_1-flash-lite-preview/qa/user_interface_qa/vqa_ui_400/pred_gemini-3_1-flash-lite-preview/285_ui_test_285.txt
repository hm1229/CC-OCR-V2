To determine the combined percentage of free apps for the iPhone and Android stores, we must first calculate the weighted average based on the data provided in the "Heavy Hitters" section of the infographic.

### 1. Identify the data points:
*   **Apple App Store for iPhone:** 71% Free apps
*   **Google Android Market:** 60% Free apps

### 2. Determine the weights (Total Apps):
The infographic provides a bar chart in the "Top Mobile App Stores" section showing the number of available apps for each platform:
*   **iPhone:** 375,566 apps
*   **Android:** 224,808 apps
*   **Total (iPhone + Android):** 600,374 apps

### 3. Calculate the number of free apps for each:
*   **iPhone Free Apps:** 375,566 * 0.71 = **266,651.86**
*   **Android Free Apps:** 224,808 * 0.60 = **134,884.80**

### 4. Calculate the combined percentage:
*   **Total Free Apps:** 266,651.86 + 134,884.80 = **401,536.66**
*   **Combined Percentage:** (401,536.66 / 600,374) * 100 ≈ **66.88%**

Taken together, approximately **66.88%** of the apps in the iPhone and Android stores are free.
