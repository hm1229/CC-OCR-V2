To find the Time-to-payment for apps through the App Store, I need to look at the "Time-to-market for apps" section in the infographic.

In that section, there are three charts: App Store, Direct Download, and Via Customer.

For the **App Store** chart:
- The dark teal color represents Time-to-market
- The light blue color represents Time-to-payment

Looking at the App Store chart:
- Time-to-market: 22 days (dark teal)
- Time-to-payment: **36 days** (light blue)

The Time-to-payment for apps through App Store is **36 days**.
