\textbf{Question 10}  

\[
(F \to (Q \& \neg S))
\]  

Select one answer. \textit{2 points}  

\begin{itemize}
    \item[A.] If the ducks are quacking loudly and the chickens aren't playing in the pond, then Bob is chasing the fox away.  
    \item[B.] The ducks are quacking loudly and the chickens aren't playing in the pond unless Bob is chasing the fox away.  
    \item[C.] The ducks are quacking loudly and the chickens aren't playing in the pond if Bob is chasing the fox away.  
    \item[D.] The ducks are quacking loudly and the chickens aren't playing in the pond only if Bob is chasing the fox away.  
\end{itemize}
