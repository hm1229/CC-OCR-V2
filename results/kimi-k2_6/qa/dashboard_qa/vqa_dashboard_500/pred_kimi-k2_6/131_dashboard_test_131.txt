Based on the infographic, the candidate for president is chosen at the **National Party Conventions**.

According to the section "HOW DO THE CANDIDATES WIN?", the process is:
- The electorate votes for presidential nominees at **Caucuses and State Primaries**
- Then **party delegates** from each state go to the **National Party Conventions**
- At the National Party Conventions, they officially choose a party candidate for president

This is shown in the diagram with the step labeled "PARTY DELEGATES" leading to "CANDIDATE FOR PRESIDENT".
