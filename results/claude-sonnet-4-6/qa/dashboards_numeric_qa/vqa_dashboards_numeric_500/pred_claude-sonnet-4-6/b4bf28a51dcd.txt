## Disability Among Senior Citizens (65+)

According to the infographic:

- **50.7%** of Australians aged **65 and older** (1.8 million people) live with a disability.
- Additionally, **2 in 5 people** with disability are **65 years or older**, highlighting that the likelihood of living with a disability **increases significantly with age**.

This contrasts with the **12.5%** rate for those **under 65** (1 in 8 people).
