# Where the Candidate for President is Chosen

According to the infographic, the candidates for president are chosen at the **National Party Conventions**. 

The process described is:

1. **The election starts with primary elections** - a preliminary election where delegates are selected
2. **The best candidates from each state go to the National Party Conventions** to officially choose/select a candidate for president

So the National Party Conventions are where the presidential candidates are officially chosen to represent their respective parties.
