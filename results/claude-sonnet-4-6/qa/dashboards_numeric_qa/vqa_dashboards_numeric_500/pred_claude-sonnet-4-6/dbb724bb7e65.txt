## Job Creation Growth Comparison

According to the infographic:

- **Female-owned firms**: **18.39% growth** (from 7.5M to 8.9M employees, 2007–2015)
- **Male-owned firms**: **0.37% growth** (from 41.05M to 41.2M employees, 2007–2015)

### Women-owned firms beat male-owned firms by **18.02 percentage points** (18.39% - 0.37%) in job creation growth during that period.

This is further reinforced by the fact that female-owned firms created **1.24 million more jobs** than male-owned counterparts during that eight-year period.
