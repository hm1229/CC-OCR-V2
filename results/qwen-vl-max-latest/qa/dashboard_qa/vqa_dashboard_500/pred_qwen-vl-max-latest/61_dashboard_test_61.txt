The total financial support extended for workers, businesses, and tax deferrals in Canada's COVID-19 Economic Response Plan is:

- **$27 billion** for direct support to Canadian workers and businesses.
- **$55 billion** for support in business liquidity through tax deferrals.

Adding these together:

**$27 billion + $55 billion = $82 billion**

So, the total financial support was **$82 billion**.
