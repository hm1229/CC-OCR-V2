According to the infographic, under the section **“Thinking Outside the Bag (or Can): Supplementing Your Dog’s Diet”**, which outlines Ian Billinghurst’s “Biologically Appropriate Raw Food” (BARF) diet, bones can be consumed by dogs in **two forms**:

> **BONES (WHOLE OR GROUND)**

This is shown with an icon of a bone and explicitly labeled as such.

---

✅ So, the two forms are:
- **Whole**
- **Ground**

These are meant to mimic what dogs’ wild ancestors would have consumed — either chewing on whole bones or consuming ground bone material along with meat and organs.
