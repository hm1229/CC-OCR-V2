# Prescription Pills

According to the infographic, **prescription pills** (specifically prescription opioids, e.g., prescription painkillers) lead to most of the deaths in the US. The infographic indicates that there was a **63% increase** in US drug-related overdose deaths, with prescription drug overdoses being a major contributor. It notes that **61% increase** in deaths were related to prescription drugs, and that the **$8.4 billion market** exists worldwide for legally manufactured and controlled drugs.

The key point is that **legal prescription drugs** — not illegal street drugs — account for the majority of drug-related deaths in the United States.
