## Major Cause of Accidental Deaths

Based on the infographic, **Motor Vehicle Accidents** is the **#1 major cause** of accidental deaths in the United States, accounting for **42,000 annual deaths**.

Key contributing factor: **Distracted driving** is cited as the No. 1 offender, with **young adults** being the primary culprits — their fatal crash rate is **three times higher** than any other age group.
