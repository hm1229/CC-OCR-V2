## Homophobic Remarks Against LGBT Students

According to the infographic, LGBT students faced the following verbal attacks:

1. **Homophobic remarks** - made in the presence of teachers (reported by **30%** of LGBT students)

2. **Homophobic slurs** - heard often or frequently by **65%** of students

3. **Verbal harassment** - experienced by **more than 8 out of 10** LGBT students in the past year

These findings highlight a pervasive culture of verbal hostility faced by LGBT youth in school environments, often without adequate intervention from school staff or officials.
