According to the infographic, 43% of Grade 5 children in South Africa were unable to read at even the basic reading level when tested as part of an international reading study (PIRLS, 2011). This implies that the percentage of Grade 5 children who were able to read at the basic level would be the remaining percentage.

To calculate the percentage of children who could read at the basic level:
100% (total) - 43% (unable to read at basic level) = 57%

So, 57% of Grade 5 children were able to read at the basic reading level.
