The infographic states:

> **“1 IN 6 HEARING LOSS”**  
> *1 in 6 Australians are affected by hearing loss.*

This means that **out of every 6 Australians, 1 is affected** by hearing loss.

Therefore, the number **not affected** is:

> **6 - 1 = 5**

---

✅ **Answer: 5 out of 6 Australians are not affected with hearing loss.**
