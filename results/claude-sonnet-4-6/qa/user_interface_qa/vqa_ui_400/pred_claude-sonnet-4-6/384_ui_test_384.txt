## Dry Dog Food vs. Canned Dog Food Storage After Opening

According to the infographic, **Dry Dog Food** can be stored for significantly longer once opened:

- **Dry Dog Food**: **90 days** (stored in a cool, dry place)
- **Canned Dog Food**: Only **3 days** (refrigerated)

So dry dog food lasts **87 days longer** than canned dog food once opened.
