1. The structure of product refer to master sample.

3. Surface can not have foreign mark, scratch, bump mark, scuffing mark, part line, shrink mark, crack, hand tear, master sample.
表面不可有料花, 裂纹, 划伤, 凸点, 毛边, 缩水线, 手指印, 批锋。

4. The position of laser number can not have screech, wipe mark, blink spot, continuation mark, gas mark and crack.
镭射数字位置不可有刮花, 裂纹, 黑点, 温差, 气纹, 爆裂。

5. Frame and clut can not have bump mark, white mark, flash, deformation, short-shot, deformation, over shot and stick mould.
骨位/卡槽不可有碰伤, 爆裂, 白点, 批锋, 变形, 缩水, 多胶, 粘模。

6. The screw position can not have crack, block, short shot and slanting.
螺丝位不可爆裂, 发白, 塞紧, 缺胶, 偏位。

7. Gate mark can not have roughness, the surrounding can not deformation.
进胶点不可凹凸不平, 水口位周围不可变形。

8. Insert part can not higher than the plane of frame.
镶件位不可高出骨位平面。

9. The edge of inner frame can not have flash, bump mark, shrinkage, toughness.
内框四边不可有批锋, 缺胶, 爆裂, 批锋, 粘模。

10. Produce date must adjust by monthly and correct.
生产日期随月份调整, 须指示正确。
