## Route Shapes in Long Routes

Based on the infographic, all the long routes have a **Loop** shape:

| Route | Shape |
|-------|-------|
| Ring of the Reeks Cycle, MacGillycuddy Reeks, Co. Kerry | Loop |
| Burren Cycling Route, Long Loop, Co. Clare | Loop |
| Inishowen Scenic Cycle, Co. Donegal | Loop |
| Ring of Kerry Scenic Cycle, Co. Kerry | Loop |

All four long routes share the same **circular/loop** route shape.
