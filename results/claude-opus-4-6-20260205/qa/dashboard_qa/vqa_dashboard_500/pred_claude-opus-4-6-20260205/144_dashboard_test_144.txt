# Characteristics of Halloween Pumpkin

Based on the infographic section titled **"Big, Round, and Orange All Over"**, the characteristics of the Halloween pumpkin are:

1. **Big**
2. **Round**
3. **Orange**

The infographic also provides additional pumpkin-related facts:

- **900 million pounds** of pumpkins are produced in the US for Halloween
- **80%** of the total pumpkin supply in the US is available during October
- That amount could make about **60 million pumpkin pies**
