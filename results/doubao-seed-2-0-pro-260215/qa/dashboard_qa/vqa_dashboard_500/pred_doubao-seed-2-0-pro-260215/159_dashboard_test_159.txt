To find this, look at the values for each metric at the *PreMatch* time point:
1.  **Preparedness (blue line)**: is at its peak/100, the highest value here
2.  **Fitness (gold line)**: is at 80, the next highest, far higher than fatigue/training load which are intentionally minimized pre-match, as part of the taper phase.

These two are at the highest levels pre-match, while training load and fatigue are kept as low as possible going into a match.
