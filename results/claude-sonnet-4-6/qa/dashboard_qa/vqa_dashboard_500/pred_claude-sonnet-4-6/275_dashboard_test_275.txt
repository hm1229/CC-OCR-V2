## Most Desired Hotel Room Item on Valentine's Day

According to the Hotels.com Romance Survey 2012, the majority of respondents (**33%**) said they would most like to find **Chocolate Covered Strawberries** in their hotel room on Valentine's Day.

This was followed by:
- 🍾 **Bottle of Champagne** - 27%
- 💆 **Massage Oil** - 21%
