SCE - Memorandum
T'shwantshanyo - Go t'shwantshanyo meriri di bolo tse pedi tse di t'shwantshanyo ka bokao
jaaka bosweu t'shwantshanyo meriri di bolo tse pediwa maswati go bonitshe ka fa
menri e fetogilefing mmala ka gona gona le
Botsfefe bo bo tsene bo lisa t'shwantshanyo ga
Dikarabo tsa baathatholwogo le go sa kiketlang mo mebeleng ya
POTSO 8: DIPOTSO TSE DI NEPAGETSING DI ELWE THOKO.)
'MENOMASWEU POLAYATHUWE'
8.1
Botswe jo bolayang batho bo sa bontshe.
8.2
Maikutlo a kutlobothoko - go bolayang batho bo sa bontshe.
8.3
T'shwantshiso - gore mmitha ya selo a se bolaleng tsala mmoki.
Pheteletso go utwatasa bosi ya thalaganyane bologanyane
Go tlhokoma go thalosa bintsotsi jo go bolayang t'shwantshanyo dilo
(Dikarabo tsa kgathatholwogo tse le bong bo neng tse bo filo thitha tsala na)
Poko e sa tl halolwang
POTSO 9: DIPOTSO TSE DIKHUTSHWE
'MOTHO' - A Mogoroso le ba bangwe
9.2
Go tlhoka botsfefe pe ntle le go nepegetseng di elwe thokoe.
Maikutlo a kutlobothoko - ka ntila ya bontshe.
9.3
Motho ga a tshepaletle, ga a itsiwe e se bontshe.
Neeletsano - go golane ga a itsiwe e se bontshe.
9.4
T'shwantshanyo - ganyana mele e mebeledi ntle le go dirisa le kathane jwa mohol
Motho ga a t'shwantshanyo mokgwa o ba itayang ka di t'shwanyang ka bokao/
(Dikarabo tsa baathatholwogo o fetogafetofego la wa sehunany,
go t'shepaletle tsa di nepegetseng di elwe thokoe.)
