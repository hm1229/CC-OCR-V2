According to the infographic, the **Louisiana Purchase** is regarded as one of the greatest real estate deals in history. In 1803, President Thomas Jefferson led the acquisition of the Louisiana Territory from France for just **$15 million**. The territory was approximately **828,000 square miles**, stretching from the Mississippi River in the east to the Rocky Mountains in the west, and from the Gulf of Mexico in the south to the Canadian border in the north. This purchase effectively **doubled the size of the United States** overnight.
