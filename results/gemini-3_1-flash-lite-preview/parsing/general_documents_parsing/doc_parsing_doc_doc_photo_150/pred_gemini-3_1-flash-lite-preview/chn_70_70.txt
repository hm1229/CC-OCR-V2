% Abstract (Chinese)
\noindent \textbf{摘要：}通过测定土壤水物理性质、持水及渗透性能，分析了小兴安岭南麓典型森林土壤的持水、蓄水和渗透能力，并利用 Kostiakov 方程、Horton 方程和 Philip 方程模拟了土壤入渗过程。结果表明：该区森林土壤容重介于 0.45 $\sim$ 1.52 g/cm$^3$ 之间；不同植被类型饱和含水率为 28.73\% $\sim$ 177.32\%，毛管含水率 25.26\% $\sim$ 156.84\%，自然含水率 13.91\% $\sim$ 119.18\%，随着土层深度加厚，三者具有明显减少趋势；土壤最大持水量范围为 282.88 $\sim$ 341.41 mm，有效持水量为 27.88 $\sim$ 61.13 mm，涵蓄降水能力为 165.98 $\sim$ 197.37 mm(除人工安落叶松林为 76.32 mm 以外)；各植被类型土壤初渗速率和稳渗速率最大值在 0 $\sim$ 10 cm 或 10 $\sim$ 20 cm 土层，平均值分别为 11.92 mm/min 和 2.78 mm/min；从土壤的入渗过程模拟看，Horton 方程的模拟效果较好。 \\
\textbf{关键词：}土壤水物理；持水能力；渗透性；小兴安岭 \\

    \Large \textbf{Soil Hydrological Properties of Main Vegetation in Down South of Xiao Xing'an Mountains}

% Authors (English)

% Abstract (English)
\noindent \textbf{Abstract:} Based on soil hydrology physical properties, water holding capacity and infiltration, the water storage and infiltration capacity were analyzed in typical forest stands in down south of Xiao Xing'an Mountains. And Kostiakov equation, Horton equation and Philip equation were used to simulate the soil infiltration processes. The results showed that soil bulk density was between 0.45 $\sim$ 1.52 g/cm$^3$, and soil saturated moisture contents of different vegetation types were between 28.73\% $\sim$ 177.32\%, capillary moisture contents were 25.26\% $\sim$ 156.84\%, the natural moisture contents were 13.91\% $\sim$ 119.18\%, and these three parameters obviously decreased with increase of soil depth; the range of soil maximum storage capacity was 282.88 $\sim$ 341.41 mm, effective capacity was 27.88 $\sim$ 61.13 mm, retention ability of precipitation was 165.98 $\sim$ 197.37 mm (except for artificial \textit{Larix gmelini} forest of 76.32 mm). Soil initial infiltration rate and steady infiltration rate of each vegetation type were the largest in 0 $\sim$ 10 cm or 10 $\sim$ 20 cm soil layer, the average value was 11.92 mm/min and 2.78 mm/min, respectively, and their minimum values in different vegetation types present in different soil layers, with average value of 2.19 mm/min and 0.66 mm/min. According to the soil infiltration process simulation, Horton equation was better for this simulation. \\
\textbf{Keywords:} soil hydrological properties; water storage capacity; infiltration; Xiao Xing'an Mountains

% Footer text
\noindent 森林土壤的水文功能主要指土壤的持水能力及渗透特性，是森林植被保持水土和涵养水源作用的重要体现，也是森林生态系统服务功能评价的一个重要指标。其中，森林土壤的持水能力是指森林生态系统中蓄留土壤水分、补充地下水和调节河川流量的功能，其与森林土壤的物理性质有直接的关系。
