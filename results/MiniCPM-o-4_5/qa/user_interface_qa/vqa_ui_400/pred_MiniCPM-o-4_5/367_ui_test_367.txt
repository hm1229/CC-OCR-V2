We are told: "OF ALL MOBILE PHONE USERS will access Instagram AT LEAST ONCE A MONTH IN 2016" and the value is "1/3".

So, 1/3 of all mobile phone users access Instagram at least once a month.

That means the percentage who do NOT access it at least once a month is:

1 - 1/3 = 2/3

Convert 2/3 to percentage: (2/3) * 100 ≈ 66.67%

Answer: 66.67%
