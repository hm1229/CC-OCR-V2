To determine the percentage of children who didn’t support the cigarette ban, we first find the percentage of children who **did** support it.  

In the “Will you support the ban?” section, it states that **86% of children support the ban**.  

To find the percentage that didn’t support, subtract the support percentage from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 86\% = 14\% \)  


Thus, 14% of children didn’t support the cigarette ban.
