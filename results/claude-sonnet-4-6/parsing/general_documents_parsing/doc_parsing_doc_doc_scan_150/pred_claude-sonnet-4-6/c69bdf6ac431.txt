\section*{III. CALCULATIONS:}

Calculating the volume of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

\[V_b = a \cdot b \cdot c\]

$V_b$ - volume of examined bodies $[\text{cm}^3]$, $a$ - width of examined bodies $[\text{cm}]$, $b$ - length of examined bodies $[\text{cm}]$, $c$ - height of examined bodies $[\text{cm}]$.

\[V_b = 2{,}5\ [\text{cm}] \cdot 4{,}2\ [\text{cm}] \cdot 1{,}5\ [\text{cm}] = 15{,}75\ [\text{cm}^3]\]

Calculating the material density of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

\[g_b = \frac{m_b}{V_b}\]

$g_b$ - material density of given body $[\text{g/cm}^3]$, $m_b$ - mass of given body $[\text{g}]$, $V_b$ - volume of examined body.

\[g_b\ (\text{ebonite}) = \frac{22{,}7}{15{,}75}\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}44127\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1441{,}27\ [\text{kg/m}^3]\]

\[g_b\ (\text{bakelite}) = \frac{19{,}8}{15{,}75}\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}25714\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1257{,}14\ [\text{kg/m}^3]\]

\[g_b\ (\text{aluminium}\ (10)) = \frac{42{,}6}{15{,}75}\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}70476\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2704{,}76\ [\text{kg/m}^3]\]

Calculating the material density using aerometer based on the formula:

\[g_b' = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{m_3 - m_2} \cdot g_w\]

$g_b'$ - material density of given body using aerometer $[\text{g/cm}^3]$, $m_n$ - values of mass from experiment $[\text{g}]$, $g_w$ - density of distilled water $= 1\ [\text{g/cm}^3]$.

\[g_b'\ (\text{ebonite}) = \frac{110 - 87}{103 - 87}\ [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{23}{16}\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}4375\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1437{,}5\ [\text{kg/m}^3]\]

\[g_b'\ (\text{bakelite}) = \frac{110 - 90}{105 - 90}\ [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{20}{15}\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1{,}33333\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 1333{,}33\ [\text{kg/m}^3]\]

\[g_b'\ (\text{aluminium}\ (10)) = \frac{110 - 67}{85 - 67}\ [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{43}{16}\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2{,}6875\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 2687{,}5\ [\text{kg/m}^3]\]

\[g_b'\ (\text{aluminium}\ (11)) = \frac{110 - 86}{94 - 86}\ [\text{g/g}] \cdot 1\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = \frac{24}{8}\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 3\ [\text{g/cm}^3] = 3000\ [\text{kg/m}^3]\]
