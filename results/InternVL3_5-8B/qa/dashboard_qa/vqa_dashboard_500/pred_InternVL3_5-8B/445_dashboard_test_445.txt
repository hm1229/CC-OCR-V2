In 2018, the Accommodation and Food Services industry in Australia had a female employment rate of 55%. This indicates that slightly more than half of the employees in this sector were women, reflecting a relatively balanced gender distribution in this industry. The industry employed a total of 896,100 people at that time, with projections to add 97,600 new jobs over the next five years. The median age for employees in this sector was 27, and 60% of the workforce was part-time, highlighting the industry's role in providing flexible employment opportunities.
