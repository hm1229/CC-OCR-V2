## Best Job Search Method in 2013

According to the infographic, **Online Recruitment Platforms** was the most popular/best job search method, used by **26%** of job seekers.

Here's the full ranking of job search methods:

| Method | Percentage |
|--------|-----------|
| Online Recruitment Platforms | **26%** |
| Personal Research & Contacts | 11% |
| Recruitment Agency | 9% |
| Traditional Media | 7% |
| Social Media | 6% |

It's also worth noting that **41%** of people said they use **ALL** of these methods combined, suggesting a multi-channel approach may be the most comprehensive strategy.
