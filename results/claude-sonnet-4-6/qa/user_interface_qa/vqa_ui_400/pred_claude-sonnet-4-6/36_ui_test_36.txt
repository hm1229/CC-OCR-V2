## The Three Nutrients in an Ideal Dog Diet

According to the infographic, an ideal diet for a dog must contain a healthy balance of:

1. 🥩 **Animal Proteins**
2. 🌾 **Carbohydrates**
3. 🫙 **Fats**

These three nutrients are visually represented in the pie chart at the top of the infographic, with the dog's **natural ancestral diet** consisting of:
- **56%** Protein
- **30%** Fats
- **14%** Carbs
