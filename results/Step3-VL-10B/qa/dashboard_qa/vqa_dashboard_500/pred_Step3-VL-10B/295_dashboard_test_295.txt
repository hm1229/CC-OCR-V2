To determine the increase in the percentage of natural features in a favourable condition from 2007 to 2016, we look at the "Natural environment" section under "Protected nature sites":  

- In 2007, 76% of natural features were in a favourable condition.  
- In 2016, 80% of natural features were in a favourable condition.  

The increase is calculated as \( 80\% - 76\% = 4\% \).
