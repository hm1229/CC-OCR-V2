To find the **percentage of paid apps on the iPhone and Android store, taken together**, we need to:

1. Get the percentage of paid apps for each platform.
2. Weight them by the number of apps (or market share) on each platform — because simply averaging 71% and 41% would be misleading if the stores have different sizes.

---

From the infographic:

### Paid App Percentages:
- **Apple App Store for iPhone**: 71% paid apps
- **Google Android Market**: 41% paid apps

### Total Apps in Each Store (from “Top Mobile App Stores” section):
- **iPhone**: 375,566 apps
- **Android**: 224,808 apps

---

### Step 1: Calculate total paid apps per platform

**iPhone paid apps** = 71% of 375,566  
= 0.71 × 375,566 ≈ **266,651.86**

**Android paid apps** = 41% of 224,808  
= 0.41 × 224,808 ≈ **92,171.28**

---

### Step 2: Total paid apps combined

= 266,651.86 + 92,171.28 ≈ **358,823.14**

---

### Step 3: Total apps combined

= 375,566 + 224,808 = **600,374**

---

### Step 4: Combined percentage of paid apps

= (358,823.14 / 600,374) × 100 ≈ **59.77%**

---

✅ **Final Answer: Approximately 59.8%**

*(Rounded to one decimal place as typical for percentages in such contexts)*
