POTSO 9: DIPOTSO TSE DIKHUTSHWANE
'MOTHO' – A Mogorosi le ba bangwe

Go tlhoka botshepegi le nnete ga motho.

9.2 Maikutlo a kutlobotlhoko – ka ntlha ya bonkgate ke tlhaname jwa motho/
Motho ga a tshepagale, ga a itsiwe e se naga.
(2)

9.3 Neeletsano - go golaganya mela e mebedi ntle le go dirisa lekopanyi.
(2)

9.4 Tshwantshanyo – Go tshwantshanya dilo tse pedi tse di tshwanang ka bokao
jaaka ba tshwantshanya mokgwa o ba itayang ka ona le wa sehunya.
Motho ga a itsiwe e se naga/molho o fetogafetoga bonolo/ga a ikanyege.
(Dikarabo tsa bathatlhojwa tse di nepagetseng di elwe tlhoko.)
