## Step 3: Visualize the Data

According to the infographic, the **third step** is to **Visualize the Data**, which involves:

- Choosing the **best chart** for your purpose:
  - 📊 **Inform** → Use a *donut chart* or *pictograph*
  - 📊 **Compare** → Try *bar, pie, or stacked column charts*
  - 📊 **Show change** → Use a *timeline* or *line chart*
