Looking at the section titled "The oh so 'HIDDEN Job Market'", there is a pie chart with two segments:

- 41%: "Have never tried applying for a role that wasn't advertised"
- 36%: "Reckon existing employees most benefit from these vacancies"

The question asks for the percent of people who have tried applying for a role that wasn't advertised. Since 41% have *never* tried, the percentage who *have* tried is the remainder.

100% - 41% = 59%

Answer: 59%
