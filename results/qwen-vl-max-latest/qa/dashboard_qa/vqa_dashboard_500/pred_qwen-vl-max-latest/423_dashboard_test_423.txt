According to the infographic, **19.3%** of businesses reported that they can continue to operate at their current level of revenue and expenditures for **less than 6 months** before considering further staffing actions, closure, or bankruptcy.

Therefore, the percentage of businesses that **feel they cannot operate for less than 6 months** with their current revenue and expenditure is:
