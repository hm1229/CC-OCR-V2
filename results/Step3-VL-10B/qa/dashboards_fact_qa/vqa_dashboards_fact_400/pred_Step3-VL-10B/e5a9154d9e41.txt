To determine which touch screen is more durable between resistive and infrared, we refer to the "DURABILITY" row in the infographic:  

- **Resistive**: Described as “Fair Durability” — screens are vulnerable to scratches and minor damage; the screen will wear over time and need replacing.  
- **Infrared**: Described as “Good Durability” — glass shatters upon major impact only.  

Thus, **infrared touch screens are more durable** than resistive touch screens.
