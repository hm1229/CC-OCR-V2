The percentage of kids who bike or walk to school decreased from **42% in 1969** to **16% in 2001**.

To calculate the reduction:

$$
42\% - 16\% = 26\%
$$

So, the percentage has reduced by **26 percentage points**.

As a percentage decrease relative to the original value:

$$
\frac{26}{42} \times 100 \approx 61.9\%
$$

Thus, the number of kids walking or cycling to school decreased by **61.9%** from 1969 to 2001.
