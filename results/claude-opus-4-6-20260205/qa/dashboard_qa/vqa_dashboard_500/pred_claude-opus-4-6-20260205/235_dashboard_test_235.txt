# Social Media: A Gateway for Child Predators - Key Statistics

## Teen Social Media Usage
- **81%** of teens have a social media account
- **65%** of teens login to social media more than once a day

## Most Popular Channels (percentage of teens using each platform)
- **Facebook**: 74%
- **Instagram**: 59%
- **Snapchat**: 57%
- **Twitter**: 32%
- **Vine**: 30%
- **Pinterest**: 20%

## Answer to Your Question

**59% of teens use Instagram**, making it the second most popular platform after Facebook (74%).

## Other Key Statistics from the Infographic

### Privacy Concerns
- Only **9%** of teens are very concerned with sharing information with 3rd parties

### Parents vs. Teens
- **93%** of parents believe teens tell them everything they do online
- **70%** of teens hide their online behavior from their parents
- **25%** of teens say their parents know little or nothing about what they do online

### What Teens Share
- Full name: **92%**
- Photos: **91%**
- Where they go to school: **71%**
- Cell phone number: **20%**
- Automatically share location info: **16%**
- **33%** of teens become friends with people they have never met in person

### Predator Statistics
- **3 out of 10 teens** have been stalked by complete strangers on Facebook
- **69%** regularly receive personal messages from people they don't know
- **16%** considered meeting face-to-face with someone they've only talked to online
- **17%** contacted online by a stranger in a way that made them feel scared or uncomfortable
