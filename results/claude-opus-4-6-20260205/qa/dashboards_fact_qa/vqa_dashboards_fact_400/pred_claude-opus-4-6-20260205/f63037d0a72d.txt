## Determining Which Vitamin Oils Provide

Based on the infographic, **vegetable oil** and **nut oil** are listed under the **Oils** category as food sources where essential vitamins can be found.

Looking at the circular chart and the food source categories, oils (vegetable oil and nut oil) are primarily sources of **Vitamin E**. Vitamin E is well-known for being found in plant-based oils, and this is consistent with the information presented in the infographic's wheel diagram, where oils appear in the Vitamin E section.
