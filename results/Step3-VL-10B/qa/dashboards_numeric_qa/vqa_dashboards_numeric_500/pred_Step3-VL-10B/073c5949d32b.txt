To determine the percentage of millennials in the U.S. who do not use pay TV, we first find the percentage that **does** use pay TV. In the "MEDIA ENGAGEMENT" section of the infographic, it shows that 46% use pay TV.  

To find the percentage that do *not* use pay TV, subtract this value from 100%:  
\( 100\% - 46\% = 54\% \)  


Thus, 54% of millennials in the U.S. do not use pay TV.
