\textbf{1.} Express each number as a product of its prime factors:

\textbf{(i)} 140

Prime factors of $140 = 2, 2, 5, 7$
$$= 2^2 \times 5 \times 7$$

\textbf{(ii)} 156

Prime factors of $156 = 2 \times 2 \times 3 \times 13$
$$= 2^2 \times 3 \times 13$$

\textbf{(iii)} 3825

Prime factors of $3825 = 3 \times 3 \times 5 \times 5 \times 17$
$$= 3^2 \times 5^2 \times 17$$

\textbf{(iv)} 5005

\textbf{2.} Find the LCM and HCF of the following pairs of integers and verify that $\text{LCM} \times \text{HCF} = \text{product of the two numbers}$

\textbf{(i)} 26 and 91

Prime factors of $26 = 2 \times 13$\\
Prime factors of $91 = 7 \times 13$

HCF of 26 and $91 = 13$\\
LCM of 26 and $91 = 2 \times 7 \times 13$
$$= 182$$

Product of two numbers $= 26 \times 91 = 2366$\\
$\text{LCM} \times \text{HCF} = 182 \times 13 = 2366$

So, Product of two numbers $= \text{LCM} \times \text{HCF}$
