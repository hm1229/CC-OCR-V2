To determine the combined percentage of blogs and social media in the "Stays Connected" section:  

- Locate the "Stays Connected" segment (the circular chart).  
- Identify the percentages for "Blogs" and "Social Media":  
  - Blogs: 15%  
  - Social Media: 16%  
- Add these two percentages: \( 15\% + 16\% = 31\% \)  


Thus, blogs and social media together account for 31% of how small business owners stay connected.
