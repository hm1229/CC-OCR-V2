According to the infographic, **80% of cigarette smoke is invisible**.

This means that only **20% of cigarette smoke is visible**.

The statistic is clearly stated in the pink cloud-shaped graphic on the left side of the image:

> **80%**  
> of cigarette smoke  
> is invisible

---

✅ So, the answer is: **20%**

*(Since 100% - 80% = 20%)*
