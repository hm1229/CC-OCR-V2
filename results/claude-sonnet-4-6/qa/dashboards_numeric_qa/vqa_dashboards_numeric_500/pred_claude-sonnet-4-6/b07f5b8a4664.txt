## Federal Government Savings if Drugs Were Decriminalized

According to the infographic, the federal government would save **$15.6 Billion** annually if drugs were decriminalized.

This breaks down by drug type:
- **Marijuana:** $8.7 Billion
- **Cocaine & Heroin:** $20 Billion (combined)
- **Other Drugs:** $12.6 Billion

Additionally, **State & Local Government expenses** would save **$25.7 Billion** annually.
