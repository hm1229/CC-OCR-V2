# Ban Ki-moon's "Education First" Initiative

The United Nations Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon's **Education First** initiative focuses on **three main pillars**:

---

## 1. 🏫 Put Every Child in School
- Eliminate cost barriers to attending and completing school
- Close the **gender gap** at all education levels
- Identify and help children who need **nutritional, health, and social support**
- Build **4 million** classrooms, especially in rural and conflict-affected areas
- **Double** education's percentage of humanitarian aid

---

## 2. 📚 Improve the Quality of Learning
- Train **2 million** well-trained teachers
- Improve **traditional and digital** learning materials
- Achieve a **45% increase** in early-childhood education programs
- Better match between schooling and needed workforce skills

---

## 3. 🌍 Foster Global Citizenship
- Promote **peace, tolerance, and respect** for diversity
- Cultivate a sense of **community, active participation, and environmental stewardship**
- Train teachers to use **innovative teaching and learning methods**

---

## 💰 Close the Financial Gap
- Increase global investment in education by **$24 billion per year**
- Invest at least **5-6% of countries' national GDP** in education
