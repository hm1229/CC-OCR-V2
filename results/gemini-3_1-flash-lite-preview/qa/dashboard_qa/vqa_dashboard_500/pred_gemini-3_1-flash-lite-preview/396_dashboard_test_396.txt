To find the difference between the percent of families that can afford child care in Ohio and Florida, we look at the bar chart titled "HOW MANY U.S. FAMILIES CAN AFFORD CHILD CARE?" (the blue bars represent the "Percent of Families That Can Afford Child Care").

1.  **Find the value for Ohio:** Locate "Ohio" in the right-hand column of the bar chart. The blue bar for Ohio extends to **31.2%**.
2.  **Find the value for Florida:** Locate "Florida" in the left-hand column of the bar chart. The blue bar for Florida extends to **30.2%**.
3.  **Calculate the difference:** 
    31.2% - 30.2% = 1.0%

The difference between the percent of families that can afford child care in Ohio and Florida is **1.0%**.
