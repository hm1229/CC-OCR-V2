To find the total hours people will spend wrapping gifts or waiting in checkout lines, we can refer to the "THE TIME SUCK" section of the infographic.

- **Wrapping gifts**: 3 hours  
- **Waiting in checkout lines**: 3.5 hours  

Now, add these two values:

$$
3 + 3.5 = 6.5 \text{ hours}
$$

So, people will spend a total of **6.5 hours** wrapping gifts or waiting in checkout lines.
