## Disney+ vs. AppleTV+ Viewership Comparison

According to the infographic, **14%** of people say they've watched **Disney+** since its launch on 24th March, compared to only **4%** who watched **AppleTV+** since its launch in November 2019.

### Key Findings:
- **Difference:** 14% - 4% = **10 percentage points higher** for Disney+
- Disney+ viewership is **3x more** than AppleTV+ viewership
- This is particularly notable given that AppleTV+ had a **head start** (launching in November 2019), while Disney+ only launched on **24th March 2020**

This suggests that Disney+ made a significantly stronger impact in a much shorter time frame compared to AppleTV+.
