The Dubai Sevens was founded in **1978**.

This is indicated on the infographic under the section that says:

> **1978**  
> THE YEAR THE TOURNAMENT STARTED IN DUBAI

Although the tournament has roots in earlier rugby sevens events (like the one that began in 1970 in Dubai as part of the World Rugby Sevens Series), the specific “Dubai Sevens” as a standalone, annual event is widely recognized as having started in **1978**, and it’s celebrated as one of the oldest and most prestigious stops on the World Rugby Sevens Series.

*(Note: The infographic also mentions “1970” as “the year the tournament started in Dubai,” which may refer to an earlier iteration or precursor event — but 1978 is the officially recognized founding year for the modern Dubai Sevens.)*
