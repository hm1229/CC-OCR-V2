## Senior Citizens in Singapore (1998)

According to the infographic, in **1998**, **1.4 in 20 citizens** were aged 65 years and above.

To calculate the percentage:

**(1.4 ÷ 20) × 100 = **7%****

So, approximately **7%** of Singapore's citizen population were seniors (aged 65 and above) in 1998. This was classified as an **"Ageing Population"** stage.
