**1. Force between two masses (Newton’s Law of Gravitation).**
From $F \propto \frac{1}{r^2}$ and $F \propto m_1m_2$
$$F = G\frac{Mm}{r^2}, \quad G = 6.67 \times 10^{-11}$$

**2. Gravitational Field Strength “g” due to mass M.**
$$g = \frac{F}{m} = \frac{GMm}{r^2m}$$
Gives
$$g = \frac{-GM}{r^2}$$
Where $M$ is source mass and $m$ is test mass.

**3. Gravitational Potential “$\phi$”.**
$$\text{work done} = \frac{F \times d}{\text{mass}}$$
Gives
$$\phi = \frac{-GM}{r}$$

**4. Gravitational Potential Energy.**
$$\phi = \frac{-GMm}{r}$$

**5. Speed of Satellite in Orbit.**
$$F_g = F_c$$
$$\frac{GMm}{r^2} = \frac{mv^2}{r}$$
Gives
$$v^2 = \frac{GM}{r}$$

**6. Period or Distance of Satellite.**
$$v = \frac{d}{t} = \frac{2\pi r}{T} \text{ squaring } v^2 = \frac{4\pi^2r^2}{T^2}$$
$$v^2 = \frac{4\pi^2r^2}{T^2} = \frac{GM}{r}$$
Rearranging gives where $r$ is distance and $T$ is period
$$T^2 = \left(\frac{4\pi^2}{Gm}\right)r^3$$
