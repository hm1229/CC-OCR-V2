To calculate how much more the total civilian casualties per month were in 2012 compared to 2011, we subtract the 2011 value from the 2012 value:

**2012:** 2,882  
**2011:** 2,510  

**Difference = 2,882 - 2,510 = 372**

✅ So, there were **372 more civilian casualties per month in 2012** compared to 2011.
