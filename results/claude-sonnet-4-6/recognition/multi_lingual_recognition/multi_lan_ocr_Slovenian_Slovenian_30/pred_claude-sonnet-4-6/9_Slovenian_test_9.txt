POTSO 8: DIPOTSO TSE DIKHUTSHWANE

'MENOMASWEU POLAYA E TSHEGA' – FS Khunou

Bolwetse jo bo bolayang batho bo sa bontshe.

8.1 Maikutlo a kutlobotlhoko – Ka nitha ya selo se se bolaileng ntsalaa mmoki. (2)

8.2 Tshwantshiso – gore mmuisi a tlhaloganye bonolo ka go tshwantshanyo dilo tse pedi go utlwatsa bogale jwa menomasweu a a go bolayang a tshega nao. (2)

POTSO 9: DIPOTSO TSE DIKHUTSHWANE

'MOTHO' – A.Mogorosi le ba bangwe

Go tlhoka potshepegi le nnete ga motho.

9.1 Maikutlo a kutlobotlhoko – ka nitha ya bonkgate ke tlhaname jwa motho/
Motho ga a tshepagale, ga a itsiwe e se naga. (2)

9.2 Neeletsano – go golaganya mela e mebedi ntle le go dirisa lekopanyi. (2)

9.3 Tshwantshanyo – Go tshwantshanyo dilo tse pedi tse di tshwanang ka bokao jaaka ba tshwantshanyo mokgwa o ba iteyang ka ona le wa sathunya.
Motho ga a itsiwe e se napa/motho o fetogafeloga bonolo/ga a kanyege.
