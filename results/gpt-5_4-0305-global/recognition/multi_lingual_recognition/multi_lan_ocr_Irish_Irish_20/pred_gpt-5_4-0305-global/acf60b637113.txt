Cuid a hAon:
Ceist 1.
Léigh an sliocht seo agus ansin freagair na ceisteanna a ghabhann leis. Tá an sliocht seo tógtha as “Coileach Ghleann Phádraig” le Buddy Jenkinson.

“Tháinig Oisín i dtír i nGleann Phádraig agus bhí sé ag foghlaim ó dhul i dtaithí ar an áit. Bhí sé ag siúl thart agus ag féachaint ar na rudaí a bhí ann. Bhí sé ag iarraidh a fháil amach cad é mar a bhí an áit agus céard a bhí ag tarlú ann. Bhí sé ag iarraidh a fháil amach cé hiad na daoine a bhí ina gcónaí ann agus cad é mar a bhí siad. Bhí sé ag iarraidh a fháil amach cad é mar a bhí an saol ann.”

Ceist (a)
Cad é a bhí Oisín ag iarraidh a fháil amach faoin áit? (4)
Ceist (b)
Cén fáth ar tháinig Oisín go Gleann Phádraig? (4)

Freagra:
Tá sé ag iarraidh a fháil amach an pobal, na daoine, an timpeallacht agus an saol sa cheantar. Níl a fhios aige cé hiad na daoine atá ann, cad é mar atá siad ná cad é mar atá an saol ann.

Coileach Ghleann Phádraig

Le Buddy Jenkinson

Cúirt an Scéil

This is an unusual story about a vet. She is called out one day to look at a prize cock, called Oisín, who has won many prizes. However, Oisín has lately taken to his bed and has had no energy. Oisín has not been in form lately and boasts about him often.

The locals laugh at her and have nicknamed her husband Coilmín – the little rooster – and jeer at him. When the vet examines the sick bird, Oisín lives in Sally’s house and has been there for years. He has become old and has been given seeds to eat – seeds that would make him drowsy. The vet suspects that Sally’s husband has tried to harm Oisín because he is sick and tired of all the jeering.
