Based on the infographic, the two types of coronavirus tests are:

1. **Swab test** – which answers the question "Do I have it?" (takes a swab sample from the throat and nose)

2. **Antibody test** – which answers the question "Have I had it?" (which comes in two forms: a **laboratory test** involving a blood test taken by a medical professional, and a **home test** involving a finger-prick test taken at home)
