This document is an **Audit Agenda** for a **Production & Process Controls Audit** conducted at **DePuy Orthopaedics, Inc.** in Warsaw, IN, from **July 13–17, 2009**.

### Key Details:

- **Audit Objective**:  
  To assess compliance with **21 CFR Part 820** (U.S. FDA Quality System Regulation) and **ISO 13485:2003** (International standard for medical device quality management systems) for selected areas of the company’s Quality Management System.

- **Audit Scope**:  
  The audit will evaluate:
  - Whether the quality management subsystems for **Production and Process Controls** are effectively implemented.
  - Effectiveness of previously identified corrective actions from internal audits.
  - It is **not** a full assessment of the entire Quality System — only specific subsystems unless otherwise identified by the company as areas of special interest or concern.
