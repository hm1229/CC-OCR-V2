According to the infographic, if drugs were decriminalized, the **federal government would save $15.6 billion annually** in expenses related to the war on drugs.

This is part of a larger total savings of $25.7 billion (including state and local government expenses).

The infographic breaks down the federal savings by drug type:
- Marijuana: $8.7 billion
- Cocaine & Heroin: $20 billion
- Other Drugs: $12.6 billion

*(Note: The sum of these categories exceeds the total federal savings figure, suggesting they may not be mutually exclusive or may represent different cost components.)*

Source cited in infographic: http://www.cato.org/pubs/wtpapers/DrugProhibitionWP.pdf
