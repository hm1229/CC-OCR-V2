To determine how many apps are downloaded in 2 minutes, first identify the number of apps downloaded **per minute** from the infographic.  

- Locate the "Apps Downloaded" segment: it shows "400,000 Apps Downloaded" (this is the rate for 1 minute).  

To find the number for 2 minutes, multiply the 1 - minute rate by 2:  
\( 400,000 \times 2 = 800,000 \)
