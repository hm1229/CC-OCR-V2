2x + 3y &= 7\\
2x &= 7 - 3y\\
x &= \frac{7}{2} - \frac{3}{2}y\\
x &= -\frac{3}{2}y + \frac{7}{2}
\end{align*}

\item \textbf{(2)}
\begin{align*}

6x + 8y &= 2(3x + 4y)\\

\item \textbf{(3)}

Let H and h be the heights of the 2 candles.

Burnt amount at any time:

H: $\dfrac{120}{180} \times H = \dfrac{2}{3}H$

h: $\dfrac{120}{300} \times h = \dfrac{2}{5}h$

Remaining:

H: $H - \dfrac{2}{3}H = \dfrac{1}{3}H$

h: $h - \dfrac{2}{5}h = \dfrac{3}{5}h$

The ratio after 2 h is $1:1$.

$$\frac{\frac{1}{3}H}{\frac{3}{5}h} = \frac{1}{1}$$

Cross multiply:
$$\frac{1}{3}H = \frac{3}{5}h$$

Cross multiply:
\begin{align*}
5H &= 9h\\
\frac{H}{h} &= \frac{9}{5}
\end{align*}

$H : h = 9 : 5$

\item \textbf{(1)}

Height after $n$ min:

$1^{\text{st}}: \dfrac{n}{180}$

$2^{\text{nd}}: \dfrac{n}{240}$

Height remaining:

$1^{\text{st}}: 1 - \dfrac{n}{180}$

$2^{\text{nd}}: 1 - \dfrac{n}{240}$

The ratio became $1:2$.

$$\frac{1 - \frac{n}{180}}{1 - \frac{n}{240}} = \frac{1}{2}$$

Cross multiply:
$$2 - \frac{n}{90} = 1 - \frac{n}{240}$$

$$\frac{n}{90} - \frac{n}{240} = 2 - 1$$

$$\frac{8n - 3n}{720} = 1$$

\begin{align*}
5n &= 720\\
n &= 144 \text{ min} = 2 \text{ h } 24 \text{ min}
\end{align*}

Working backwards: 14 36 h
