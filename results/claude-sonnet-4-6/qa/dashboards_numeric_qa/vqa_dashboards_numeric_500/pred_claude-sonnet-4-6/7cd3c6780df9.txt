## Generation Z is More Likely to Try New Things Online

According to the infographic, **Generation Z (61%)** is more likely to purchase items they would not usually buy online compared to **Millennials (52%)**.

This is highlighted in the section *"Consumers are experimenting, purchasing items they would not usually purchase online,"* where:

- **Overall**: 39%
- **Millennials**: 52%
- **Gen Z**: 61%

Gen Z leads by **9 percentage points** over Millennials in this behavior.
