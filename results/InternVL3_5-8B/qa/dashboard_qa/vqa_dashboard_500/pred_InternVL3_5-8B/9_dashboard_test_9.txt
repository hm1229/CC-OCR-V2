To determine the percentage decrease in spam mail from 2010 to 2013, we can follow these steps:

1. Identify the percentage of spam mail in 2010 and 2013.
   - In 2010, 89% of email attacks were spam.
   - In 2013, 69% of email attacks were spam.

2. Calculate the absolute decrease in the percentage of spam mail.
   - Absolute decrease = Percentage in 2010 - Percentage in 2013
   - Absolute decrease = 89% - 69% = 20%

3. Calculate the percentage decrease relative to the original percentage (in 2010).
   - Percentage decrease = (Absolute decrease / Percentage in 2010) * 100
   - Percentage decrease = (20% / 89%) * 100

4. Perform the division and multiplication.
   - Percentage decrease ≈ (20 / 89) * 100
   - Percentage decrease ≈ 0.2247 * 100
   - Percentage decrease ≈ 22.47%

Therefore, the percentage of spam mail decreased by approximately 22.47% from 2010 to 2013.
