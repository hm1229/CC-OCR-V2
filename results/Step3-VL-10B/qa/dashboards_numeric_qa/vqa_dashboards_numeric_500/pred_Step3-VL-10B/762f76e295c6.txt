To find the combined percentage of the case fatality ratio in the UK and the US, we first identify the case fatality ratio (blue bars) for each country:  

- For the UK, the case fatality ratio is 13.5%.  
- For the US, the case fatality ratio is 5.5%.  

Adding these two values together: \( 13.5 + 5.5 = 19 \).
