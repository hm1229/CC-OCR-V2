Based on the infographic, **21%** of learning materials were accessed via apps on tablets. However, it's important to note that **17%** of users installed the app on both tablets and smartphones, which means they used it on both devices.

To find the percentage of people who used **only** tablets (and not smartphones), we need to subtract those who used both from the total tablet users:

- Total tablet users: 21%
- Users who used both tablet and smartphone: 17%

So, the percentage of people who used **only** tablets is:
**21% - 17% = 4%**

Thus, **4%** of participants used only tablets for accessing learning materials via apps.
