To find the difference in number of store visits per month by an average customer and a loyal customer, we refer to the "HOW BIG" section of the infographic:  

- An average Starbucks customer visits the store **6 times a month**.  
- A loyal 20% of customers visits the store **16 times a month**.  

Calculate the difference: \( 16 - 6 = 10 \).  


The difference in number of store visits per month is 10.
