\noindent \textbf{2014 INTERNATIONAL APPLICANT FINANCIAL FORM -- STANDARD GRADUATE}

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent International applicants must affirm that they are responsible for paying all tuition, fees and living expenses for the entire period of the intended study program. Applicants must document the full cost of only the first year of study before the university can issue a Certificate of Visa Eligibility (Form I-20 or DS-2019).

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent \textbf{Instructions:} \quad \textbf{Part I} \quad Answer questions 1--7 completely.

\noindent \hspace{2.8cm} \textbf{Part II} \quad Indicate the sources of your funding, the amounts available, and the totals for each year, and have the sponsors verify these amounts by signing this form. Include required documentation as indicated below.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent Applicants must document financial support equal to or greater than the amounts indicated below (one year of study).

\noindent These figures are estimated costs and are subject to increase without notice.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\noindent \textbf{Estimated Budget for International Graduate Students (Standard Programs)}

\vspace{0.2cm}

\noindent \begin{tabular}{@{}llr@{}}
TUITION\footnotemark[1] & & \$20,190 \\
FEES (including health insurance)\footnotemark[2] & & \$3,579 \\
ROOM AND BOARD\footnotemark[3] & & \$12,410 \\
\cline{3-3}
ESTIMATED TOTAL (per year of study) & & \$36,179 \\
\end{tabular}

\vspace{0.2cm}

{\small
\noindent \footnotemark[1]Tuition indicated above is the actual 2014-15 tuition rate for the academic year only (September--May). Tuition may increase up to 10\% per year. Above tuition for Fall and Spring semesters is based on the ``flat tuition'' model where the cost is the same for 12 or more credits (not per credit). Summer and Winter session tuition and fees are not included above; Summer and Winter sessions are optional; students are not required to study in the summer and winter sessions; Summer and Winter tuition is charged per credit; standard graduate tuition and fees are approximately \$990 per credit hour.

\noindent \footnotemark[2]Fees and health insurance are listed for the full year.

\noindent \footnotemark[3]This includes housing and food costs for nine months. Students will need an additional funds for books, supplies, personal expenses and incidental costs.
}
