To determine the percentage of UK and US national newspaper companies using the @author or @site in their default tweets (which aligns with the “required hashtags” context in the infographic):  

- For the UK and US national newspapers: 44 out of 44 had onsite Twitter buttons. Of those, 18 out of 44 included either the @author or @site in the default tweet.  
- Calculate the percentage: \( \frac{18}{44} \times 100 \approx 40.91\% \).  


Thus, approximately 40.91% of UK and US national newspaper companies use the @author or @site in their default tweets.
