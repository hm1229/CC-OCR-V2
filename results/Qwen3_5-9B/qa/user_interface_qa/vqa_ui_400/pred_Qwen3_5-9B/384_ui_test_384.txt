Based on the infographic, **dry dog food** can be stored for a longer time once opened.

Here’s the breakdown from the “BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD” section:

- **Canned dog food (once opened):**
  - Store at: < 40°F (refrigerated)
  - Keeps for: **3 days**

- **Dry dog food (once opened):**
  - Store at: < 80°F (in a cool, dry place)
  - Keeps for: **90 days**

✅ So, dry dog food lasts **30 times longer** than canned dog food once opened — 90 days vs. 3 days.

*(Note: Canned food must be refrigerated after opening, while dry food can be stored at room temperature if kept cool and dry.)*
