## Females Had Higher Annual Payroll Growth

According to the infographic, **females** had a significantly higher annual payroll growth compared to males during the period **2007-2015**:

- **Females (F): 36.52%**
- **Males (M): 16.54%**

This means female-owned businesses had **more than double** the annual payroll growth rate of male-owned businesses during that eight-year period.
