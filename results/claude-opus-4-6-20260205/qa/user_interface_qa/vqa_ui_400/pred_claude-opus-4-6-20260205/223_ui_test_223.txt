## Search Engine Technologies Before 1995

Based on the "A Quick History of Search Engine Technology" timeline in the infographic, there were **2** search engine technologies before 1995:

1. **Archie (Pre-WWW)** - launched around **1990** (the first search engine that didn't really crawl sites but kept an index of public FTP servers so users could find and download files)

2. **Lycos** - launched around **1994** (the Lycos index grew from 54,000 documents in July to 60 million by 1996)
