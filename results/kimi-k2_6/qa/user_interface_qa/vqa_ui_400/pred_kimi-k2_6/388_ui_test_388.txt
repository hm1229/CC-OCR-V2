Looking at the "THINKING OUTSIDE THE BAG (OR CAN): SUPPLEMENTING YOUR DOG'S DIET" section, the items listed under the "Biologically Appropriate Raw Food" diet are:

1. Muscle meat (on bone)
2. Bones (whole or ground)
3. Organ meats (liver, kidney)
4. Raw eggs

7. Some dairy, such as yogurt
