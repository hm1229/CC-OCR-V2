According to the infographic, **60% of drivers did not consider colour as an important factor**.

Therefore, the percentage of drivers who *did* consider colour as an important factor is:

**100% - 60% = 40%**

✅ **Answer: 40%**

*(Note: The research was conducted by NRMA Insurance on a sample of over 1000 Australians nationally in February 2014, as noted at the bottom of the infographic.)*
