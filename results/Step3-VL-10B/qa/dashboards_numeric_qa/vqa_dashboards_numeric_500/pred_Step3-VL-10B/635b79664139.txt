To determine how many children out of 68 are not affected by autism, we first note the information from the infographic: “1 in 68 children are affected by autism.”  

- Number of children affected by autism: 1 (out of 68)  
- Number of children **not** affected by autism: Total children (68) − Children affected (1) = 67
