Based on the infographic, there are **three** basic approaches for organizing websites mentioned under the section “SEARCH ENGINE BASICS”:

1. **CRAWLING** – Search engines follow links to browse the web.
2. **SORTING** – Results get sorted by a variety of factors.
3. **INDEXING** – Sorted pages get stored in an index for faster matching with queries.

These three steps represent the fundamental process all modern search engines use to organize websites.

✅ **Answer: 3**
