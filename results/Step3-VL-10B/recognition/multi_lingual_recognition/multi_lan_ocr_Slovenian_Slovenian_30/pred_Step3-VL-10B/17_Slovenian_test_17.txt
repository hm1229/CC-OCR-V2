19Mme a mo robatsa fa mangoleng a gagwe; a bitsa monna mongwe, a beola makaodi a supa a tlhogo ya gagwe; a simolola go mo koafatsa, le thata ya gagwe ya mo tlogela.  
20Mosadi a re: “Samessone, Bafilitisita ba a go wela.” Mme a kubuga mo borokong a ithaya a re: “Ke tlaa tswea kwa ntle jaaka gale ke ikudubatsa.” A ba a sa itse fa Jehofa a mo tologetsa.  
21Bafilitisita ba mo tshwara, ba mo gonya matlho; ba mo isa kwa Gasa, ba mo golega ka mefiri ya kgotlho; a sila mo ntlong ya kgolegele.  
22Le fa go ntse jalo moriri wa tlhogo ya gagwe wa simolola go tlhoga a sena go beolwa.  

**Losso Iwa ga Samesone**  

23Barena ba Bafilitisita ba phuthega ba tla go isetsa Dagone modimo wa bone tlhabelo e kgolo ge go itumela; gonne ba re: “Modimo wa rona o tsentse Samesone, mmaba wa rona mo seatleng sa rona.”  
24E rile batho ba mona, ba boka modimo wa bone, gonne ba re: “Modimo wa rona o re tsentse mamba wa rona seatleng, le mosenyi wa lefatshe la rona, yo o bolaileng ba rona ba le bantsi.”  
25Ga dirala ya re dipelo tsa bone di dira mokete, ba re: “Bitsang Samesone gore a tle go re direla ditshameko.” Ba bitsa Samesone kwa ntlong ya kgolegele; a dira ditshameko fa pele ga bone; ba mmaya fa gare ga maotwana a ntlo.  
26Samesone a rayaya mosimane yo o mo tshweringe ka letsogo a re: “Ntesa ke apaape maotwana a ntlo e emeng mo go one, gore ke ikagae mo go one.”  
27Jaana ntllo ya bo o tletse banna le basadi; le barenge ba botlhe ba Bafilitisita ba le gone; le mo godimo ga ntlo go ne go le banna le basadi, e ka ne e le 3 000, ba ba lebiling Samesone a dira ditshameko.  
28Samesone a biletsa kwa go Jehofa a re: “Ao, Jehofa Modimo, ke a go rapela, a ko o nkgopole, o nthatafatsa, ke lobaka lona fola Modimo, gore ke ipusolosetse Bafilitisita ka bonakwa ka ntlhy a matlho a me a mabedi.”  
29Samesone a tshwara maotwana a mabedi a gare, a ntlo e emeng mo go one, a ikaega mo go one, mo go lengwe ka letsogo le legolo, mo go le lengwe ka la molema.  
30Samesone a re: “A ke swe le Bafilitisita.” A inama ka thata yotlhe ya gagwe; mme ntlo ya wela mo baraneng, le mo bathong botlhe ba ba mo go yone. Jalo baswi ba o ba bolaileng ka lobaka lwa loso lwa gagwe ba ne ba le bantsi go feta ba o ba bolaileng a sa tshedile.
