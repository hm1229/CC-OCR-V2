The zipper was invented by **Whitcomb Judson**, and it was first introduced to the public at the **1893 Chicago World’s Fair**.

According to the infographic, Judson came up with the idea after seeing a friend struggle to put on stiff-backed shoes — leading him to create a metal fastener that could “fasten your trousers.” This revolutionary invention made its debut at the 1893 World’s Fair in Chicago.
