Based on the infographic, Ban Ki-moon's initiatives as United Nations Secretary-General are the **"Education First"** initiative, which has three main priorities:

## 1. Put Every Child in School
- Ensuring access to education for all children, especially the 61 million primary-aged children currently out of school

## 2. Improve the Quality of Learning
- Addressing the 250 million children who cannot read, write, or count well, even after four years of schooling

## 3. Foster Global Citizenship
- Cultivating active care for the world and promoting peace, tolerance, and respect for diversity across cultures

Additionally, the initiative emphasizes **closing the financial gap** in education through:
- Increased global investment ($24 billion per year for poor countries' school systems)
- Investing at least 5-6% of countries' national GDP in education
- Sustained global advocacy efforts

The overarching goal is to reduce poverty and hunger, end wasted potential, and build stronger, more equitable societies through education.
