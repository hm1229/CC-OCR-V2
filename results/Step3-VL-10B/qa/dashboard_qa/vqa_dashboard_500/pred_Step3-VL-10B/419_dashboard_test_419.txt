To find the total percentage of businesses that laid off staff and reduced staff hours or shifts, we need to add the two relevant percentages from the "HOW ARE BUSINESSES IN CANADA GETTING THROUGH THE COVID - 19 PANDEMIC?" section:  

- Percentage of businesses that reduced staff hours or shifts: 39.9%  
- Percentage of businesses that laid off staff: 28.4%  

Adding these together: \( 39.9\% + 28.4\% = 68.3\% \)
