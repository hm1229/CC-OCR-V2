Based on the infographic, Torfaen County Borough Council provided the following support for children with additional learning needs between March and 12 June 2020:

*   **1000 days of support** provided at childcare hubs specifically for children with additional learning needs.
*   **1200 laptops** and **550 MiFi devices** made available for digitally excluded pupils at home.

These measures were part of the council's broader response to the pandemic to ensure continued access to education and care for vulnerable children.
