To determine the year with the lowest opium cultivation in Afghanistan, refer to the **“Afghanistan Drug Wars”** section of the infographic:  

- In 2000, the Taliban imposed a ban on all opium production, stating the drug was “anti - Islamic.”  
- This ban led to a record of only 19,768 acres of opium cultivation in 2001 (the year after the ban).  

Compared to later years (e.g., 2014, with 553,155 acres of cultivation), the year marked by the lowest cultivation is **2000** (due to the ban, which resulted in the lowest subsequent recorded cultivation in 2001).
