According to the survey, 30% of independent women owned their own businesses. This statistic is part of a breakdown that shows the distribution of independent women workers, with 4% being women in boards, 25% in women in C-suite roles, 30% in women-owned businesses, and 53% as women independent workers. This indicates a significant portion of women are engaged in entrepreneurship, reflecting a growing trend of women choosing to own and operate their own businesses as a form of independent work.
