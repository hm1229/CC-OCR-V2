## Increase in DNB Outbreak Percentage

According to the infographic, the percentage of Scots pine sites within 1km of a Dothistroma Needle Blight (DNB) outbreak increased from:

- **2007: 9%**
- **2013: 61%**

Therefore, the increase was **52 percentage points** (from 9% to 61%) between 2007 and 2013 *(note: the data point shown is 2013, not 2016)*.
