## Difference in Reduced Spending on Luxury Items

According to the infographic, in the **"Consumers are more concerned with essential everyday items"** section:

- **Gen Z**: 27% stated their spend on luxury products had reduced
- **Gen X**: 18% also said their spend had decreased

**Difference = 27% - 18% = 9%**

Generation Z was 9 percentage points more likely than Generation X to have reduced their spending on luxury items.
