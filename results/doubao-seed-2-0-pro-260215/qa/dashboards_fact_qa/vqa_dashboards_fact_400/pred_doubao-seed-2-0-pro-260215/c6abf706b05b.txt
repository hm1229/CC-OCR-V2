Looking at the *A QUICK HISTORY OF SEARCH ENGINE TECHNOLOGY* section, the search technologies released before 1995 are:
1.  **Archie (Pre-WWW)**: launched 1990, the first ever search engine.
2.  **Lycos**: it already had a 54,000 document index by 1994, before 1995.
Yahoo released in 1995, and Google released later in 1998, so they are not counted here.
