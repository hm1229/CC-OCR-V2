To find the difference in the percentage approval at inauguration between Truman and Clinton, follow these steps:  

1. Locate the “% PUBLIC APPROVAL AT INAUGURATION” row in the “HOW DID THEY DO?” section.  
2. Find the value for **Truman**: 88.  
3. Find the value for **Clinton**: 55.  
4. Calculate the difference: \( 88 - 55 = 33 \).
