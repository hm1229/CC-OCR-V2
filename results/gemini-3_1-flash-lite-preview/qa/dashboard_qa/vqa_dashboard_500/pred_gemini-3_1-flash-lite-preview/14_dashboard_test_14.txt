Based on the infographic, the long-term benefits of early childhood education are categorized into two main areas:

**Student Success:**
*   **Higher graduation rates:** Students are 29% more likely to graduate from high school.
*   **Academic progression:** Students are 44% less likely to repeat a grade.
*   **Test performance:** Students pass standardized tests more frequently.

**Adult Responsibility:**
*   **Employment:** Adults at age 40 who had early childhood education are more likely to be employed.
*   **Income:** These 40-year-olds have a 33% higher average income.
*   **Reduced crime:** 18-year-olds *without* early childhood education are 70% more likely to be arrested for a violent crime.
