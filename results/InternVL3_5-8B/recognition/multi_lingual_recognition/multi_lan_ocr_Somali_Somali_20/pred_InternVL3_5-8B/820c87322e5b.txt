bandhigtay meel gooni u baxsan ee goob bandhig dab ah, sanduuq deeq shaqee hey' adda Konstansnämden ama sharaxad dadweynye ee farshaxan. Gargaarka qof ba la siyaa wanaa in caanshuur lagu bixiya.

En snabb slant
Deedzan aaya ahinta u haxeya 2000 iyo 20 000 kr iyadao xili kastaa la dalban karo. Dalbo En snabb slant haddii da'aadu u haxeyso 18-25 sano oo aad dadweynaha Göteborg u soo bandhigeysa mashruuc dhaqan.

Waxa loogu tala galay:
Haddii aad ka diiwaan gashan tahay Göteborg, Da daaduna ay u haxeyso 18-25 sano. Dalabka waa in la sameeaya ugu raaran hal bil ka xiliga mashruuca dhaqan la fulinayo. Mashruuca dhaqan waa in loo soo bandhiga dadweynaha.
Nala soo xirir inaga bixna gargaarka dhaqan si aad noogala hadasho waxa suura galka ah ee aad mashruuca ku samey karto.

Deeq qoraa
Qorragu wuxuu gudiga dhaqanka kale karaara deeq gooni ahaa gaareysa 50 000 karoon. Dalbo deeqda qorraga haddii aad goreysa sheekha dheer ama gaaban ee iswidlish ah, ruwaayad, gabay iyo musalsal ama aad turjumeyso siigaaan. Waa inaad soo saartay ama turjuntay ugu yaraan hal wax soo saar suigaan sharati sano ee ugu danbeyysa.

Waxa loogu tala galay:
Waa in codsahuu ka diiwaan geshan yahay farshaxan ahana ka howl-galaag Göteborg. Waa inaad leedahay waxbarasho farshaxan ama aad ku howlaneyd howl farshaxan caddeyn leh oo xirfadesan lehna tayo sar oo yaraan sadax sanoo. Howl farshaxan caddeyn leh waxa loola jeedaa inaad tahay/ayeyd qof la naqdiyey, la siyey kontarakti ama ka muqday nidaamyo farshaxan ee kala duwan.

Deeq dhaqan
Deeqda dhaqan ee Magaalada Göteborg ee la eg 30 000 karoon waxa la bixiyaan sanadki mar.

Dalbo deeqda dhaqan haddii aad wax ku soo birirsay nolosha dhaqan ee Göteborg.

Waxa loogu tala galay:
Waa in codsahuu ka diiwaan geshay yahay farshaxan ahana ka howl-galaag Göteborg. Waa inaad leedahay waxbarasho farshaxan ama aad ku howlaneyd howl farshaxan caddeyn leh oo xirfadesan lehna tayo sar oo yaraan sadax sanoo.
