## The Louisiana Purchase

The **Louisiana Purchase** is regarded as one of the greatest real estate deals in history. In **1803**, President **Thomas Jefferson** acquired approximately **828,000 square miles** of land from France for just **$15 million**, which effectively **doubled the size of the United States overnight**.

### Key highlights of the deal:
- The territory stretched from the **Mississippi River** in the east to the **Rocky Mountains** in the west
- It extended from the **Gulf of Mexico** in the south to the **Canadian border** in the north
- France agreed to sell **New Orleans** for **$10 million** and the rest of the Louisiana Territory for an additional **$5 million**
- The land eventually became part of **15 different states**
- In today's dollars, the $15 million would equal approximately **$284 million** — still considered a bargain!
