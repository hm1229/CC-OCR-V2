During the Operation Phantom Fury depicted in the infographic, the coalition forces had more strength. The coalition included U.S. Navy SEALs/Mattis and U.S. Marines/Cowans, with a total of 10,500 U.S. personnel, 2,000 Iraqi forces, and 850 U.K. troops. In contrast, the insurgents, led by commanders Al-Janabi/Hadiid, had 4,000 men. Therefore, the coalition forces were significantly stronger in terms of manpower.
