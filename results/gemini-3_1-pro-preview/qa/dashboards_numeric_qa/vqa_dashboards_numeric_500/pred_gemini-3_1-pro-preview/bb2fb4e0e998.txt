Based on the infographic in the top left section under "Scotland's climate change plans", the text states:

"...of the 109 'on track' actions, **103 do not** have fixed timescales for delivery"

To find how many *do* have fixed timescales, you subtract the number that do not from the total:

109 (total 'on track') - 103 (no fixed timescale) = **6**

Therefore, **6** of the 'on track' actions have fixed timescales for delivery. This is also represented by the light green section of the pie chart labeled with the number 6.
