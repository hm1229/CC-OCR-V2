The document is a letter from the **American Medical Association (AMA)**, specifically from the **Division of Scientific Activities, Department of Foods and Nutrition**.

It is addressed to **William J. Darby, M.D., Ph.D.** of The Nutrition Foundation, Inc., dated **March 24, 1975**, and concerns a **reimbursement check for $56.80** for meals, gratuities, and taxi fares incurred during his participation as a moderator at the **Western Hemisphere Nutrition Congress-IV, August 19-22, 1974**.
