## Shoppers Trying Out New Online Retailers

According to the infographic, **25% of shoppers overall** said they had tried new online retailers because of the lockdown. The generations trying out new online retailers are:

1. **Gen Z** - 32% said YES
2. **Millennials** - 35% said YES

These two generations showed the highest adoption of new online retailers. In contrast, the older generations were less inclined:

- **Baby Boomers** - 43% said NO
- **Silent Generation** - 48% said NO

So primarily **Gen Z and Millennials** are the generations most actively trying out new online retailers, with Millennials leading at 35%, and they intend to continue shopping with them due to the good experience they had during the crisis.
