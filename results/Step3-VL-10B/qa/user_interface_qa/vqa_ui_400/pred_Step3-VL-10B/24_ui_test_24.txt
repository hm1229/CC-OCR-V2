To determine the total number of CAR (Central African Republic) refugees since Jan 2013, we refer to the **"Total population of CAR refugees"** section in the bottom - right corner of the map. This section lists the refugee populations in neighboring countries:  

- Cameroon: 98,504  
- Chad: 80,364  
- Democratic Republic of the Congo (DR Congo): 56,000  
- Republic of Congo: 11,000  

To find the total, we sum these values:  
\[ 98,504 + 80,364 + 56,000 + 11,000 = 245,868 \]  


Note: The large red - boxed “935,000” on the map refers to **IDPs (Internal Displaced Persons) within the CAR** (not refugees, as refugees are defined as those displaced across an international border seeking protection). The question specifically asks for “refugees,” so we use the total from the neighboring - country breakdown.
