To determine which generations are trying out new online retailers, we refer to the section “Shoppers are trying out new online retailers”:  

- **Gen Z**: 32% of Gen Z shoppers tried new online retailers (due to the lockdown, rising from the overall 25%).  
- **Millennials**: 35% of Millennials tried new online retailers (also due to the lockdown, rising from the overall 25%).  

In contrast, Baby Boomers (43%) and the Silent Generation (48%) *did not* try new online retailers (as indicated by the “NO” data for these groups).  

Thus, the generations trying out new online retailers are **Gen Z and Millennials**.
