According to the **Foodbank Hunger Report 2017** infographic, under the section **“How Many People Are Receiving Food Assistance?”**, it states:

> “This is because they feel:  
> - 42% Others need help more than they do  
> - 36% Embarrassed  
> - **33% Ashamed**”

---

✅ Therefore, **33%** of food insecure Australians who have not sought assistance from a charity feel **ashamed**.

*(Note: This percentage refers to those who have NOT sought help — specifically, the 54% of food-insecure Australians who haven’t turned to charities — and among them, 33% cite shame as a reason.)*

---

**Final Answer: 33%**
