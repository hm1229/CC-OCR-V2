## Two Specific Dog Food Nutrient Profiles (AAFCO Standards)

According to the infographic, the two specific dog food nutrient profiles are designed for:

1. **Profile 1** — Designed for **canine growth and reproduction**

2. **Profile 2** — Designed for **adult dog maintenance**

These profiles were established by **AAFCO (Association of American Feed Control Officials)** and account for the differences in formulation found in today's pet foods.
