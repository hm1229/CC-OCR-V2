## How the Fatality Rate is Calculated

According to the infographic, the fatality rate is calculated as **per 100,000 full-time workers in equivalent**, based on the Bureau of Labor Statistics' formula.

More specifically, the footnote at the bottom explains:

**Fatality Rate = (Number of fatal occupational injuries / Number of hours worked by all employees during the calendar year) × 200,000,000**

This standardized rate allows for comparison across different occupations regardless of the total number of workers in each field, providing a per-100,000 full-time worker equivalent measure of how dangerous each job is.
