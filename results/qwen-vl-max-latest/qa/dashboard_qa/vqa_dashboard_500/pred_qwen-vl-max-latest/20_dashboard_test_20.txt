The color **red** in the original LGBTQ+ rainbow flag signifies **life**.

This is based on the symbolic colors of the original eight-color rainbow flag designed by Gilbert Baker in 1978. The colors and their meanings are:

1. **Red** – Life  
2. Orange – Healing  
3. Yellow – Sunlight  
4. Green – Nature  
5. Blue – Harmony  
6. Purple – Spirit  

(Note: The original flag had eight colors, including pink and turquoise, which were later removed due to production constraints.)

So, **red represents life** in the LGBTQ+ pride flag.
