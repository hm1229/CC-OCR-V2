The two types of entrepreneurs mentioned in the infographic are:

1. **Opportunity-Driven**  
   - Found in markets with strong enabling conditions such as New Zealand, Canada, United Kingdom, UAE, and Portugal.  
   - These entrepreneurs start businesses due to favorable conditions and opportunities.

2. **Necessity-Driven**  
   - Found in markets with fewer supporting conditions, such as Ghana, Brazil, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Mexico.  
   - These entrepreneurs start businesses out of necessity, often due to limited alternatives for employment or income.
