Based on the provided text, **under-reaming of the acetabulum is dependent on two main factors**:

1.  **Bone quality**
2.  **The size of the acetabular component**

The text elaborates:
*   A 1 mm under-ream is usually sufficient for **smaller sockets**.
*   A larger socket may require **1–2 mm** under-ream.
*   **Soft bone** will more readily accommodate a greater press-fit than **sclerotic (hard) bone**.

Therefore, the surgeon must adjust the amount of under-reaming based on both the patient's bone density/quality and the chosen size of the implant component.
