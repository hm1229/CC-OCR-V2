Based on the infographic, **infrared** is more durable than resistive.

Here's what the infographic states:

- **Resistive**: "Fair Durability. Screens are vulnerable to scratches and other minor damage. The screen will wear over time and need replacing."

- **Infrared**: "Good Durability. Glass shatters upon major impact only."
