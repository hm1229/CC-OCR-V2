Based on the provided infographic, **The 3 R’s** in the context of the UK Construction Industry and the Environment are:

1.  **REDUCE**
    *   *Definition:* Eliminate the generation of waste at the source by delivering only what is required to site.

2.  **REUSE**
    *   *Definition:* Make use of excess or unused materials in their original state on other sites.

3.  **RECYCLE**
    *   *Definition:* Modify excess products to create new, usable products.

These principles are presented as key strategies for the construction industry to manage waste and reduce its environmental impact, alongside commitments like reducing landfill waste and using sustainable practices such as IBCs (Intermediate Bulk Containers) to minimize packaging waste.
