## Increase in DNB Outbreak from 2007 to 2013

Based on the infographic, the DNB (Dothistroma Needle Blight) outbreak data shows:

- **2007**: 9% of Scots pine sites within 1km of a DNB outbreak
- **2013**: 61% of Scots pine sites within 1km of a DNB outbreak

The increase is **61% - 9% = 52 percentage points**.

Note: The data points shown are for 2007 and **2013** (not 2016 as mentioned in your question).
