MAANSADA SOOMALIIDA & QAYBTA

Wārqad beriha lagu jinloy jeedhoo tagmo gelin?  
Wax adduunki ala joogay ma ifkaasun baa jira  
In Obyanme jiri doono nimo yunno jaastidhii  
Jincahaa shahuu marag ah Jimigyo Wannadaalaha!

Jalahaynku wuxuu sheegay ma ku jaastiyay  
An jujjua u dhiman maayan waxba tiga jumayn  
Adna waano uma jeeddo ama waxbo jijaday  
Haddi aadan jagalaynin badhakayga jawhar ah  
Jinnigi isha caddaa iyo jatalille kuu tali!  

Maansada Xaaji Cali waa jiifo. Wax nasafona waad ornin karta, saa labadu waa isku mide.

Techa 2d ee jiftada tusalaha u noqon karta waxan, Allaha u naxaristee, tiriyey gabyaagi weynaa ee Cali Xuseen Xirsi lagu magacab jirey. Wax tix digesho oo uu u tiriyey nin Gumeysigri Talyaniku gobannimadi jaajjus u ahaa, aadna u cadadin jirey xubnabi. Ururku gayaa waagti Soomaalaya u soo halgumi jirey ee SYL loo yiqin. Ninkaayoodcy. Sida la danbee Boosaaso loo baddaley, halkaas oo uu ku geeriyalida ku hayn siduu sheegay, iyada oo lagu aanaynayo dhibki uu Soomaalida kuu xuseen ku ayaa cid aasta dakkii reer-Boosaasood laga wayey! Cali Xuseen dibedaha ku masafaadan inoogu sheegayo; bahal baa ninki meydkisa diibedaha ku cunay! Masafaadas qaybta ngu dambaysa ayuu, dabadeed, ku yiri:

Dadow maql nin dulni qaaday ~ wax xillow danbeeyee  
Xaqu haddu ku soo degona ~ ninna duugi maayo e  
Qabrigina loo diidyo ~ ku dabooli waaye e  
Wax dabayshu noo keentay ~ in dhurwaa daligayei;

Diivanka kuma yaal ~ Shaafci nin diidaye  
Dariqooyinkii dhaaf ~ nebigna dafaaryaye  
Dello ma laha aakiro ~ ifkana derejo beelyaye  
Doofaar wedki helay ~ u dubaxin mayno e  
Hadduu daanka buuxsadyna ~ Rabbi waa ka daashaye!;  
Ninki saa damcaayee ~ dubka sare madoobow  
Waan kula dardarmiye ~ dacwigayga maqloo  
Ha ku dayannin kaa hoogey ~ dhulku yuu ku diidne!
