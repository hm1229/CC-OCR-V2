Based on the infographic, the two ways antibody testing can be done are:

1. **Laboratory test** - A blood test taken by a medical professional
2. **Home test** - A 'finger-prick' test taken at home

However, the infographic notes that the home tests are currently not accurate enough and the Government is not offering them at present, with plans to offer them in the future once more accurate tests are available. Currently, the laboratory test is being offered to NHS and social care staff, and to NHS hospital inpatients and care home residents based on their clinician's advice.
