Based on the infographic provided:

- There are **over 6 million** disabled people of working age in the UK.
- Of these, **only about 3.2 million are currently in employment**.

To find the number of unemployed disabled people of working age, we subtract the employed from the total working-age disabled population:

> **6 million - 3.2 million = 2.8 million**

---

✅ Therefore, the number of **unemployed disabled people of working age in the UK is approximately 2.8 million**.

*(Note: This figure refers specifically to those of working age who are not currently employed — it does not include disabled people outside the working-age population or those who may be economically inactive for other reasons.)*
