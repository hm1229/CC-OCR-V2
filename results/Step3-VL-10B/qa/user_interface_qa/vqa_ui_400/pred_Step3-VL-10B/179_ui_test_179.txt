To find the combined food price change of beef and bread, we first locate their respective percentages in the "FOOD PRICE CHANGES SINCE 2006" section:  

- Beef: 29.4%  
- Bread: 40.9%  

Adding these two values together: \( 29.4 + 40.9 = 70.3 \)  


The combined food price change of beef and bread is 70.3%.
