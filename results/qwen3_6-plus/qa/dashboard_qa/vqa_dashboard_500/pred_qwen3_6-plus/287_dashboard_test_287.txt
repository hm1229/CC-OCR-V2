According to the **Hotels.com Romance Survey (United States, 2012)**, for respondents aged **18–29**, the most ideal Valentine’s Day scenarios are:

1. **Exploring a new city** — **33%**  
   *(represented by a taxi icon)*

2. **Snuggling by the fire** — **33%**  
   *(represented by a fireplace icon)*

These two options are tied for first place in this age group.

Other scenarios for 18–29 year olds:
- **Dinner at a romantic restaurant** — 11%
- **Spending the night in a hotel suite** — 22%

---

✅ So, the **most ideal** scenarios for 18–29 year olds are:  
> **Exploring a new city** and **Snuggling by the fire** — both at **33%**

This suggests younger respondents favor adventure and cozy intimacy over traditional dinners or luxury stays for their ideal romantic getaway.
