To determine the percentage of internet users who are not Europeans, we look at the section with the two key percentages:  

- “21% INTERNET USERS WHO ARE EUROPEAN” (the green bar).  

Since the total percentage of all internet users is 100%, the percentage of non - European internet users is calculated as:  
\( 100\% - 21\% = 79\% \)  


Thus, 79% of internet users are not Europeans.
