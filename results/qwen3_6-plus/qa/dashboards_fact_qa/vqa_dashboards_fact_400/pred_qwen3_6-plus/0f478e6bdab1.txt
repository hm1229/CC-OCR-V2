Therefore, to find the percentage who are **fertile**, we subtract:

> **100% - 10% = 90%**

*(Note: “Fertile” here means not classified as infertile — i.e., they have become pregnant within a year of unprotected sex or are capable of doing so. The infographic defines infertility as failure to conceive after at least one year of unprotected sex.)*
