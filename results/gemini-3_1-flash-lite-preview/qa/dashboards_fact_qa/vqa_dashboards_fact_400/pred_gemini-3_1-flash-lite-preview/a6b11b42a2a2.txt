To determine the combined percentage of paid apps for the iPhone and Android stores, we must look at the "Heavy Hitters" section of the infographic:

*   **Apple App Store for iPhone:** 29% paid apps
*   **Google Android Market:** 41% paid apps

To find the combined percentage, we calculate the weighted average based on the total number of apps available in each store (as provided in the "Top Mobile App Stores" section):

1.  **Identify the totals:**
    *   Apple App Store: 375,566 apps
    *   Google Android Market: 224,808 apps
    *   Combined total: 600,374 apps

2.  **Calculate the number of paid apps for each:**
    *   Apple: 375,566 * 0.29 = 108,914.14
    *   Android: 224,808 * 0.41 = 92,171.28
    *   Total paid apps: 201,085.42

3.  **Calculate the combined percentage:**
    *   (201,085.42 / 600,374) * 100 ≈ **33.5%**

Taken together, approximately **33.5%** of the apps in the iPhone and Android stores are paid.
