This is a misinterpretation of the infographic: it lists **separate, independent leading causes of death for 15-24 year olds**, they are not causes of each other:
- *Suicide itself is the top cause of death for this age group*, making up 23.3% of all deaths of people aged 15-24.
- Motorcycle accidents are a *different, unrelated cause of death*, responsible for 4.2% of all deaths in this age group. They are not a cause of suicide, there is no data here for motorcycle accidents causing suicides.
