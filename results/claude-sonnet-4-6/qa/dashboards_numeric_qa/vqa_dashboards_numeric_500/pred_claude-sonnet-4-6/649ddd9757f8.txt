## Most People Blame the Driverless Car Manufacturer

According to the infographic, **73.72%** of respondents believe that **the driverless car manufacturer** is at fault if a driverless car hits a person.

This is the overwhelming majority view, compared to:
- **13.76%** who blame **those inside the car**
- **12.52%** who blame **the pedestrian**

This suggests that most people hold the **manufacturer responsible** for the actions and safety of autonomous vehicles, likely because they created and programmed the technology.
