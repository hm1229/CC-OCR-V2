\noindent
\textbf{摘\quad 要：}
通过测定土壤水文物理性质、持水及渗透性能，分析了小兴安岭南麓典型森林土壤的持水、蓄水和渗透能力，
并利用 Kostiakov 方程、Horton 方程和 Philip 方程模拟了土壤入渗过程。结果表明：该区森林土壤容重介于
$0.45\sim1.52\ \mathrm{g/cm^3}$ 之间，不同植被类型饱和含水率为 $28.73\%\sim177.32\%$；毛管含水率
$25.26\%\sim156.84\%$，自然含水率 $13.91\%\sim119.18\%$，随土层深度加厚，三者有同现象减小趋势；
土壤最大持水量范围为 $282.88\sim341.41\ \mathrm{mm}$；有效持水量为 $27.88\sim61.13\ \mathrm{mm}$；
蒸留水能力为 $165.98\sim197.37\ \mathrm{mm}$（除人工兴安落叶松林为 $76.32\ \mathrm{mm}$ 以外）；
各植被类型土壤初渗速率和稳渗速率最大值在 $0\sim10\ \mathrm{cm}$ 或 $10\sim20\ \mathrm{cm}$ 土层，平均值分别为
$11.92\ \mathrm{mm/min}$ 和 $2.78\ \mathrm{mm/min}$，过程模拟表明，Horton 方程的模拟效果较好。

\noindent
\textbf{Abstract:}
Based on soil hydrology physical properties, water holding capacity and infiltration, the water storage and infiltration capacity were analyzed in typical forest stands in down south of Xiaoxing'an Mountains. Horton equation and Philip equation were used to simulate the soil infiltration process. The results showed that soil bulk density was between $0.45\sim1.52\ \mathrm{g/cm^3}$, and soil saturated moisture contents of different vegetation types were between $28.73\%\sim177.32\%$; capillary moisture contents were $25.26\%\sim156.84\%$, the natural moisture contents were $13.91\%\sim119.18\%$, and these three parameters obviously decreased with increase of soil depth; the nature of soil maximum storage capacity was $282.88\sim341.41\ \mathrm{mm}$; effective capacity was $27.88\sim61.13\ \mathrm{mm}$; retention ability of precipitation water was $165.98\sim197.37\ \mathrm{mm}$ (except for artificial Larix gmelini forest of $76.32\ \mathrm{mm}$), and initial infiltration rate and steady infiltration rate of each vegetation type were the largest in $0\sim10\ \mathrm{cm}$ or $10\sim20\ \mathrm{cm}$ soil layer. The average values were $11.92\ \mathrm{mm/min}$ and $2.78\ \mathrm{mm/min}$, respectively, and their minimum values in different vegetation types present in different soil layers, with average value of $2.19\ \mathrm{mm/min}$ and $0.66\ \mathrm{mm/min}$. According to the soil infiltration process simulation, Horton equation was better for this simulation.

\noindent
\textbf{Keywords:}
hydrological properties; water storage capacity; infiltration; Xiao Xing'an Mountains

\vspace{1em}

森林土壤的水文功能主要指土壤的持水能力及渗透特性，是森林植被保持水土和涵养水源作用的重要体现，
也是森林生态系统健康功能评价的一个重要指标$^{[1]}$。其中，森林土壤的持水能力是指森林生态系统中涵养土壤水分、
补充地下水和调节河川流量的功能，其与森林土壤的物理性质有直接的关系。
