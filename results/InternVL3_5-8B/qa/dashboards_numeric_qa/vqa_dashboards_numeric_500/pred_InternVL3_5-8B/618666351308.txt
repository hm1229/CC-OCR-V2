To determine the percentage of Malaysian people who do not shop online on a daily basis, we need to consider the data provided in the chart. The chart shows the breakdown of the frequency of shopping online among Malaysian online shoppers as of February 2017.

Here's the breakdown from the chart:
- 56.7% shop a few times per year
- 24.7% shop monthly
- 13.8% shop once a year
- 4.4% shop weekly
- 0.4% shop daily

To find the percentage of people who do not shop online daily, we need to sum up the percentages of all other categories:

\[ 56.7\% + 24.7\% + 13.8\% + 4.4\% + 0.4\% = 100\% \]

Since 0.4% of the online shoppers shop daily, the percentage of online shoppers who do not shop daily is:

\[ 100\% - 0.4\% = 99.6\% \]

Therefore, 99.6% of Malaysian online shoppers do not shop online on a daily basis. 

However, this calculation is specific to online shoppers. To generalize this to the entire population, we would need additional data on the percentage of the total population that are online shoppers. But based on the information provided, 99.6% of online shoppers in Malaysia do not engage in daily online shopping.
