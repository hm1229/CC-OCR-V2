Among the options (bulbs, CFL, LED, or sun), the **sun** is a source of renewable energy. In the infographic, under the “GREEN ENERGY” category, there’s an illustration of the sun with the text “use the sun windows,” indicating the use of solar energy, which is renewable. Bulbs, CFL, and LED are energy - saving technologies but not themselves renewable energy sources (they rely on electricity, which can come from non - renewable sources like fossil fuels unless the electricity is generated from renewable sources).
