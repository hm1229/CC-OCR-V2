Based on the infographic, there are **5** unique ways Pinterest can help your business:

1.  **Creates Backlinks To Your Site:** Every time a user pins something, a new link is created, which helps with search engine rankings.
2.  **Boosts Your Keyword Targeting:** Using keywords in your pins and content helps search engines find your page, improving your ranking.
3.  **Boosts Your Social Signals:** Pins generate social signals that are factored into Google and other search engine rankings.
4.  **SEO Image Boost:** Optimizing photos with keyword-rich names and descriptions helps search engines index your images.
5.  **Opportunity for New Content:** It gives you a platform to post new images and content, which search engines reward.
