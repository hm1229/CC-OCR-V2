There are two dedicated emergency benefits for working Canadians under this COVID-19 response plan:
1.  **Emergency Care Benefit**: It provides up to *$900 bi-weekly, for up to 15 weeks*, for people who need to stay home due to COVID-19 and do not have paid sick leave. This covers people who cannot access EI (Employment Insurance), are sick themselves, or are caring for a sick child or other ill person.
2.  **Emergency Support Benefit**: This is a $5 billion program for workers who are not eligible for EI, and are facing unemployment or reduced work hours.
