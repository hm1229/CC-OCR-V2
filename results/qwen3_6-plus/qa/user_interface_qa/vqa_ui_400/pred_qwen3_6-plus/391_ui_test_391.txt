According to the infographic, **once opened, dry dog food should be stored at <80°F** and will keep for **90 days** if stored in a **cool, dry place**.

This information is found in the “BEST IF USED BY: STORING DOG FOOD” section, under the illustration of the dry food bag (purple with a dog face logo).

> **Dry Dog Food (Bag):**
> - Shelf life (unopened): 12–15 months from date packaged
> - Once opened, store at: **<80° F**
> - Once opened, keeps for: **90 days** (store in a cool, dry place)

So, the answer is:  
👉 **In a cool, dry place at less than 80°F.**

*(Note: The infographic contrasts this with canned food, which requires refrigeration after opening and has a much shorter shelf life — only 3 days.)*
