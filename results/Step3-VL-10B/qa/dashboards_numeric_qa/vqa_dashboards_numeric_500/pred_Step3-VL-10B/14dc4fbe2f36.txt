To determine the increase in percentage from overall consumers to millennials in trying new items while shopping online, we refer to the section “Consumers are experimenting, purchasing items they would not usually purchase online”:  

- Overall consumers: 39%  
- Millennials: 52%  

Calculate the difference: \( 52\% - 39\% = 13\% \)  


The increase in percentage from overall consumers to millennials is 13 percentage points.
