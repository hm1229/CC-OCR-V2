To determine which acetabular shell is measured over the Porocoat porous coating, we examine the labels below each image in Figure 17:  

- The first image: “A 54 mm Quickset grater reams a 54 mm cavity.” (not a shell, and not measured over Porocoat)  
- The second image: “A 54 mm trial shell is 54 mm in diameter.” (trial shell, not specified as measured over Porocoat)  
- The third image: “A 54 mm Pinnacle acetabular shell is 54 mm in diameter as measured over the Porocoat Porous Coating.”  

Thus, the **Pinnacle acetabular shell** is the one measured over the Porocoat porous coating.
