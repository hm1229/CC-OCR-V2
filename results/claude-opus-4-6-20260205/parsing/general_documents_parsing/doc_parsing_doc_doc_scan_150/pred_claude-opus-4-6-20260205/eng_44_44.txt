\item \textbf{(3)}\\

$x \geqslant \dfrac{1}{8}$

\item \textbf{(3)}\\
$6 - 8x < -2x$\\

$6 < 6x$\\

\item \textbf{(3)}\\
Let $H$ and $h$ be the heights of the 2 candles.\\
Burnt amount at any time:\\
H: $\dfrac{120}{180} \times H = \dfrac{2}{3}H$\\
h: $\dfrac{120}{300} \times h = \dfrac{2}{5}h$\\
Remaining:\\
H: $H - \dfrac{2}{3}H = \dfrac{1}{3}H$\\
h: $h - \dfrac{2}{5}h = \dfrac{3}{5}h$

\columnbreak

The ratio after 2 h is $1 : 1$.\\
$\dfrac{\frac{1}{3}H}{\frac{3}{5}h} = \dfrac{1}{1}$\\
Cross multiply:\\
$\dfrac{1}{3}H = \dfrac{3}{5}h$\\
Cross multiply:\\
$5H = 9h$\\
$\dfrac{H}{h} = \dfrac{9}{5}$\\
$H : h = 9 : 5$

\item \textbf{(1)}\\
Height after $n$ min:\\
1\textsuperscript{st}: $\dfrac{n}{180}$\\
2\textsuperscript{nd}: $\dfrac{n}{240}$\\
Height remaining:\\
1\textsuperscript{st}: $1 - \dfrac{n}{180}$\\
2\textsuperscript{nd}: $1 - \dfrac{n}{240}$\\
The ratio became $1 : 2$.\\
$\dfrac{1 - \frac{n}{180}}{1 - \frac{n}{240}} = \dfrac{1}{2}$\\
Cross multiply:\\
$2 - \dfrac{n}{90} = 1 - \dfrac{n}{240}$\\
$\dfrac{n}{90} - \dfrac{n}{240} = 2 - 1$\\
$\dfrac{8n - 3n}{720} = 1$\\
$5n = 720$\\
$n = 144$ min $= 2$ h 24 min\\
Working backwards: 14 36 h
