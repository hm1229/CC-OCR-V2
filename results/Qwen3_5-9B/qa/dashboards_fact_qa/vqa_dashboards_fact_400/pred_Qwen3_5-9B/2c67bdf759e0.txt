The **Battle of Blore Heath** happened in **1459**.

It was a key early battle in the **War of the Roses**, fought between the **Lancastrians** and the **Yorkists**. The Lancastrians, led by Baron Audley and Baron Dudley, faced the Yorkists under the Earl of Salisbury. Despite being outnumbered, the Yorkists used tactical feints and superior positioning to defeat the Lancastrians.

This battle is notable for being one of the first major engagements of the War of the Roses and for showcasing the importance of cavalry and terrain in medieval warfare.
