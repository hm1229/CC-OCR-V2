To find the difference between the percent of families that can afford child care in Michigan and Nevada, we look at the bar chart titled "HOW MANY U.S. FAMILIES CAN AFFORD CHILD CARE?" (the blue bars represent the "Percent of Families That Can Afford Child Care").

1.  **Find the value for Michigan:** Locate "Michigan" in the list. The blue bar corresponds to **26.0%**.
2.  **Find the value for Nevada:** Locate "Nevada" in the list. The blue bar corresponds to **23.8%**.
3.  **Calculate the difference:** 26.0% - 23.8% = 2.2%.

The difference between the percent of families that can afford child care in Michigan and Nevada is **2.2%**.
