The largest indicator of the impact of happiness in relation to jobs, according to the infographic, is **"less burnout than their peers"**, which shows a **125%** increase among thriving (happy) employees.

This is significantly higher than the other indicators:
- **Better overall job performance**: 16%
- **More satisfied with their jobs**: 46%
- **More committed to the organization**: 32%

Thus, **reduced burnout** is the most impactful outcome linked to employee happiness.
