> “Likewise, **soft bone** will more readily accommodate a greater press-fit of the acetabular component than sclerotic bone.”

---

✅ Therefore, **soft bone** accommodates a greater press-fit of the acetabular component.

This is because soft bone is more compliant and can deform slightly to allow for a tighter fit of the implant, whereas sclerotic (hard, dense) bone is less forgiving and may not allow as much press-fit without risk of fracture or improper seating.
