\vspace{1em}

\textbf{SECTION A: READING SKILLS} & \textbf{(12+10=22 Marks)} & \textbf{50 Periods} \\

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{itemize}
    \item The section will have two unseen passages with the maximum word limit of 750 words. The passages can have continuous and non-continuous text inspired from the themes in prescribed books. Please refer to the Main Course Book (MCB) for types of non-continuous texts. (For example - Unit 1 has lists, tables, cues, message, telephone conversation etc.)
    \item Objective Type Questions (including Multiple Choice Questions), Very Short Answer Type Questions (one word/one phrase/one sentence) and Short Answer Type Questions (30-40 words each) will be asked to test interpretation, analysis, inference and evaluatory vocabulary in context.
\end{itemize}

\vspace{1em}

\textbf{SECTION B: WRITING SKILLS} & \textbf{22 Marks} & \textbf{50 Periods} \\

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{itemize}
    \item This section will have a variety of short and long writing tasks.
    \item Email to school authorities (Application for leave/ change of subject/ change of topic/ bus-timings or similar topics) in maximum 50 words & 3 Marks \\
    \item Factual Description of a person/object in maximum 100 words & 4 Marks \\
    \item One out of two formal letters, in maximum 120 words, thematically aligned to topics in MCB. & 4 Marks \\
    \item Two out of two articles based on verbal cues, in maximum 150 words, thematically aligned to MCB topics. & 4 Marks \\
\end{itemize}

\vspace{1em}

\textbf{SECTION C: GRAMMAR} & \textbf{10 Marks} & \textbf{20 periods} \\

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Grammar items will be taught and assessed over a period of time.
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item Tenses
        \item Modals
        \item Subject -- verb concord
        \item Reported speech
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Commands and requests
            \item Statements
            \item Questions
        \end{itemize}
    \end{enumerate}
\end{itemize}
