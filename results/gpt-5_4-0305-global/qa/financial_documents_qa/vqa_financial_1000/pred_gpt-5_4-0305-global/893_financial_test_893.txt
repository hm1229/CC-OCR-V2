On this form, **“RJR invoice”** means an invoice from **R. J. Reynolds Tobacco Company**.

RJR = **R. J. Reynolds**.

In the document, it appears in lines like:
- **“RJR Invoice #”** — the invoice number issued by RJR
- **“Less Amount Deducted From Invoice”** — the amount already credited or deducted on that RJR invoice

So here, the RJR invoice is the company billing document tied to the tobacco products and tax reimbursement request.
