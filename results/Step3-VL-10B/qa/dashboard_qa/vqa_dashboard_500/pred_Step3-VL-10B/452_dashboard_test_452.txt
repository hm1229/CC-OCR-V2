To determine which social media platform has the most users, we analyze the user data for each platform:  

- **Facebook**: 1.44 Billion Users  
- **Twitter**: @320m Monthly Active Users  
- **LinkedIn**: 39m Recent Grads & Students (and 400 million Users Worldwide, but the primary user count highlighted for comparison is the 39m for recent grads/students, which is still far less than Facebook’s 1.44B)  

Among these, Facebook has the largest number of users (1.44 billion) compared to Twitter (320 million) and LinkedIn (39 million for recent grads/students, with 400 million worldwide users still less than 1.44 billion).  

Thus, the social media platform with the most users is **Facebook**.
