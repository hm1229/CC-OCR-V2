## Highest Levels During Pre-Match

According to the infographic, the two aspects that should be at their **highest levels during Pre-Match** are:

1. 🔵 **Preparedness** — reaching its **peak (~100)** at Pre-Match
2. 🟡 **Fitness** — also reaching its **peak (~90)** at Pre-Match

This is the goal of the **2-Day Taper** phase, which strategically reduces **Fatigue** and **Training Load** in the days leading up to the match, allowing Preparedness and Fitness to peak at the optimal time for competition.
