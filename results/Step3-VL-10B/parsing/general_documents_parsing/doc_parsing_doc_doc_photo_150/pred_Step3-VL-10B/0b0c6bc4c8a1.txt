\subsection*{SECTION A: READING SKILLS (12+10=22 Marks) 50 Periods}
- This section will have two unseen passages with the maximum word limit of 750 words. The passages can have continuous and non-continuous text inspired from the themes in prescribed books. Please refer to the Main Course Book (MCB) for types of non-continuous texts. (For example – Unit-1 has lists, tables, cues, message, telephone conversation etc.)
- Objective Type Questions (including Multiple Choice Questions). Very Short Answer Type Questions (30-40 words each) will be asked to test interpretation, analysis, inference, evaluation and vocabulary in context.

\subsection*{SECTION B: WRITING SKILLS 22 Marks 50 Periods}
This section will have a variety of short and long writing tasks.
\begin{itemize}
    \item Email to school authorities (Application for leave/ change of subject/ change of section/ bus-times or similar topics) in maximum 50 words \hfill 3 Marks
    \item Factual Description of a person/object in maximum 100 words \hfill 4 Marks
    \item One out of two formal letters, in maximum 120 words, thematically aligned to topics in MCB \hfill 7 Marks
    \item One out of two articles based on verbal cues, in maximum 150 words, thematically aligned to MCB topics. \hfill 8 Marks
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{SECTION C: GRAMMAR 10 Marks 20 Periods}
Grammar items will be taught and assessed over a period of time.
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Tenses
    \item Modals
    \item Subject - verb concord
    \item Reported spec
    \item Commands and requests
    \item Statements
    \item Questions
\end{enumerate}
