## Emergency Benefits for Working Canadians

Based on Canada's COVID-19 Economic Response Plan, two key emergency benefits were provided:

### 1. 🏥 Emergency Care Benefit
- **Amount:** Up to **$900 bi-weekly**
- **Duration:** Up to **15 weeks**
- **Eligibility:** For those who:
  - Must stay home due to COVID-19
  - Don't have paid sick leave
  - Can't access Employment Insurance (EI)
  - Are sick or caring for a sick child or person

---

### 2. 💼 Emergency Support Benefit
- **Amount:** Up to **$5 billion** in total support
- **Eligibility:** For workers who:
  - Are **not eligible for EI**
  - Are facing **unemployment**
  - Are experiencing **reduced hours**

---

### Overall Funding
| Category | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Direct support for workers & businesses | **$27 Billion** |
| Business liquidity through tax deferrals | **$55 Billion** |
