The pie chart shows the frequency of online shopping among online shoppers in Malaysia as of February 2017. The "Daily" category is represented by the green segment, which accounts for **0.4%** of online shoppers.

To find the percentage of Malaysian **online shoppers** who do **not** shop online on a daily basis, we subtract the daily shoppers from 100%:

$$
100\% - 0.4\% = 99.6\%
$$

So, **99.6%** of online shoppers in Malaysia do not shop online on a daily basis.

> Note: This refers only to **online shoppers**, not the entire population of Malaysia.
