To get the share of people who *only* use smartphones for the app, we calculate:
79% (total people using smartphones for the app **minus** 17% (people using the app on *both* smartphone and tablet) = **62%**

Note: the 79% figure cited in the infographic is the total share of people accessing learning material access via smartphones, while 17% of users have the app installed on both device types, so we subtract the overlap to get the "only smartphone" user share.
