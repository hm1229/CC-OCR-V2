This is a **Western Union Telegram** from 1961.

Specifically, it is a **day letter** (indicated by the "DL" code in the class of service box), which was a type of telegram that was sent during daytime hours at a lower cost than a regular telegram, with delivery typically made the same day.

The telegram was sent on **December 30, 1961** from **Houston, Texas** to **Dr. Roger Adams** at the **Department of Chemistry, University of Illinois, Urbana, Illinois**. It was sent by **Mark Edwin Andrews** to inform Dr. Adams about the death of his father and to express appreciation for Adams' service as a trustee of the Welch Foundation.
