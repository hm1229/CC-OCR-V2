To find the difference between the unemployment rate of New Zealand and Australia, we first identify the unemployment rates from the "UNEMPLOYMENT COMPARED TO AUSTRALIA" section:  

- New Zealand’s unemployment rate: 6.8%  
- Australia’s unemployment rate: 5.3%  

Then, calculate the difference: \( 6.8\% - 5.3\% = 1.5\% \)  


The difference between the unemployment rate of New Zealand and Australia is 1.5 percentage points.
