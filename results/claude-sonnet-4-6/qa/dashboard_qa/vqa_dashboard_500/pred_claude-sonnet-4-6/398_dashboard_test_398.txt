## First Symptom After Hospital Admission

According to the timeline, **Shortness of Breath** is the first symptom observed after hospital admission.

- **Hospital Admission** occurs at **Day 7**
- **Shortness of Breath** appears at **Day 8** — just **1 day after** hospital admission

This is followed by:
- **ARDS** (Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome) at Day 9
- **Intensive Care Unit (ICU) Admission** at Day 11
