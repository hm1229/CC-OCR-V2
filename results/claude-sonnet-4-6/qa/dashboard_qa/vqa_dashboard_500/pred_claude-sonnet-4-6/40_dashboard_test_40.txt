## Hospital Admission Timeline

According to the **Timeline of Coronavirus Onset** infographic from *The Lancet*, the COVID-19 infected patient was admitted to the hospital on **Day 7** after the onset of symptoms.

### Key Timeline Milestones:
| Day | Event |
|-----|-------|
| Day 0 | Onset of symptoms |
| Day 7 | **Hospital admission** |
| Day 8 | Shortness of breath |
| Day 9 | ARDS (Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome) |
| Day 11 | Intensive Care Unit (ICU) admission |

> *Note: These figures represent **median times** from onset of symptoms, based on early cases from Wuhan, China, published on February 15, 2020.*
