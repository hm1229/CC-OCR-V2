## Combined Percentage of Polytechnic Graduates and College Graduates Experiencing Joblessness

The combined percentage of **polytechnic graduates** and **college graduates** who experience joblessness for over a year after graduation is:

**75% + 30% = 105%**

However, since these are separate categories (not meant to be added as a total), individually:
- **Polytechnic Graduates**: 75%
- **College Graduates**: 30%

Their **combined total is 105%**, though this figure represents the sum of two distinct graduate groups' joblessness rates, not a single meaningful statistic.
