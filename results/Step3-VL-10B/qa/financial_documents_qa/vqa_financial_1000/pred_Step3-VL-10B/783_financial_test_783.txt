To determine the first Sunday in December 2006, we examine the "Sun" column of the calendar.  

- Look at the rows: The first row (topmost) has Friday (Fri) as 1 and Saturday (Sat) as 2, so the "Sun" column in that row is empty (since December 1 is a Friday, the prior Sunday would be in November).
