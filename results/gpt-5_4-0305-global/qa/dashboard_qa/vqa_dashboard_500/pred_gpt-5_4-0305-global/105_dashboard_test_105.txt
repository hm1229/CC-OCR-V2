The infographic shows:

- 42% in 1969
- 16% in 2001

So the reduction is **42% - 16% = 26 percentage points**.

If you want the **percent decrease relative to 1969**, that is:

\[
\frac{42-16}{42} \times 100 \approx 61.9\%
\]

So kids walking or cycling to school **reduced by 26 percentage points**, or about **62%** relative to 1969.
