To determine the number of unemployed disabled people of working age in the UK, we analyze the relevant data from the infographic:  

- There are **over 6 million disabled people of working age** in the UK.  
- Only about **3.2 million of these working - age disabled people are currently in employment**.  

To find the number of unemployed working - age disabled people, subtract the employed count from the total working - age count:  
\( 6\text{ million} - 3.2\text{ million} = 2.8\text{ million} \)  


Thus, approximately 2.8 million working - age disabled people in the UK are unemployed (based on the data provided in the infographic).
