Based on the **Community Sport and Active Recreation COVID-19 Factsheet** from the Department of Local Government, Sport and Cultural Industries (Western Australia), as of 26 April 2020:

> **For social distancing, ensure 4 square metres of space per person and maintain a 1.5 metre distance from others.**

This applies to:
- Outdoor activities (including bootcamps) in small groups (max 10 people total, including instructors).
- Indoor yoga or Pilates classes within a private residence.

---

✅ **Answer: 4 square metres per person, with at least 1.5 metres of physical distance between individuals.**

*(Note: This guidance was specific to WA in April 2020 and may have changed since then. Always check current local health guidelines.)*
