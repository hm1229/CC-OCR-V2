The **left bottom quadrant** in the “Pandemic Popularity Quadrant” infographic is called:

> **SLOWDOWN**

---

### Explanation:

The quadrant chart is divided into four sections based on two axes:

- **Vertical axis**: Active Users (Increase ↑ / Decrease ↓)
- **Horizontal axis**: Downloads (Decrease ← / Increase →)

So, the **bottom-left quadrant** represents:

> **Decline in both active users and new app downloads**

This matches the definition given in the top-left section of the infographic under “SLOWDOWN”.

Examples placed in this quadrant include:
- **Mobility** (e.g., Uber — downloads plummeted)
- **Real Estate** (e.g., Zillow — 23.6% decline in new downloads)
- **Travel & Hospitality** (e.g., Airbnb — bookings dropped >50% in some cities)

---

✅ So, the answer is: **SLOWDOWN**
