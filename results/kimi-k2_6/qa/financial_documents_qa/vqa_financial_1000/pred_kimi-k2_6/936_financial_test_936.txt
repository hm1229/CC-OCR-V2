Based on the newspaper article, Lazlo Toth was arrested for **"industrial espionage."**

Specifically, he was arrested after it was alleged he took several photographs of a beet-sugar plant in Vrbas, a Belgrade suburb. He was charged with industrial espionage and sentenced to seven years after a closed door trial. At the time of his arrest, he was working as Director of research and development at the Great Western Sugar Co. in Loveland, Colorado.
