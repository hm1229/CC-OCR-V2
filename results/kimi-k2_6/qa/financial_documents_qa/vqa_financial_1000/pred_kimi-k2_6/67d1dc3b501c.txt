Change of the Due Dates for Monthly Payroll Recommendations for December - Academic and Nonacademic. 

The subject line indicates that this memorandum is about adjusting the deadline for submitting payroll recommendations for December, covering both academic and nonacademic staff, due to fewer working days available for payroll processing. The new deadline for submissions is December 14, 1967, while the paycheck issuance date remains unchanged at 2:00 p.m. on December 29, 1967.
