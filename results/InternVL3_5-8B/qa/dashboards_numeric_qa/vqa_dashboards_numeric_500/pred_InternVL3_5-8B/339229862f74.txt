According to the infographic, 70% of respondents were not able to answer what ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) stood for. This means that only 30% of the respondents could correctly identify the meaning of ABS. The infographic highlights a significant gap in knowledge regarding essential car safety features among Australian drivers. Understanding such technologies is crucial for ensuring road safety, as ABS is a critical system that helps prevent wheel lock-up during braking, thereby allowing drivers to maintain steering control and avoid accidents.
