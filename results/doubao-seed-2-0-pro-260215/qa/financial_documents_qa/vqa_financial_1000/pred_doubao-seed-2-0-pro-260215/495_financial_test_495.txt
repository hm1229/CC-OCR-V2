This is explicitly labelled a **DISCIPLINE REVIEW LETTER** at the top of the document:
It is a regulatory review letter from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA, part of the Department of Health and Human Services), sent to Takeda Global Research & Development Center, to share the results of the FDA's completed review of the clinical section of Takeda's response to the FDA's proposed labeling revisions for their supplemental new drug application (NDA) for Actos® (pioglitazone HCl) tablets, and explain the FDA's rationale for required changes to the proposed drug label.
