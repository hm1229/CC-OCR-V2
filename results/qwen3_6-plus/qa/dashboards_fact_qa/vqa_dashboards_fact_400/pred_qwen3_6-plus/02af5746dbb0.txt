The **Swab test** (labeled “Do I have it?”) indicates if a person:

- needs to continue to self isolate  
- can return to work  
- needs to be re-tested  

This is shown in the bottom-left section under **“What does it mean?”** for the Swab test column.

---

✅ **Answer: Swab test**

*(Note: The antibody tests — laboratory and home — tell you whether you’ve had the virus in the past, but not whether you’re currently infectious or need to isolate. Only the swab test detects active infection.)*
