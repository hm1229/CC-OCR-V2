Tokyo had the second highest temperature. It was only 2°C lower than in Singapore at noon. The temperature in Rome at noon was 30°C, the same as in New York. However, New York's temperature at midnight was one degree lower than Rome's. The noon temperature in Cairo was 29°C, one degree higher than that in Hong Kong. The temperature at midnight in Paris was 11°C less than that at noon in Paris yesterday. The coolest city was London, with a temperature of only 22°C at noon and 13°C at midnight.

\noindent The cities with the highest temperature had a lot of sunshine throughout the day. It was cloudy, and dry at Stockholm, but it rained heavily in both London and Hong Kong.
