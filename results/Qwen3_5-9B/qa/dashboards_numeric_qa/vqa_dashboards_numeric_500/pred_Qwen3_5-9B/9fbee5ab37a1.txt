Based on the infographic, the two types of entrepreneurs are:

1.  **Opportunity-Driven Entrepreneurs**
    *   These entrepreneurs are found in markets with strong enabling conditions (like New Zealand, Canada, United Kingdom, UAE, and Portugal).
    *   They tend to start businesses to seize new opportunities.

2.  **Necessity-Driven Entrepreneurs**
    *   These entrepreneurs are found in markets with fewer supporting conditions (like Ghana, Brazil, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Mexico).
    *   They tend to start businesses out of necessity, often due to a lack of other employment options.

The infographic explains that the Mastercard Index of Women Entrepreneurs (MIWE) tracks how different local environments influence whether women become opportunity-driven or necessity-driven entrepreneurs.
