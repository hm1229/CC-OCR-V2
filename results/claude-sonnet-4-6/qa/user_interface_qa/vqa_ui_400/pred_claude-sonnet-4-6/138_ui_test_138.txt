## "I'll be back!" - Glass #4

The glasses associated with the iconic quote **"I'll be back!"** are the **dark/black wraparound sunglasses** (Number **4**), which are from the movie **Terminator** 🤖🕶️

This is the famous quote by **Arnold Schwarzenegger** as the **T-800 Terminator**, and the glasses represent the character's distinctive dark, futuristic eyewear from the film.
