To determine the fourth item in the "Biologically Appropriate Raw Food" diet, look at the section **"THINKING OUTSIDE THE BAG (OR CAN): SUPPLEMENTING YOUR DOG’S DIET"**. The items are listed with icons from left to right:  

1. Muscle meat (on bone)  
2. Bones (whole or ground)  
3. Organ meats (liver, kidney)  
4. Eggs  

Thus, the fourth item is **eggs**.
