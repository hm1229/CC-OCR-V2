## Best Aspects of Apple iPhone Platform

Based on the infographic "The Mobile Developer Journey," the **best aspects** listed for platforms (which apply to iPhone among others) are:

1. **Quick coding and prototyping**
2. **Great UI in apps**
3. **Low-cost developer tools**

These represent the best platform aspects as voted by at least 20% of the platform's respondents.

Additionally, in the **Learning Curve** section, iPhone is noted as one of the easier platforms to master, and in the **Development** section, **Android** is listed as the easiest platform to master at **5 months**, while **Symbian** is the hardest at **15 months** - with iPhone falling somewhere in between.

iPhone is also noted in the **Platform Selection** section as being chosen primarily for **commercial** and not technical reasons, with factors including:
- Large market penetration
- Revenue potential (direct or advertising)
- Quick coding and prototyping
- Rich feature/experience
