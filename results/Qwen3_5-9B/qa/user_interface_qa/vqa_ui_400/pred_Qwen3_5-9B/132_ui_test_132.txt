Based on the infographic, **THE 3 R’s** in the context of the UK construction industry and environmental responsibility are:

1.  **REDUCE**  
    *Eliminate the generation of waste at the source by delivering only what is required to site.*

2.  **REUSE**  
    *Make use of excess or unused materials in their original state on other sites.*

3.  **RECYCLE**  
    *Modify excess products to create new, usable products.*

These principles are presented as key strategies for the construction industry to address its significant environmental impact, particularly regarding waste generation.
