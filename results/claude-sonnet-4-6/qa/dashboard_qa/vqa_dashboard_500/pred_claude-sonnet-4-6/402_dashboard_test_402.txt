## Why Millennials Choose Online Viewing

According to the infographic, the **majority of millennials choose online viewing primarily for:**

### 🥇 **Immediate Access — 56%**
This is the **top reason**, as millennials prefer instant, on-demand content without waiting for scheduled broadcasts.

### 🥈 **Multitasking — 49%**
Nearly half choose online viewing because it allows them to **do multiple things simultaneously** while consuming media.

### 🥉 **Convenience of Viewing Location — 44%**
Millennials also value the **flexibility to watch content anywhere**, whether on mobile devices, tablets, or computers.

---

This aligns with their overall media behavior, as they:
- Spend **18 hours a day** consuming media
- Use **free streaming** (YouTube, Hulu) at **72%**
- Use **subscription video** (Netflix, Hulu, Amazon) at **60%**

The overarching theme is **freedom and flexibility** — millennials want content **when they want it, where they want it**.
