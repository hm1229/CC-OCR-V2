### Important Note:
The question asks for the **most densely populated** continent — which technically means **population per unit area (e.g., per square kilometer)**, not just total population.

However, **this infographic does not provide land area data for each continent**, so we cannot calculate true *density* from it alone.

But in common usage — and especially in the context of this infographic — when people say “most densely populated” without specifying area, they often mean **which continent has the largest share of the world’s population**.

In that sense, **Asia** is clearly the answer: it holds **60% of the world’s population** despite covering only about **30% of Earth’s land area** (a fact not stated here but widely known).

Moreover, the infographic implies Asia’s dominance in population distribution — and since no other continent comes close (next is Africa at 14%), **Asia is both the most populous and, by implication, the most densely populated continent** according to this visual.
