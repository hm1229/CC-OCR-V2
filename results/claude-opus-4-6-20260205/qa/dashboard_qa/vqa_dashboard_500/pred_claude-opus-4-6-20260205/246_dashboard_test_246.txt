## Nitrites/Nitrates

According to the infographic, **Nitrites/Nitrates** are the additives used in smoked fish. The infographic states that Nitrites/Nitrates are "one of the most dangerous additives" and are "used in most processed meats," with smoked fish being listed among the products that contain them (along with lunch meats, sausage, bacon, hot dogs, and canned meats).
