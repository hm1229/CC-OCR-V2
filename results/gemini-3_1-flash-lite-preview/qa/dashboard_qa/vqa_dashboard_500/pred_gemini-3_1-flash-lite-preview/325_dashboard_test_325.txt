Based on the infographic, which states that **1 in 5 people** in Australia have some form of disability, we can calculate the number of people who are not disabled in a group of five:

*   **Total people:** 5
*   **People with a disability:** 1
*   **People without a disability:** 5 - 1 = **4**

Therefore, in a group of five people, **4 people** are not disabled.
