Looking at the durability section of the infographic:
- Resistive screens only have *fair durability*: they are vulnerable to scratches and minor damage, and wear out over time and need replacement.
- Infrared touchscreens have *good durability*: their glass only shatters if subjected to major impact, with no regular wear/scratch vulnerability like resistive screens.

**Infrared touch screens are more durable than resistive ones.**
