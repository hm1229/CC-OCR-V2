## Key Population Distribution Statistics from the Infographic

According to the infographic, **14%** of the world's population lives in **Africa**.

This is shown in the "Where do we all live?" section, which breaks down global population distribution as follows:

- **Asia**: 60%
- **Africa**: 14%
- **Europe**: 11%
- **North America**: 8%
- **South America**: 5.3%
- **Australia**: 0.3%
