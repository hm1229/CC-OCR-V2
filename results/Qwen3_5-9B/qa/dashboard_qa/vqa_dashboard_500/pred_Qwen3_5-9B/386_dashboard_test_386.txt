Based on the infographic titled **“Top 5 Causes of Accidental Deaths in the United States”**, the major reason (i.e., the leading cause) is:

> **Motor Vehicle Accidents** — with **42,000 annual deaths**.

---

### Breakdown of the Top 5 Causes (by number of deaths):

1. **Motor Vehicle Accidents** – **42,000**
2. **Poisoning** – **39,000**
3. **Falls** – **25,000**
4. **Fires** – **2,700**
5. **Choking** – **2,500**

*(Note: Drowning and Accidental Shootings are listed under “Other Causes” — 2,000 and 600 respectively — so they are not among the top 5.)*

---

✅ Therefore, **Motor Vehicle Accidents** is the **major reason** for accidental deaths according to this data.

*(Data source: National Safety Council, as noted at the bottom of the infographic.)*
