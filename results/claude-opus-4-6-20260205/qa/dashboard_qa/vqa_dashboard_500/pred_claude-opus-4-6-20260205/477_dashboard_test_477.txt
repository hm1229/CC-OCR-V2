According to the infographic on **Canada's Economic Response Plan for COVID-19**, the two emergency benefits provided to working Canadians are:

## 1. **Emergency Care Benefit**
- For those who must stay home due to COVID-19 and don't have paid sick leave
- Individuals may include those who can't access EI and are sick, or who are taking care of a child or someone who is sick
- **Up to $900 bi-weekly for 15 weeks**

## 2. **Emergency Support Benefit**
- Provides up to **$5 billion** in support to workers who are not eligible for EI and who are facing unemployment and reduced hours
