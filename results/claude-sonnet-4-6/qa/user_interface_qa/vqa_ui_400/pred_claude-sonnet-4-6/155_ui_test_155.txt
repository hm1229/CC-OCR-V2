## Role of a Data & Analytics Manager

According to the infographic, the role of a **Data & Analytics Manager** is to:

> **"Manages a team of analysts and data scientists"**

### Additional Details:
- **Mindset:** Data Wizard/Cheerleader
- **Languages Used:** SQL, R, SAS, Python, Matlab, Java
- **Key Skills & Talents:**
  - Database systems (SQL and NO SQL based)
  - Planning & project management
  - Interpersonal communication
  - Data mining & predictive modeling

### Hired By:
- Coursera
- Slack
- Motorola Solutions

They essentially serve as a **leadership role**, bridging the gap between technical data teams and broader business objectives while managing and guiding analysts and data scientists.
