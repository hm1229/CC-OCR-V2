## Cancer Incidence in Women Over 50

Based on the infographic, the percentage of women **over the age of 50** with cancer incidence is calculated by adding all age groups above 50:

| Age Group | Percentage |
|-----------|------------|
| 50 to 64 yrs | 27% |
| 65 to 74 yrs | 25% |
| Over 74 yrs | 30% |

**Total = 27% + 25% + 30% = 82%**

This is also consistent with the infographic's statement that only **18% of cancers occur before the age of 50**, meaning **82% occur in women aged 50 and above**.
