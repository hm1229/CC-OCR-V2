The long-term benefits of early childhood education, as outlined in the infographic, include:

### **Student Success:**
- Students with early childhood education are **29% more likely** to graduate from high school.
- They are **44% less likely** to repeat a grade.
- They **pass standardized tests more frequently**.

### **Adult Responsibility:**
- Adults aged 40 who had early childhood education are **more likely to be employed**.
- These individuals have a **33% higher average income**.
- In contrast, 18-year-olds without early childhood education are **70% more likely to be arrested for a violent crime**.

These benefits highlight how early childhood education contributes significantly to academic achievement and positive adult outcomes.
