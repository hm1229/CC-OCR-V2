In Q2 2016, the mobile internet speed in China was 6.7 Mbps. This information is part of the broader data presented in the infographic, which compares broadband and mobile internet speeds across various countries in Asia. The infographic highlights that China's mobile speed is relatively low compared to other countries in the region, such as South Korea, which had a mobile speed of 11.8 Mbps. The data is sourced from Akamai's State of the Internet Report, which surveyed 119 countries/regions for broadband data and 58 for mobile data.
