According to the **MIWE 2018** (Mastercard Index of Women Entrepreneurs 2018), **70.9%** of women entrepreneurs are in **Canada**.

This is shown in the “Mapping Out the Landscape” section, which lists the top 5 countries by MIWE score:

- **New Zealand**: 74.2%
- **Sweden**: 71.3%
- **Canada**: **70.9%**
- **U.S.**: 70.8%
- **Singapore**: 69.2%

So, Canada ranks third among these top countries with a score of **70.9%**.

*(Note: The MIWE index measures the enabling environment for women entrepreneurs — not the percentage of women who are entrepreneurs — but rather how supportive the conditions are for female entrepreneurship in each country.)*
