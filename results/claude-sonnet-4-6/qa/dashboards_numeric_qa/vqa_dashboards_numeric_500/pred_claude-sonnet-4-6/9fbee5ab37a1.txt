## Two Types of Entrepreneurs

According to the infographic, the two types of entrepreneurs are:

1. **Opportunity-Driven** 🟢
   - Found in markets with **strong enabling conditions** (e.g., New Zealand, Canada, UK, UAE, and Portugal)

2. **Necessity-Driven** 🟠
   - Found in markets with **fewer supporting conditions** (e.g., Ghana, Brazil, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Mexico)
