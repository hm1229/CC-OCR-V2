To determine how many people are not disabled in Australia when considering a group of 5 people, we start with the information from the infographic:  

- It states “DISABILITY: 1 IN 5 PEOPLE” — meaning **1 out of every 5 people has a disability**.  

For a group of 5 people:  
- Number of disabled people = 1  
- Number of non - disabled people = Total people − Disabled people = 5 − 1 = 4  


So, in a group of 5 people, 4 people are not disabled in Australia.
