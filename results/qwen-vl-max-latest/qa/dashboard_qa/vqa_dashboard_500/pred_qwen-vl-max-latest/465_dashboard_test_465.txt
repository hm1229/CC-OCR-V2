To find the increase in percentage from overall consumers to millennials when it comes to trying new things while shopping online, we look at the data provided in the section titled **"Consumers are experimenting, purchasing items they would not usually purchase online."**

- **Overall**: 39% of respondents said the lockdown encouraged them to try and buy goods online they had not considered before.
- **Millennials**: 52% said the same.

### Increase:
$$
52\% - 39\% = 13\%
$$

So, the increase in percentage from overall consumers to millennials is **13 percentage points**.
