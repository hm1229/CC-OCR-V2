To determine the percentage of senior citizens (aged 65 years and above) in Singapore in 1998, we refer to the "1998" section of the infographic.  

It states: *"Ageing Population: - 1.4 in 20 citizens are aged 65 years and above"*.  

To convert this to a percentage:  
- First, calculate the proportion: \( \frac{1.4}{20} = 0.07 \)  
- Then, multiply by 100 to get the percentage: \( 0.07 \times 100 = 7\% \)  


Thus, the percentage of senior citizens in Singapore in 1998 was **7%**.
