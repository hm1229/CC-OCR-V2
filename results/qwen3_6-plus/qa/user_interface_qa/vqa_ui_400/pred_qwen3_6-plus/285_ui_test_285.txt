To calculate the **percentage of free apps in the iPhone and Android stores taken together**, we need to:

1. Extract the number of **free apps** for each store.
2. Extract the **total apps** for each store.
3. Add the free apps together and total apps together.
4. Compute the percentage:  
   > **(Total Free Apps / Total Apps) × 100**

---

From the infographic:

### Apple App Store for iPhone:
- Total apps: **375,566**
- Free apps: **29%** → So, free apps = 29% of 375,566  
  → 0.29 × 375,566 ≈ **108,914**

### Google Android Market:
- Total apps: **224,808**
- Free apps: **60%** → So, free apps = 60% of 224,808  
  → 0.60 × 224,808 ≈ **134,885**

---

### Combined:

- Total free apps = 108,914 + 134,885 = **243,799**
- Total apps = 375,566 + 224,808 = **600,374**

---

### Percentage of free apps (combined):

> (243,799 / 600,374) × 100 ≈ **40.61%**

---

✅ **Final Answer: Approximately 40.6%**
