## Cancer Incidence in Women Over 50

According to the infographic, **82%** of cancers in women occur at or after the age of 50.

This is derived from the fact that only **18%** of cancers in women occur before the age of 50, meaning 100% - 18% = **82%** occur in women aged 50 and above.

The breakdown for women over 50 is:
- **50 to 64 years**: 27%
- **65 to 74 years**: 25%
- **Over 74 years**: 30%

Total: 27% + 25% + 30% = **82%**
