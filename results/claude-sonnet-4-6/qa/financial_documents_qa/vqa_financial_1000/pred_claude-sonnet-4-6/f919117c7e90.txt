This is a **Discipline Review Letter** from the **Department of Health & Human Services** (Public Health Service / Food and Drug Administration), addressed to Mary Jo Pritza, MPH, PharmD, Manager of Regulatory Affairs at **Takeda Global Research & Development Center, Inc.**

The letter references NDA 21-073/S-026 and pertains to a supplemental new drug application (NDA) for **Actos® (pioglitazone HCl) tablets** (15 mg, 30 mg, and 45 mg).
