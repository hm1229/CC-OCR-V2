Looking at the LinkedIn section of the infographic:

- It states: **“400 million Users Worldwide”**
- And: **“70% of which are outside the US”**

If 70% are *outside* the US, then the remaining percentage must be *inside* the US.

> **100% - 70% = 30%**

✅ **Answer: 30% of LinkedIn users are from the US.**
