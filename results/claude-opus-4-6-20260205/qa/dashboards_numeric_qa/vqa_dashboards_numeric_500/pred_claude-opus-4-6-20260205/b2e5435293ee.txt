## Top Contributing Country for Women in UN Peacekeeping

Based on the infographic, **Ethiopia** contributes the most women as UN peacekeeping forces, with **394** female military personnel, making it the **#1 top female uniformed personnel contributing country** in the military category.

If considering police contributions, **Rwanda** leads with **183** female police personnel.

Overall, **Ethiopia** has the highest single contribution at **394** female uniformed personnel.
