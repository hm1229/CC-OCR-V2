Checklist & Specification
检验项目规格
1. The structure of product refer to master sample.
产品结构参照于主样办。
2. The color of product refer to master sample.
产品颜色光泽参照于主样办。
3. Surface can not have foreign mark, scratch, bump mark, scuffing mark, part line, shrinkage must refer to master sample.
表面不可有料花,划花,碰伤,手触刮伤,夹线,应力痕/气纹参照于主样办。
4. The position of laser number can not have scratch, wipe mark, black spot, contamination mark, gas mark and crack.
镭射数字位置,不可有刮花,擦花,黑点,混色,气纹,爆裂。
5. Frame and clutch can not have bump mark, white mark, flash, deformation, short-shot, deformation, over shot and stick mould.
骨位/卡槽不可有碰伤,爆花,变形,批锋,变形,缺胶,多胶,粘模。
6. The screw posit can not have crack, block, short shot and slanting.
螺丝位不可爆裂,发白,堵丝,缺胶,偏位。
7. Gate mark can not have roughness, the surrounding can not deformation.
进胶点不可凹凸不平,水口位周围不可变形。
8. Insert part can not be higher than the plane of frame.
镶件位不可高出骨位平面。
9. The edge of inner frame can not have flash, bump mark, shrinkage, roughness.
框内四边不可有批锋,碰伤,缩水,凹凸不平。
10. Produce date must adjust by monthly and correct.
生产日期随月份调整,须指示正确。
