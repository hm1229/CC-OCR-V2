From the infographic, the **developmental benefits** of early childhood education are:

- **Social and emotional development**
  - Helps children **develop relationships** with adults and peers
  - Provides a **sense of security**
  - Encourages **greater participation and cooperation**

- **Cognitive development**
  - Offers **age-appropriate learning**
  - Builds on existing **skills**
  - Increases **knowledge**
  - Helps children reach **important developmental milestones**

- **Language development**
  - Language develops quickly between ages **1 and 5**
  - Interactions with teachers and peers help **develop language skills**
  - Supports more advanced **conversation and vocabulary** growth
