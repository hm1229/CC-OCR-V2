In 2009, in the U.S., **more people died from falls** than from drowning or accidental shootings.

Here’s the breakdown based on the infographic:

- **Falls**: ~25,000 deaths  
- **Drowning**: ~2,000 annual deaths  
- **Accidental shootings**: ~600 annual deaths  

✅ So, **falls** were by far the leading cause among these three.

*(Note: The infographic also shows that motor vehicle accidents (42,000) and poisoning (39,000) were even higher overall, but among the three you asked about — drowning, falls, accidental shootings — falls topped the list.)*
