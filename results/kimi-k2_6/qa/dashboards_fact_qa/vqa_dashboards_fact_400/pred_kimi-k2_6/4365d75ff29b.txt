I need to find the percentage of newspaper companies using required hashtags (specifically @author or @site) in their tweets.

From the "UK AND US NATIONAL NEWSPAPERS" section:

- Total newspapers surveyed: 44
- All 44 have onsite Twitter buttons
- Of those with onsite buttons: **18 out of 44** have either @author or @site within the default tweet

To calculate the percentage:
(18 ÷ 44) × 100 = 40.91%

This is also confirmed in the infographic text which states: "Of the newspapers analysed, **40.91%** have integrated either the @author or @site within their default tweet template."

So **40.91%** of newspaper companies are using the required hashtags (@author or @site) in their tweets.
