To calculate how much the value of **exports** is more than the value of **imports**, we use the data from the “AUSTRALIA-CHINA TRADE” section:

- **Total Exports** = $101.54 billion  
- **Total Imports** = $49.33 billion  

Now subtract imports from exports:

**$101.54b - $49.33b = $52.21b**

---

✅ **Answer: $52.21 billion**

This means Australia’s exports to China exceeded its imports from China by **$52.21 billion** in 2013.
