## Regular Cyclists Show Better Work Performance

According to the infographic, **regular cyclists** demonstrate better work performance compared to non-cyclists. Specifically:

- Regular cyclists take **1 less sick day** than non-cyclists
- This saves the economy **£128 million a year** in reduced absenteeism
- Inactivity costs in the UK are reduced, saving **£760 million per annum**

This highlights the significant health and productivity benefits of regular cycling.
