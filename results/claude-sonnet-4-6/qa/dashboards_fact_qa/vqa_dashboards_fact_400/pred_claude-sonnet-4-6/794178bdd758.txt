## The Ferris Wheel

According to the infographic, **the Ferris Wheel** is the most common fixture found in amusement parks across the globe. It made its debut at the **1893 Chicago World's Fair**, where it stood as the largest attraction. It was designed and constructed by **George Washington Gale Ferris Jr.** in 1893, standing **80.4 metres tall** and rotating on a **71-tonne, 45.5-foot axle**.
