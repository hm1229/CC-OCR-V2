## The 3 R's in UK Construction

According to the infographic, the **3 R's** are:

1. 🔴 **REDUCE**
   - Eliminate the generation of waste at the source by delivering only what is required to site.

2. 🔄 **REUSE**
   - Make use of excess or unused materials in their original state on other sites.

3. ♻️ **RECYCLE**
   - Modify excess products to create new, usable products.

These principles are part of the UK Construction Industry's approach to addressing waste management and environmental responsibility.
