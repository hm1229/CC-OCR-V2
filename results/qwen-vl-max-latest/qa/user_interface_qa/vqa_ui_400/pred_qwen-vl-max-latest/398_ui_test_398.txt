The two specific dog food nutrient profiles defined by the AAFCO (Association of American Feed Control Officials) standards are designed for:

1. **Canine Growth and Reproduction** – This profile is formulated to meet the nutritional needs of puppies, pregnant dogs, and lactating dogs, supporting healthy development and reproductive health.

2. **Adult Dog Maintenance** – This profile is tailored for adult dogs to maintain optimal health, energy levels, and overall well-being once they have reached maturity.
