Looking at the infographic, under the section "WORKING CANADIANS", two emergency benefits are listed:

1. Emergency Care Benefit: Provides up to $900 bi-weekly for up to 15 weeks for those who must stay home due to COVID-19 and don’t have paid sick leave, including caregivers.

2. Emergency Support Benefit: Provides up to $5 billion in support to workers not eligible for EI who are facing unemployment or reduced hours.

Answer: The emergency benefits provided to working Canadians are the Emergency Care Benefit (up to $900 bi-weekly for up to 15 weeks) and the Emergency Support Benefit (up to $5 billion for workers not eligible for EI facing unemployment or reduced hours).
