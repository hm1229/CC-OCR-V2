We are told:

- In **2003**, **57.2%** of academic leaders believed online education was the same or even superior to face-to-face learning.
- “And just two years later” — that would be **2005** — that number had increased to **77%**.

To find how many **more percent** in 2005 compared to 2003:

> **77% - 57.2% = 19.8%**

✅ **Final Answer: 19.8%**
