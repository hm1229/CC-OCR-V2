The company that built a plant to extract lignin from pulp is **FP Innovations**. This is mentioned in the infographic under the section "Waking Thunder Bay's 'Sleeping Giant' Through Innovation," where it states: "FP Innovations has established a pilot plant in Resolute Forest Products' Thunder Bay mill to extract lignin from pulp to make products like adhesives, carbon fiber and bioplastics or to use as a replacement for petrochemical-based products."
