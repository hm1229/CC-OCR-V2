\begin{enumerate}

    \item Buttons are packed in packets of 576 each. How many buttons are there in 10 packets?

    \item The price of 17 table fans is ₹ 6545. What is the cost of 1 table fan? What is the cost of 8 table fans?
    
    \item Three planes can carry 1185 passengers. How many passengers can be carried by 5 planes?
    
    \item 2250 clips are equally packed in 18 packets. How many clips are there in each packet?
    
    \item A car covers 2025 km in 25 hours. How much distance does it cover in 7 hours?
    
    \item An off-set printing machine prints 37600 forms in 16 hours. How many forms does it print in 1 hour?
    
    \item A factory produces 6816 motor horns in 12 days. How many horns does it produce in 1 day?
    
    \item If 13 buses can carry 715 passengers, how many passengers can be carried by 20 buses?
    
    \item A car runs 285 km on 15 litres of petrol. How many kilometres can it run on 22 litres of petrol?
    
    \item The annual rent of a house is ₹ 58500. What is its rent for 17 months?
    
    \item A worker earned ₹ 1080 in 8 days. How much did he earn in 3 days?
\end{enumerate}
