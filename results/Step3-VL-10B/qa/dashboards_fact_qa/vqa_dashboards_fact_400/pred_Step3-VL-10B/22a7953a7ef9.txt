To determine the number of different dog food formulations, look at the section titled "DIFFERENCES IN FORMULATION COME FROM CURRENT AAFCO STANDARDS WHICH INCLUDE TWO SPECIFIC DOG FOOD NUTRIENT PROFILES". It states there are two specific nutrient profiles: one for canine growth and reproduction, and another for adult dog maintenance.
