The infographic says:

- **Images with faces receive 38% more likes.**

The **inverse percentage** asks: compared to photos with faces, how much fewer likes do photos **without** faces get?

Compute the inverse relative decrease:

\[
\frac{38}{138} \times 100 \approx 27.5\%
\]

So, **photos without faces get about 27.5% fewer likes** than photos with faces.
