Looking at the infographic, under the section **“EMPLOYERS WANT YOUNG STAFF WITH WORK EXPERIENCE”**, there is a statistic:

> **56%** believe that work experience has become central to their recruitment strategy.

To find the percentage who believe it has **not** become central, we subtract this from 100%:

> **100% - 56% = 44%**

---

✅ **Answer: 44%**
