During that same session, **Wernicke-Korsakoff’s Syndrome in North America** has been listed.

The letter also asks whether a speaker has been arranged for this topic and whether anyone will present the “somewhat new hypothesis concerning the enzyme deficiency in this Syndrome.” The author suggests inviting **John Blass** as a discussant if no one else is covering it.
