To calculate the **percentage of civilian women** among the total female personnel in UN peacekeeping missions, we use the numbers provided in the infographic:

- **Civilian women**: 1,603  
- **Uniformed women**: 3,557  
- **Total female personnel**: 5,160 (as shown in the center circle)

> Note: The total 5,160 = 1,603 (civilian) + 3,557 (uniformed) — this checks out.

Now, to find the **percent of civilian women**:

\[
\frac{1603}{5160} \times 100 \approx ?
\]

Let’s compute:

\[
1603 ÷ 5160 ≈ 0.31066
\]

\[
0.31066 × 100 ≈ 31.07\%
\]

---

✅ **Answer: Approximately 31%**

So, around **31%** of the female personnel in UN peacekeeping missions are civilians.
