This is a **Western Union Telegram** from 1961.

Specifically, it is a **day letter** (indicated by the "DL" code in the service box), which was a type of telegram service that offered a lower rate for messages that could be delivered during the next business day rather than immediately. The document shows it was sent from Houston, Texas on December 30, 1961, and was addressed to Dr. Roger Adams at the Department of Chemistry, University of Illinois, Urbana. The message was from Mark Edwin Andrews, informing Dr. Adams about his father's death and thanking him for his service as a trustee of the Welch Foundation.
