## Access to Digital Tools by Community Type (In School)

Based on the infographic, the percentages of students in **urban** areas who have access to digital tools **in school** are:

- **9%** - All or almost all
- **22%** - Most
- **16%** - Only some
- **48%** (remaining category)

The infographic shows that **teachers in urban areas are the least likely to say their students have sufficient access to digital tools in school**.

Looking at the "Access to Digital Tools by Community Type" section for **In School** access, the urban breakdown shows: **57%** and **56%** and **52%** for the in-school columns.

To directly answer: For urban students' access to digital tools at school, **9%** of teachers say all or almost all students have access, making urban areas the most disadvantaged in terms of in-school digital tool access.
