To find the combined food price change of "Ready to Eat Food" and "Food Price Index", we first identify their respective percentages from the "FOOD PRICE CHANGES SINCE 2006" section:  

- Ready to Eat Food: 24.3%  
- Food Price Index: 27.3%  

Then, we add these two values together:  
24.3% + 27.3% = 51.6%  


The combined food price change is 51.6%.
