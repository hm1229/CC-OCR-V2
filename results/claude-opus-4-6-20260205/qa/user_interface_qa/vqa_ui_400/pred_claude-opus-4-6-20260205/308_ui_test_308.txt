## Time-to-Market for Apps via Direct Download

According to the infographic, the **Time-to-market** for apps via **Direct Download** is **36 days**.

This is compared to:
- **App Store**: 22 days
- **Via Customer**: 41 days

The infographic notes that app stores have dramatically reduced the time-to-market for apps, from **68 days** across traditional channels to **22 days** via an app store.
