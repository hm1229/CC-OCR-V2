To determine the percentage of millennials who do not want their daily tasks automated, we first look at the section “I wish more of the day - to - day tasks I do were automated”. For Millennials, 56% **do** want their tasks automated. To find the percentage that do not want them automated, we subtract this value from 100%:  

\( 100\% - 56\% = 44\% \)  


So, 44% of millennials do not want their daily tasks to be automated.
