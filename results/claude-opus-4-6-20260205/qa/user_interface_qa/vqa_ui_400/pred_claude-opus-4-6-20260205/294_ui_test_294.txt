Based on the infographic, **6 benefits** of having a website are listed:

1. **Cost-Effective Advertising** - Websites offer a low cost advertising solution compared to traditional media.
2. **Analysis of Customer Behavior** - Internet analytics enable businesses to log and track everyone who visits their websites.
3. **Round-the-Clock Availability** - Operating a website enables a business to be available to new and current customers 24 hours per day.
4. **Customer Feedback** - Websites allow customers to interact with companies and provide feedback on their experience.
5. **Niche Markets** - The relatively low cost of running a website allows small and medium-sized businesses to cater for niche markets.
6. **Business Profile** - Doing business online levels the playing field for small and medium-sized companies.
