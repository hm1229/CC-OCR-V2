According to Frank L. Iber, M.D., **alcohol** is the only nutrient antinutrient.

He states in the text:  
> “Alcohol is the only nutrient antinutrient. It is a food that causes a very special kind of malnutrition.”

This means that while alcohol provides calories (a form of energy/nutrient), it interferes with the body’s ability to absorb and utilize other essential nutrients — hence, acting as an “antinutrient.”
