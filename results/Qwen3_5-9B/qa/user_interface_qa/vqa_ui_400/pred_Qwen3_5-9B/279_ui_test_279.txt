Based on the provided infographic from the Department of Health & Social Care:

**A clinician's advice is required** for conducting a laboratory test (specifically, the antibody test).

This is stated in the section under "Can I get tested now?" for the **Laboratory test**:

> "This test is being offered to NHS and social care staff, and to NHS hospital inpatients and care home residents based on their clinician's advice"

So, while the test itself is a blood test taken by a medical professional, the decision to offer it is based on the recommendation of a clinician.
