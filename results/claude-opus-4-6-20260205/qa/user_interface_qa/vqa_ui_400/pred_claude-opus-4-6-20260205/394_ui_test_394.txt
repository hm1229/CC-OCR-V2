# ANZAC Biscuits

According to the infographic, **Anzac biscuits** were used as a **bread substitute** during WWI. The simple biscuit used in WWI soldier rations were hard and savoury.

The infographic also notes that:
- Anzac biscuits are closely associated with the Australian and New Zealand Army Corps (ANZACs) established in WWI
- **1924** was the year the first known Anzac Biscuit recipe was published, in *The War Chest Cookery Book*
- **Eggs** were scarce during the war, so syrup was used as the biscuit's binding agent
- They are made from **rolled oats, coconut, flour and golden syrup**
