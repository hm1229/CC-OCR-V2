The English Football Association (The FA) was founded in **1863**.

This is confirmed by the infographic, which notes:

> “THE FA  
> THE ENGLISH FOOTBALL ASSOCIATION CELEBRATES ITS 150TH ANNIVERSARY THIS YEAR  
> 1863 — 150 YEARS — 2013”

Since 2013 marked its 150th anniversary, subtracting 150 years from 2013 gives us **1863** as the founding year.

✅ So, the answer is: **1863**
