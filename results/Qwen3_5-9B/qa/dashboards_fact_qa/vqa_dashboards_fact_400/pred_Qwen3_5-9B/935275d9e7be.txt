Based on the infographic **“UK iPhone users: A snapshot”** (surveyed in September 2013), the **top five most common iPhone activities** in the UK are:

- **SMS**: **96.2%**
- **Calls**: **91.4%**
- **Web**: **88.1%**
- **Photos**: **79.7%**
- **News**: **76.7%**

---

✅ **Answer: SMS**

The majority of iPhone users in the UK spent their time on **SMS (text messaging)**, with **96.2%** using it — the highest among all listed activities.

*(Note: The survey was conducted by OnePoll for Three between 13th–16th September 2013, as noted at the bottom of the infographic.)*
