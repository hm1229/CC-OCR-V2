Looking at the calendar for December 2006:

- The days of the week are labeled: Sun, Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat.
- The first day of the month, December 1, is a Friday.
- So, the previous Sunday would be December 31 of November, but we are looking for the first Sunday in December.
- December 3 is a Sunday (as shown in the grid: row 1, under Sun column is 3).

Therefore, the first Sunday in December 2006 is the 3rd.

Answer: 3
