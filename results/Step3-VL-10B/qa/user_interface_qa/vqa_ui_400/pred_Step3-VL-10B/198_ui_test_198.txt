To determine how much less the recipient values the sweater as a gift compared to its actual price, we find the values in the "THE GIFT VALUATION GAP" section:  

- Gift Price of the sweater: $50  
- Value to Recipient of the sweater: $41  

Subtract the recipient’s perceived value from the gift price: \( 50 - 41 = 9 \).  

So, the recipient values the sweater $9 less than its actual price.
