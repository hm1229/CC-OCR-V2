To determine the percentage of people who either forget or have other reasons for not using a gift card, we refer to the **"THE GIFT CARD ECONOMY"** section of the infographic.  

- The pie chart shows reasons for not using gift cards:  
  - 41% “Forgot”  
  - 6% “Other” (other reasons)  

Adding these two categories: \( 41\% + 6\% = 47\% \).  


Thus, 47% of people either forget or have other reasons for not using the gift card.
