TOKO FAHA-7

Olo Maty ma
Mbala Hivilomiiiy?

Ino ahizantsika fa olo maty
mbala hivilomiiiy?

Ino mahavy Jehovah hameloio olo maty?
Olo karakory ma hivilominy Zanahary?

OHATRA izio misy olo fatatra ndreky farany rasỳ fanjari-
manjaina anao. Efa hainao fa maro olo no farany
anao noho ny miliôlogy. Misy valiny araky ny hand-
anano lany hevitry momba ny fiainany araky ny
io. Izy matanjaka manlatra olo maritiana anao
io. Anao faavoravo marel.

Izy misy faharatsy maninta atsika jaby. Tsy ino
tsy fa rahafatesana. Izy io koa olo hantsika na zovy.
Fa Jehovah avỳ. Efa maro koa olo hantsika nazony. Fa je-
hany, fotony izy efa nanano raha hanavotana atsika,
baka atsika tsy hoaty. “Ato” nifehyvato farany 26:19 m-
zainatra fa “haharese” Jehovah farany hifoatany
zanahary. Tena mamparavoravo vovovo zeny!

Atsika koa fa maharejy olo hantsika naty, atsika
teña malahelo. Nahatsara izy, Jehovah efa nolaika fa
olo naty aby io. Ny Baibala hivilominy Isaia 26:19 Baka
amy zeny zeny mahavy atsika mahantatra fa io mbola no
velono.

1-3. Ino ma fahavalo marintina atsika jaby? Izikoa Baiboly zahaña,
hatao karakory fahavalo io?
