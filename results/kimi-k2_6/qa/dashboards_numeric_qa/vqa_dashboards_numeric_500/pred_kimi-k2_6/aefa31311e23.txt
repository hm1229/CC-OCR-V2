Based on the infographic, the majority of millennials in the U.S. choose online viewing for the following reasons:

1. **Immediate access (56%)** - The ability to instantly access content without waiting for scheduled programming

2. **Multitasking (49%)** - The convenience of being able to do multiple things while viewing content online

3. **Convenience of viewing location (44%)** - The flexibility to watch from anywhere, not just from a traditional television set

These three factors highlight that millennials value **convenience, flexibility, and control** over their media consumption experience, which online viewing platforms provide better than traditional media formats.
