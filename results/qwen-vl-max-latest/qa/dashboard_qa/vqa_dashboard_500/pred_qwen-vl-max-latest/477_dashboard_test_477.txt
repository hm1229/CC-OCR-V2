The emergency benefits provided to working Canadians under Canada's COVID-19 Economic Response Plan include:

1. **Emergency Care Benefit**:
   - Provides up to **$900 bi-weekly for up to 15 weeks**.
   - Designed for individuals who must stay home due to COVID-19 and do not have paid sick leave.
   - This includes people who are sick, cannot access Employment Insurance (EI), or are caring for a child or someone who is sick.

2. **Emergency Support Benefit**:
   - Offers up to **$5 billion in support**.
   - Aimed at workers who are not eligible for EI and are facing unemployment or reduced hours.

These benefits are part of a broader economic response plan that includes:
- **$27 billion** in direct support for Canadian workers and businesses.
- **$55 billion** in support for business liquidity through tax deferrals.
