The infographic states that currently, **440,000 birds** are killed by wind turbines each year. It also predicts that **by 2030, that number will exceed 1 million**.

To calculate the expected rise:

- Current figure: 440,000
- Projected figure by 2030: Over 1,000,000

So, the **expected rise** is:

**1,000,000 - 440,000 = 560,000**

Thus, the expected rise in bird deaths due to wind turbines by 2030 is **more than 560,000 birds**.
