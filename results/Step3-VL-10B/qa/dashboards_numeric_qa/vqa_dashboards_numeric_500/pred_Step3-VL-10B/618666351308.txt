To determine the percentage of Malaysian online shoppers who do **not** shop online daily, we first identify the percentage of those who *do* shop daily.  

- From the pie chart, the “Daily” category (marked by the green segment) accounts for **0.4%** of online shoppers.  

Since the question asks for those who *do not* shop daily, we subtract the daily shopping percentage from 100%:  

\( 100\% - 0.4\% = 99.6\% \)  


Thus, 99.6% of Malaysian online shoppers do not shop online on a daily basis.
