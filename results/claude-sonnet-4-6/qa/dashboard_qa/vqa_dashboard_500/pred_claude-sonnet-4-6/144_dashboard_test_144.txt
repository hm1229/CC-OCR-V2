# Characteristics of Halloween Pumpkins (from the infographic)

Based on the **"Big, Round, and Orange All Over"** section of the infographic:

## Key Facts:

- 🎃 **Big** - large in size
- 🟠 **Round** - circular/spherical shape
- 🟧 **Orange** - distinctive orange color

## Production & Supply Stats:

- **900 million pounds** of pumpkins are produced in the US for Halloween
- **80%** of the total pumpkin supply in the US is available **during October**
- They could make about **60 million pumpkin pies**

## Cultural Significance:
- **48%** of people **carve a pumpkin** as a way to celebrate Halloween
- More furry monsters (pets) are dressing up as **pumpkins** than anything else
- **27.1%** of Americans will look for pumpkin/costume ideas via social media sites

The pumpkin is clearly one of the most iconic symbols of Halloween, both as a decoration and as a food source during the October season.
