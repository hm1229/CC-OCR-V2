## BARF Diet Foods Mentioned

According to the infographic, **7 food categories** are mentioned in Ian Billinghurst's "Biologically Appropriate Raw Food" diet for dogs:

1. **Muscle meat** (on bone)
2. **Bones** (whole or ground)
3. **Organ meats** (liver, kidney)
4. **Raw eggs**
5. **Veggies** (broccoli, spinach, celery)
6. **Apples or other fruits**
7. **Some dairy** (such as yogurt)
