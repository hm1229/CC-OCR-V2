Based on the infographic, the majority of millennials in the U.S. choose online viewing for the following reasons:

1. **Immediate access (56%)** - This is the top reason, allowing them to watch content instantly without waiting

2. **Multitasking (49%)** - Online viewing enables them to do multiple things at once while watching content

3. **Convenience of viewing location (44%)** - They can watch from anywhere, not being tied to a traditional TV set

The infographic also notes that millennials are a "technology friendly generation" that prefers to find their news and media elsewhere than traditional sources, and they spend **18 hours a day consuming media** on average. Additionally, **72% use free streaming video** such as YouTube and Hulu, making it their #1 used viewing source.
