To determine the percentage of proteins in dry dog food, refer to the section **"HOW TODAY’S PET FOODS MEASURE UP (OR DON’T)"** in the infographic.  

- Locate the image of the "WOOF! FOR DOGS" dry pet food bag.  
- Check the annotations below the image: the purple dashed arrow labeled **"18-32%"** corresponds to the "Proteins" (chicken leg icon) on the dry food packaging.  


Thus, dry dog food comprises **18-32% protein**.
