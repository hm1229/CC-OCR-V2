The steps to derive the quadratic formula are shown below:

| Step | Equation |
| :--- | :--- |
| Step 1 | $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ |
| Step 2 | $ax^2 + bx = -c$ |
| Step 3 | $x^2 + \frac{b}{a}x = -\frac{c}{a}$ |
| Step 4 | $x^2 + \frac{b}{a}x + \frac{b^2}{4a^2} = -\frac{c}{a} + \frac{b^2}{4a^2}$ |
| Step 5 | $x^2 + \frac{b}{a}x + \frac{b^2}{4a^2} = -\frac{4ac}{4a^2} + \frac{b^2}{4a^2}$ |
| Step 6 | $\left(x + \frac{b}{2a}\right)^2 = \frac{b^2 - 4ac}{4a^2}$ |
| Step 7 | $\left(x + \frac{b}{2a}\right) = \pm \sqrt{\frac{b^2 - 4ac}{4a^2}} = \pm \frac{\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{4a}$ |
| Step 8 | $x = -\frac{b}{2a} \pm \frac{\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$ |
| Step 9 | $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$ |

Which of the following is the first incorrect step?

*   Step 4
*   Step 6
*   Step 7
*   Step 8
