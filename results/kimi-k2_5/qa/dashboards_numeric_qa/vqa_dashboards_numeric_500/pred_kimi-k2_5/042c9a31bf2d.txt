Based on the infographic, the long-term benefits of early childhood education are:

## Student Success
- Students are **29% more likely to graduate from high school**
- Students are **44% less likely to repeat a grade**
- Students **pass standardized tests more frequently**

## Adult Responsibility
- Adults at age 40 are **more likely to be employed**
- These 40-year-olds have a **33% higher average income**
- 18-year-olds without early childhood education are **70% more likely to be arrested for a violent crime** (implying that those with early education are less likely to be arrested)
