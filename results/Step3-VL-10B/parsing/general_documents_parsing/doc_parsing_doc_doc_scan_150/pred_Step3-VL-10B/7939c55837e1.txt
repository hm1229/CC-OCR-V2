\subsection{2.2.2 Intron 2 polymorphic site}
Sequencing results showed that a C$\rightarrow$T mutation occurred at this site. After digestion of PCR products with the restriction endonuclease Msp I, three genotypes were observed on agarose gel electrophoresis at this site: homozygous C/C individuals had an Msp I cleavage site, showing a single 585 bp band, which disappeared after Msp I digestion; heterozygous C/T individuals showed two bands at 585 and 619 bp; homozygous T/T individuals showed a single 619 bp band. The 83 and 34 bp fragments generated by cleavage were not observed.

\subsection{2.2.3 Polymorphic sites at codons 347 and 360}
Sequencing results indicated that the nucleotides at these two sites were A/A and G/C homozygotes, respectively. No ACT$\rightarrow$CAT mutation at codon 347 or CAG$\rightarrow$CAT mutation at codon 360 was found. Specifically, there was no ACT$\rightarrow$TCT mutation at codon 347 or CAG$\rightarrow$CAT mutation at codon 360.

\subsection{2.3 Comparison of apoAⅣ gene polymorphic site genotypes and allele frequencies between HTG group and control group}
By Table 2, the frequency of the G allele at codon 9 in the HTG group was significantly higher than that in the control group (0.453 vs 0.366, $P<0.05$), while the mutation frequency at the Msp I site showed no significant difference between the two groups. At codon 347 and codon 360, the nucleotides were ACT and CAG, respectively, and no TTC or CAT mutations were observed.
