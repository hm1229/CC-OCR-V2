The calculation is as follows:

1.  **Start with the number of fatal injuries.**

3.  **Multiply the result by 200,000,000.**

$$
\text{Fatality Rate} = \left( \frac{\text{Number of Fatal Injuries}}{\text{Total Hours Worked}} \right) \times 200,000,000
$$
