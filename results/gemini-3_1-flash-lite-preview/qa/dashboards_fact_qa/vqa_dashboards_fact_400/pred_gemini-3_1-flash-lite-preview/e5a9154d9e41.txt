Based on the infographic provided, **infrared** touch screens are more durable than resistive ones.

Here is the breakdown of the durability for each:

*   **Resistive:** Described as having "Fair Durability." The screens are vulnerable to scratches and other minor damage, and they wear out over time, requiring replacement.
*   **Infrared:** Described as having "Good Durability." The screen is made of glass, which only shatters upon major impact.
