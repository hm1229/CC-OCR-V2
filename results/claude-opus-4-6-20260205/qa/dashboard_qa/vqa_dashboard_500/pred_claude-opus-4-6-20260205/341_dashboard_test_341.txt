## Calculating Non "Few Times Per Year" Shoppers

According to the pie chart, **56.7%** of online shoppers in Malaysia purchased over the internet **a few times per year**.

Therefore, the percentage who do **not** shop online a few times per year is:

**100% - 56.7% = 43.3%**

This 43.3% is comprised of:
- **Monthly**: 24.7%
- **Once a year**: 13.8%
- **Weekly**: 4.4%
- **Daily**: 0.4%
