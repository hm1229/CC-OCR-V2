To determine the total strength of the Coalition during Operation Phantom Fury, we look at the "2004 OPERATION PHANTOM FURY" section of the infographic.  

- Coalition “MEN” are listed as: 10,500 U.S. + 2,000 IRQ + 850 U.K.  

Adding these together:  
10,500 + 2,000 + 850 = 13,350  

Thus, the total strength of the Coalition during Operation Phantom Fury was 13,350.
