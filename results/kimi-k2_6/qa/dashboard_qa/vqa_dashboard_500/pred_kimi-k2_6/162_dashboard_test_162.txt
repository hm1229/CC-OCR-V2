I need to find what percentage of the world's executives are not women.

From the infographic, in the "INCLUSION & PARTICIPATION" section, it states: "Women make up only **22%** of Parliamentarian seats, and **8%** of the world's executives."

So women make up 8% of the world's executives.

To find the percentage that are NOT women:
