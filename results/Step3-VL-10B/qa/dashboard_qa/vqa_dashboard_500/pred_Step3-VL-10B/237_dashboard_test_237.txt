To determine the mortality rate due to heating systems and other products, we refer to the **"United States estimated NonFire Carbon Monoxide Poison deaths by fuel burning consumer product 2007 - 2009"** pie chart in the bottom - left section:  

- **Heating Systems**: Identified by the blue segment, which accounts for 30%.  
- **Other Products**: Identified by the light - blue segment, which accounts for 2%.  

To find the combined mortality rate for these two categories, we add their percentages: \( 30\% + 2\% = 32\% \).
