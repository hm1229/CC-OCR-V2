III. CALCULATIONS:

Calculating the volume of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

$V_b = a \cdot b \cdot c$

$V_b$ - volume of examined bodies $[cm^3]$, $a$ - width of examined bodies $[cm]$, $b$ - length of examined bodies $[cm]$, $c$ - height of examined bodies $[cm]$.

$V_b = 2,5 [cm] \cdot 4,2 [cm] \cdot 1,5 [cm] = 15,75 [cm^3]$

Calculating the material density of the given examined bodies based on the formula:

$\rho_b = \frac{m_b}{V_b}$

$\rho_b$ - material density of given body $[g/cm^3]$, $m_b$ - mass of given body $[g]$, $V_b$ - volume of examined body.

$\rho_b \text{ (ebonite)} = \frac{22,7}{15,75} [g/cm^3] = 1,44127 [g/cm^3] = 1441,27 [kg/m^3]$

$\rho_b \text{ (bakelite)} = \frac{19,8}{15,75} [g/cm^3] = 1,25714 [g/cm^3] = 1257,14 [kg/m^3]$

$\rho_b \text{ (aluminium (10))} = \frac{42,6}{15,75} [g/cm^3] = 2,70476 [g/cm^3] = 2704,76 [kg/m^3]$

Calculating the material density using aerometer based on the formula:

$\rho_b' = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{m_3 - m_2} \rho_w$

$\rho_b'$ - material density of given body using aerometer $[g/cm^3]$, $m_n$ - values of mass from experiment $[g]$, $\rho_w$ - density of distilled water $= 1 [g/cm^3]$.

$\rho_b' \text{ (ebonite)} = \frac{110 - 87}{103 - 87} [g/g] \cdot 1 [g/cm^3] = \frac{23}{16} [g/cm^3] = 1,4375 [g/cm^3] = 1437,5 [kg/m^3]$.

$\rho_b' \text{ (bakelite)} = \frac{110 - 90}{105 - 90} [g/g] \cdot 1 [g/cm^3] = \frac{20}{15} [g/cm^3] = 1,33333 [g/cm^3] = 1333,33 [kg/m^3]$

$\rho_b' \text{ (aluminium (10))} = \frac{110 - 67}{83 - 67} [g/g] \cdot 1 [g/cm^3] = \frac{43}{16} [g/cm^3] = 2,6875 [g/cm^3] = 2687,5 [kg/m^3]$

$\rho_b' \text{ (aluminium (11))} = \frac{110 - 86}{94 - 86} [g/g] \cdot 1 [g/cm^3] = \frac{24}{8} [g/cm^3] = 3 [g/cm^3] = 3000 [kg/m^3]$
