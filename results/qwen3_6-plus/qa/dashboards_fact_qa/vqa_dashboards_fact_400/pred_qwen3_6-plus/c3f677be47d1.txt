The question asks for the **largest indicator** — meaning the highest percentage value — among:

> low exhaustion (i.e., *less burnout*), high commitment, or high satisfaction

Let’s map them:

- **Low exhaustion** → corresponds to **“125% less burnout”**
- **High commitment** → **“32% more committed”**
- **High satisfaction** → **“46% more satisfied”**
